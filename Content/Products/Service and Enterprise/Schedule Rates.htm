Schedule Rates

Overview

Create schedule rates in simPRO to keep track of actual costs. You may wish to create schedule rates for various levels of overtime, leave, and various activities.

Labour rates can then be associated with schedule rates on activities and employee and contractor card files; used for scheduling; viewed in timesheets, specific forms and reports; and selected in Connect.

Users' security group settings may restrict access to creating, editing, archiving and deleting schedule rates.

Create a schedule rate

To create a schedule rate:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.
  2. Click Create Schedule Rate.
  3. Enter a Schedule Rate Name.
  4. Enter the desired Multiplier up to two decimal places. This cannot be changed after creation and must be a positive value.
  5. Select the Schedule Text Colour as Default or Custom. If custom, use the slider to select a colour.
  6. Select Options as desired.
  7. Click Create.

The Options available to select include:

Show in Connect

Select if you want Connect users to be able to view and select this schedule rate.

Includes Overhead

Select whether or not your schedule rate includes an overhead cost, depending on your business needs.

Activity Only

Select if your schedule rate only applies to a non-billable activity.

You can create schedule rate multipliers 0x and above, however negative multipliers are not supported.

Calculate resource costs using schedule multipliers

The following examples will demonstrate how schedule multipliers multiply your resource costs on a job.

The following rates will apply to the employee for all examples:

Pay Rate (PR)

$20 per hour. This value will be multiplied by the Multiplier value of the schedule rate.

Employee Cost (EC)

$5 per hour. This value is never multiplied by the schedule rate.

Overhead (O)

$10 per hour. This value is never multiplied by the schedule rate.

If the employee works three hours of double time (2x) on a job, and the schedule rate has not been selected to Include Overhead as above:

Resource Cost

(Pay Rate x Hours) x Multiplier, or with the values applied, (20 x 3) x 2

60 x 2 = $120

If the employee works three hours of double time (2x) on a job, and the schedule rate has been selected to Include Overhead as above:

Resource Cost

(Pay Rate x Hours) x Multiplier + (Employment Cost x Hours) + (Overhead x Hours), or with the values applied, (20 x 3) x 2 + (5 x 3) + (10 x 3)

60 x 2 + 15 + 30 = $165
Edit a schedule rate

To edit a schedule rate:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.
  2. Click the schedule rate you wish to edit.
  3. Make changes as desired.
  4. Click Save.

To prevent changes to existing costs, the Multiplier cannot be edited after creation.

If you select or clear the Includes Overhead check box when editing an existing schedule rate, this may change costs for any schedules this rate applied to.

 Archive a schedule rate

To archive a schedule rate:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.
  2. On the schedule rate you wish to delete, click the red delete icon.
  3. Click OK.
 Restore an archived schedule rate

To view archived schedule rates, select Show Archived.

To restore an archived schedule rate:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.
  2. Select the Show Archived? check box.
  3. On the schedule rate you wish to restore, click the green plus icon.
  4. Click OK.
 Assign a schedule rate to an activity

Assign schedule rates to activities to track your costs effectively. When creating a schedule rate, select Activity Only to make a rate unavailable to select when scheduling quotes and jobs.

To assign a schedule rate to an activity:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Activities.
  2. Open an activity or create a new one.
  3. Select a Schedule Rate from the drop-down menu.
  4. Click Save.

The activity will then be associated with that schedule rate when scheduling.

 Select a schedule rate when scheduling

To select a schedule rate when scheduling a quote or job:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day View or Week View.
  2. Under Schedule, search and select the job or quote to schedule.
  3. On the right above the schedule view, select a schedule rate from the drop-down list.
  4. Schedule as usual.
  5. Click Finish.

It is best practice to assign schedule rates when creating activities. When you are scheduling an activity, the schedule rate will default to the linked schedule rate. However, you are able to manually select another rate.

To select a schedule rate when scheduling an activity:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day View or Week View.
  2. Under Schedule, search and select the activity to schedule.
  3. If a schedule rate is assigned to that activity, it will default in the schedule rate drop-down list.
  4. If you wish to manually select a schedule rate, select one from the drop-down list.
  5. Schedule as usual.
  6. Click Finish.

If a custom schedule text colour was selected when creating the schedule rate, the text of the schedule will display in that colour.

 Manually update a schedule rate on a quote or job card

To manually update a schedule rate on a quote or job card:

  1. Within an existing quote or job, go to the Schedule tab.
  2. Ensure someone is scheduled to the quote or job.
  3. Right-click the schedule or click Options.
  4. Click Quote / Job Card > Open Quote / Job Card.
  5. Click the Actual tab.
  6. Select a schedule rate from the Schedule Rate drop-down list.

 View schedule rates in forms

Schedule rates display in the Allocated Schedule Overview and Schedule Timetable forms for quotes and jobs. This is particularly useful for large projects.

To generate these forms:

  1. Open the desired quote or job and go to the Forms tab.
  2. Select Allocated Schedule Overview or Schedule Timetable from the Form drop-down list.
  3. View or send the form as desired.
 View schedule rates in the Employee Activity report

Schedule rates can be viewed in the Employee Activity report, under Reports > View Reports > Employees > Activity.
