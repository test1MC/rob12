STCs

Overview

If you deal with Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) under the Australian Government's Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme, or Victorian Energy Efficiency Certificates (VEECs), you can set these up in simPRO Enterprise.

Enable STCs / VEECs

To view the STCs tab in pre-builds, you will need to enable STCs.

To enable STCs in Enterprise:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Defaults > Extensions > System Extensions.
  2. Under STCs, select On.
  3. Select the Enable VEECS check box if you are using VEECs.
  4. Click the Financial sub-tab.
  5. Enter STCs Value, VEECs Value, STCs Account and VEECs Account details as applicable.
  6. Click Save.

The STC values can be overridden on a quote level by going to the Optional tab and changing the STC value.

 STCs in Pre-Builds

When STCs are enabled, an STCs tab will appear when creating a pre-build. Enter in the amount of STCs for each zone, or Metropolitan or Regional certificates for VEECs.

 Quotes

To present quotes to your customer with any STCs listed, you will need to select the appropriate pre-build.

If the site doesn't recognise the zone, you can manually add it by opening the site card and selecting the zone under STC Zone.

 Invoices

To ensure that the STCs appear on your invoice:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Form Setup > Invoice > Display Options.
  2. Under Show STCs / VEECs, select On.

Create an invoice as usual. Under STCs Received? select Yes.

 GST on STCs

simPRO calculates the STCs prior to GST being applied on an invoice.

 STC Reports

To view the STCs report, go to Reports > View Reports > Sales > STCs.

Search for STCs sold by the adjusting the filters. To view an itemised report, change the Display filter from Summary to Breakdown.
