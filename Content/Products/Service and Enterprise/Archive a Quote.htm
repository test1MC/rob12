Archive a Quote

Overview

If a quote is unsuccessful, you can archive it and select an archive reason to assist in reporting. Set up archive reasons in System > Setup > System Setup > Archive Reasons > Quotes.

Archive a quote

To archive a quote:

  1. Go to Quotes > Open.
  2. Click Options > Archive Quote on the desired quote.
  3. Select the Reason for archiving the quote.
  4. Click Archive.

The quote will then move to Closed. To report on unsuccessful quotes, go to Reports > View Reports > Quotes > Activity, then select Quotes Lost (Date Created) as the Quote Type as well as the desired Archived Reasons. Then, click Search.
