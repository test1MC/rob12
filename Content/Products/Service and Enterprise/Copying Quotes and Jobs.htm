Copying Quotes and Jobs

Overview

If you need to create a quote or job similar to an existing one in simPRO, save time by copying cost centre or section data to a new quote or job, by duplicating cost centres or sections within a quote or job, or by copying the entire quote or job.

Copy cost centre or section data between quotes and jobs

Reduce the need for manual data entry by copying cost centre or section data between quotes and jobs. This will copy settings including cost centre stage, billable and non-billable items, description, notes, and any discounts, fees and adjusted prices. Tasks, schedules, work orders, assigned stock, and purchase orders will not be copied.

This information can be copied between service and project quotes jobs, although section names will not copy from project to service. If you copy cost centre data from a service quote or job to a project quote or job, a new section called Copy will be created in the project quote or job to contain the data. This feature is restricted by some security group settings; a user can copy from any quotes and jobs they can view, but can only paste to quotes and jobs they are able to edit.

If a cost centre is locked when it is copied to another quote or job, it will also be locked on the other quote or job, and will need to be unlocked before changes can be made.

If you copy a section, all its cost centres will be copied. However, only cost centres of the same type will be copied across. For example, if copying a section in the Primary sub-tab of a project quote, only Primary cost centres under that section will copy, and cost centres in the Options tab will need to be copied separately.

To copy cost centre or section data to another quote or job:

  1. Open the quote or job.
  2. Go to the Cost Centre List tab, or click Cost Centres at the top of the page.
  3. On the section or cost centre you wish to copy, click Options > Copy To...
  4. Search and select the quote or job you wish to copy to.
  5. Select or clear the option to Keep cost centre pricing.
  6. Click OK, then click OK to confirm.
  7. Click Save or Finish.

If you are working in a multi-company build, you can only copy cost centre data within companies. If you work with multiple currencies, you can only copy data between customers using the same currency.

 Duplicate cost centres or sections within a quote or job

Just as you can copy cost centres and sections between quotes and jobs, you can duplicate them within a quote or job.

To duplicate a cost centre or section within a quote or job:

  1. Open the quote or job.
  2. Go to the Cost Centre List tab, or click Cost Centres at the top of the page.
  3. On the section or cost centre you wish to copy, click Options > Duplicate / Duplicate Section.
  4. Click Save or Finish.
 Copy an entire quote or job

If you copy an entire quote or job, its customer details, site details, estimated costs such as materials and labour, will be copied across to a new quote or job. Tasks, schedules, work orders, assigned stock, and purchase orders will not be copied.

To copy an entire quote or job:

  1. Open the quote or job you wish to copy.
  2. Go to Details > Settings.
  3. Under Options, click Copy Quote / Job.
  4. Adjust quote or job details as desired, including Customer and Site.
  5. Click Finish or Next.
