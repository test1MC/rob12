Create a Lead

Overview

Use leads to manage potential business opportunities. Once created, you can Convert a Lead if it is successful, or Close a Lead if it is unsuccessful.

 Create a lead

If a customer expresses interest in doing business with your company in the future, create a lead that can later be converted into a quote.

To create a lead:

  1. Hover over Leads.
  2. Click Create New Lead.
  3. Select the Customer. If the lead is from a new customer, click Create New and enter the new customer's details. Click Finish to save the new customer and continue creating the lead.
  4. Select the Site.
  5. Click Save.
  6. If desired:
    • Select the Primary Contact, Site Contact and Additional Contacts.
  7. Select Tags.
  8. Select the Cost Centre.
  9. Select the Status.
  10. Enter the Description.

After creating the lead, you can assign or schedule it.

 Assign a lead

You can assign a lead to a salesperson and select a date for them to follow-up the lead.

To assign a lead:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click the desired lead.
  3. In the Details tab, select the desired Salesperson.
  4. Select a Follow up Date from the date picker.
 Schedule a lead

You can schedule one or multiple salespeople to a lead in the same way you would schedule a technician to a job.

To schedule a lead:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click the desired lead.
  3. Click the Schedule tab.
  4. Next to the desired salesperson, click the schedule icon.
  5. Click and drag the schedule block and click the close icon in the top right corner.
  6. Repeat the above steps to schedule more employees.

Schedules work in either 15 or 30 minute blocks, and it may not take this long for a salesperson to follow up on a lead. If you want to record time spent on leads more accurately, you can use the Notes tab to keep records of phone calls made to customers.

 Send a lead summary form

You can send a lead summary form to the customer or salesperson assigned to the lead.

To send a lead summary form:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click the desired lead.
  3. Click the Forms tab.
  4. Select Lead - Summary to Salesperson if you are sending the form to the salesperson, or select Lead - Summary to Customer if you are sending to the customer.
  5. Enter the email address.

  7. Click View to view the lead summary form.
  8. Click Send.
  9. Click OK to confirm.
Send a calendar appointment to a salesperson / customer

If you have created a schedule for the lead, you can send a calendar appointment to the customer or scheduled employee.

To send a calendar reminder:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click the desired lead.
  3. Click the Forms tab.

  4. Select Lead - Calendar Appointment to Salesperson or Lead - Calendar Appointment to Customer from the Forms drop-down list.

  5. In the pop-up window, select the desired appointment from the Forms drop-down list.

  6. Select the Primary Contact to address the email to, then click to add additional Contacts.

    • Alternatively, enter a new email address in the To field.

  7. Adjust the email details and enter a Subject and Body.

  8. Click Send > OK.

Advanced

Create a lead from a customer

You can create a lead from within a customer card.

To create a lead from a customer:

  1. Go to Customers and click the desired customer.
  2. Click Options on the far right.
  3. Hover over Create.
  4. Click Lead.

Continue creating the lead as normal.

 Record price estimates and expected due dates

Use the Forecast tab to record estimates of the price and approximate date if a lead is successful. This tab is necessary for the Sales Forecast report.

To use the Forecast tab:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click the desired lead.
  3. Click the Forecast tab.
  4. Enter the Estimated Price.
  5. Enter the Probability of Sale.
  6. Select the month and year under Expected Due Date. Alternatively, click This Month or Next Month instead of manually setting the date.
 Additional Customers

You can add additional customers to a lead.

To add additional customers:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click the desired lead.
  3. Click the Additional Customers tab.
  4. Select an existing customer or click Create New to create a new one.
