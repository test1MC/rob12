Workflows

Most of the action in simPRO happens in workflows. In just a few minutes you can get through the whole process of creating a quote, job, invoice, purchase order and more.

There are lots of ways to go through a workflow, and you might need to improvise a bit to figure out what suits you and your business best. Here we'll show you the basics, from start to finish, of each kind of workflow. If you need something more, make sure to look in the Advanced section for more useful features.
