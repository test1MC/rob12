eForms Field App

Overview

simPRO eForms is a mobile application that allows your technicians to fill in digital forms in the field and submit them directly to the eForms Portal.

simPRO eForms is free to download from the App Store for iOS devices and from Google Play for Android devices and is designed to work on a variety of tablets and operating systems.

You need a user licence to log in to eForms.

Compatible operating systems and tablets​

While it is possible to run eForms on a mobile phone device, we recommended using tablets. eForms also requires a device with a camera that supports auto-focus.

simPRO eForms is designed to run on the following operating systems (OS):

  • Apple iOS 7.0 and above
  • Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) and above

simPRO eForms has been designed for and tested on the following tablets:

  • Apple iPad Mini
  • Apple iPad with Retina display
  • Apple iPad Air
  • Samsung Galaxy Tab 3 10.1
  • Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1 LTE

Other operating systems and tablets

Only the device versions specifically listed above have been confirmed to run simPRO eForms successfully, however other devices that have not been tested may also be compatible. Should you choose to run simPRO eForms on a different device, we recommend you test the app thoroughly before commencing actual work.

If the device you are using runs simPRO eForms successfully and is not included in the compatible tablet list above, please let us know by contacting simPRO Support.

It is not recommended to assume that newer or older versions of the same device or operating systems will also be compatible, as there may have been significant changes between releases. It is also not recommended to assume that a device or operating system that works with an earlier version of the eForms mobile app will work with a newer version.

If the required operating system for simPRO eForms is not pre-installed on your device, it is recommended you check with your provider to see if it is available to download, as new OS versions can be delayed for quality assurance purposes.

 Log in and out of simPRO eForms

To log in, simply enter your eForms username and password as set up in the eForms portal, then tap Login.

The app will then connect to the server update the available forms.

If you have forgotten your password, tap I’ve forgotten my password or contact your manager to set a new password in the simPRO eForms Portal.

If you have a custom simPRO eForms server, tap Change login URL, then enter your server URL, tap Change and log in as normal.

When you log in on another device, you will be asked to authorise the new device. Enter your password to authorise and log out of the other device.

To log out of eForms, tap the logout power icon in the top right corner and confirm when prompted. eForms will remember your username for your next login on the same device.

It is recommend to remain logged in to ensure you always have access to your forms, especially when working in remote areas with intermittent mobile coverage.

 Access the eForms dashboard

When you log in to the eForms field app, you will be taken to the dashboard.

Here you can start a new form or view and edit your current form drafts.

You can view up to five recently submitted forms as well as the dates of their submission in the Forms Submitted table.

Tap Update to update your current forms and view available forms you have not yet downloaded to your device. The app will then perform a sync with the server and restrict your access until the sync has finished.

Tap Settings to view the current Version, Server URL and User of eForms on your device.

 Start a new form

You can start any form created as a form template in the eForms form designer.

To start a new form:

  1. Go to the dashboard and tap Start a New Form.
  2. Under Select Form, tap the form you wish to complete.
  3. Enter information in the fields as required.
    • To enter information in a signature field, trace the signature with your finger or a digital pen, then tap Save. Tap Clear to delete the signature.
    • To enter information in a date or number field, select from the drop-down lists, then tap Finished.
    • To embed an image in an image in an image field, tap the field, then tap New Image, capture the desired photograph, then tap Use Photo.
  4. Attach additional images by tapping the camera icon, then capturing the desired photograph and tapping Use Photo.
  5. Tap Send to submit the form to the eForms Portal, then tap Ok to confirm.
    • Alternatively, to save the form as a draft, tap the save icon, enter a form name, tap Save, then tap Ok to confirm.
    • Tap the trash icon to delete a form.

When a form has submitted successfully, you will be returned to the dashboard and an Upload successful message will appear. Tap Ok to close.

Edit form drafts

To edit form drafts:

  1. Go to the dashboard and tap Drafts.
  2. Locate the desired form and tap the edit icon.
    • Alternatively, tap the list icon to rename the form or tap the trash icon to delete it.
  3. Make change as desired.
  4. Tap Send to submit the form to the eForms Portal, then tap Ok to confirm.
    • Alternatively, to resave the form, tap the save icon, confirm or edit the form name, tap Save, then tap Ok to confirm.
