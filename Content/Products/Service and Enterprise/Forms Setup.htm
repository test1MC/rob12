Forms Setup

Overview

The Forms Setup tab is located under System > Setup > Forms Setup. The settings here affect the layout of forms in simPRO.

Forms General Configuration

Use the Forms General Configuration section to set up the default page layout and PDF viewer options for all forms.

Page Setup sub-tab

Use the options under the Page Setup sub-tab to control form layout.

The options available are:

Display

Adjust display options, such as whether your forms will Show Letterhead, and adjust Letterhead Height and Footer Height.

Address Box

Select whether the address box should have its Position based on System Default, or set the alignment and width manually. The default setting suits window-faced envelopes.

Letterhead Layout

Adjust how the letterhead appears on your form. You can add one to four columns to a letterhead, and assign each column a percentage of the available width.

Assign and manage fields for each column, and use the drag handles to reposition fields.

Signature

Select the content to display in a form's footer area. Choose from a range of details to add, such as Logged-In Employee Name, Company Name and Company Email.

If you have uploaded your company letterhead as a PDF Background in the System > Setup > Company, adjust the letterhead layout settings as required to avoid overriding or duplicating information displayed.

 PDF Viewer Options sub-tab

Use the options under the PDF Viewer Options sub-tab to control how form PDFs are viewed in simPRO.

The options available are:

General

Adjust the basic settings of simPRO's PDF Viewer, such as Full Screen, Layout, Toolbar and Zoom.

Form Options

Choose whether to print a DL fold line, and whether the Page Number displays.

Email

Select if you wish to Send Email With Company Logo.

PDF Permission

Limit access to PDFs by setting a password to print, modify, copy or extract.
Email Setup sub-tab

Use the Email Setup sub-tab to apply system-wide settings to all emailable forms. If you wish to adjust email template settings for an individual form, for example in System > Setup > Forms Setup > Invoice > Email, these default settings will be overridden.

If you need to adjust an email template of a form for an individual customer, you can make changes to the email Subject and Message when sending the email from the Forms tab. The original template will remain the same.

The options available are:

Send Email From

System Default: The From Name and From Address fields will not be available to edit when sending an email. Emails will send from message-service@simprogroup.com. Replies to an email will be delivered to the email address of the user logged in when the email was sent.

Use Logged in User's Email Details: Emails will send from the logged-in user's email address defined in their employee / contractor card. The company email address will be used if there is no email saved in their employee / contractor card.

Manually Specified Below: Add a desired From Name and From Address for sending emails. Ensure your SPF record is set up first.

Request Read Receipt

Choose whether to request and receive read receipts for sent emails.

Send CC of Email to Current Logged In User

Choose whether to send a copy of the email to the logged-in user.

CC to

Add any email addresses you want to automatically receive a copy.

Email Template

Create a template to apply to all emails sent through simPRO, using insert fields to automatically generate details. The [body] field must be used if you have customised text in each individual form's Email Template in Form Setup (see screenshot below), and you can also insert other fields such as the sender's name and role, images and hyperlinks. This template will apply regardless of whether email signatures have been set up on individual forms, so adjust settings accordingly.

 Forms Setup sub-tabs

There are a range of other helpful sub-tabs located under the various Forms Setup tabs, such as under Barcode, Contractor, Invoice, Job, Lead, Purchase Order and Supplier Quote.

The sub-tabs available are:

General

Adjust general options such as Label Layout, and whether to display a Part Number or Description.

Job

Select whether to display Total Show Materials/Labour Breakdown for a contractor job form, and adjust Terms and Conditions options.

Invoice

Customise Header Fields and adjust Terms and Conditions options for contractor invoices.

Email

Choose whether to Use default settings or adjust email settings for individual types of forms, overriding the system-wide email settings.

Display Options

Choose what displays on a form and adjust display settings such as Footnote, Cost Centres, Variation Sections, Site Detail Headers and Materials/Labour.

Consolidated Invoice

Assign and manage columns for consolidated invoices.

How To Pay

Select How to Pay section details for invoices, quotes and statement forms, such as How To Pay Section Height, Payments Options and Payment Claim Message.

Forms

Assign and manage the forms you can choose from in the Form List when creating a PDF.

Detail Forms

Assign and manage the detail forms you can choose from in the Form List when creating a PDF. The detail form is set up from within a quote or a job.

Sign Off Area

Adjust settings for the Sign Off Area of a jobcard and add a statement, such as 'The job is performed to my complete satisfaction.'

Scripts

Create scripts to choose from when communicating with customers and sites.

Overdue Letters

Activate and create a custom message to email customers with outstanding accounts.

Overdue Letter - Final

Activate and create a custom message to email a final notice to customers with outstanding accounts.

Claim Statement

Adjust claim statement options and create a Payment Claim Statement to include important information, such as legal disclaimers.

Columns

Assign and manage columns for a statement form.

Display Options sub-tab

To customise how information is displayed in a specific form in simPRO, go to System > Setup > Forms Setup, click the desired form, then click the Display Options sub-tab.

In addition to adjusting how elements such as the header, footer, terms and conditions, and more are displayed on a form, you can also enter a new form title to display on the printable form document. The form will still retain the simPRO default title when you select it from the Form drop-down list.

To enter a new form title:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Forms Setup.
  2. Click the desired form in the Forms Setup menu.
  3. Click Display Options.
  4. Under [form] Title, enter the new title as desired.
  5. Click Save.

In System > Setup > Forms Setup > Quote > Display Options, you can enter an additional title to display if you select Show as Estimate when emailing a quote form.

You may wish to adjust the form email template in the Email sub-tab to reflect new form titles.
