Add New Geofences

Overview

Create geofences to monitor the movements of vehicles in the field. You can use geofences to define restricted areas or areas of interest, such as toll roads or state boundaries.

You can set up alerts to recieve an email or an onscreen alert when a vehicle enters or exits a geofence.

Add a new geofence

To add a new geofence:

  1. Go to Map > GeoFences.
  2. Click New Geofence.
Draw a geofence

The drag and draw icons will appear in the top left corner of the map. This means you are in drawing mode.

To draw a geofence:

  1. Click on the map to create the first point of the geofence.
  2. Draw your geofence by continuing to click points on the map.
  3. Double-click to finish.
  4. Name the geofence.
  5. Enter a Description if desired.
  6. Set a speed limit if you wish to create an overspeed alert for this geofence.
  7. Select Vehicle(s) or Group(s) to apply the geofence as desired, then select the specific vehicles or groups from the drop-down list.
  8. Click Create.

 Manage geofences

Click Manage to Manage Geofences and Sites.
