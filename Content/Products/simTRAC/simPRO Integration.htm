simPRO Integration

Overview

When you integrate simPRO with simTRAC, you can send jobs directly to vehicles from simPRO. You can also map data from simPRO onto simTRAC maps.

Integrate simPRO and simTRAC

You will need to enable integration in both simPRO and simTRAC.

To enable simTRAC integration in simPRO:

  1. Log in to simPRO.
  2. Go to System Setup >Defaults > Extensions.
  3. Under Tracking Extensions > simTRAC, select On.
  4. Click Save.

To enable simPRO integration in simTRAC:

  1. Log in to simTRAC.
  2. Go to Settings > Manage Company Details.
  3. Select the simPRO Integration check box.
  4. Enter your simPRO company URL.
  5. Click Update to save your settings.

To assign a vehicle to an employee:

  1. Go to People > Employees / Contractors and click the name of an employee / contractor.
  2. Under Profile > Details > Vehicle Registration, click Connect to simTRAC.
  3. Log in to simTRAC.
  4. Under Vehicle Registration, select the vehicle you want to assign.
  5. Click Save.

To send job details to vehicles, you will need access to the correct security group.

To allow a security group to send job details:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Security Groups.
  2. Click the security group you want to have access.
  3. Under Permissions, click the Schedules tab.
  4. Select the Send Vehicle check box.
  5. Click Save or Finish.
 Send a message from simPRO

To send a message from simPRO:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day / Week View.
  2. Right-click a schedule for an employee / contractor who has a vehicle assigned.
  3. Hover over Send and click Vehicle.

The driver's navigator will receive all job details as a message.

 Load simPRO data

By integrating simPRO and simTRAC, you can load quotes, jobs and sites from simPRO directly onto simTRAC and view them on the map.

To connect to your simPRO database:

  1. Click the Work tab.
  2. Click Connect to simPRO.
  3. Click Authorise.

Once you have connected to your simPRO database, you will then see your company name at the top of the page in the Work tab. If you have a multi-company simPRO database, you can change the company by clicking the company name and selecting another company from the list.

Load quotes and jobs

To load quotes or jobs from your simPRO database:

  1. Click the Work tab.
  2. Select Quotes or Jobs as desired.
  3. Select Scheduled or Not Scheduled as desired.
  4. Choose your date range and relevant cost centres.
  5. Click Search.

simTRAC will now connect with simPRO and load all of the jobs or quotes within the specified date range. View job details by clicking a job in the list or by clicking the pin on the map.

Click the arrow under your company name to change the search filter.

 Load sites

To load sites from your simPRO database:

  1. Click the Work tab.
  2. Select Sites.
  3. Click Search.
  4. Select the size and site type from the drop-down lists at the bottom of the Work tab and click Save Sites to create the sites on the map.

These sites will remain on the map even when simTRAC is not connected to simPRO.

 Incorrect addresses

If there is a line through a site name in the results list, this means that Google has been unable to find that specific address. Hover over the site name to find any close matches. You can select another address to pin the job in the correct place. Sometimes the correct address cannot be found because the wrong address has been entered into simPRO.
