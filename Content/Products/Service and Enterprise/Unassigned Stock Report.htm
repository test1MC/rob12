Unassigned Stock Report

Overview

Use the Unassigned Stock report to view either jobs that have not yet been assigned stock or stock required for a job that has yet to be assigned. This report may be used by stock controllers and project managers to determine which jobs need to have stock assigned to meet the quoted materials requirement.

View Unassigned Stock report

To view the report:

  1. Go to Reports > View Reports.
  2. Hover over Stock and click Unassigned Stock.
  3. Filter the report by selecting from the drop-down lists.
  4. Select a specific Customer, Site, or Job Number, Stock Item Name or Part Number, if desired.
  5. Under Group Results By, select whether to group results by Job Number, Part Number or to have No Grouping.
  6. Click Search.
