Assigned Stock Report

Overview

Use the assigned stock report to view jobs that have had stock assigned. You can control what the report displays using different search filters, as well as download the report into a CSV file to distribute internally and to customers.

View the assigned stock report

To view the report:

  1. Go to Reports > View Reports.
  2. In the left column, click Stock > Assigned Stock.
  3. Filter results by:
    • Selecting a date range under Assigned.
    • Selecting the check boxes in the drop-down lists.
    • Entering information under Customer, Site, Job Number, Stock Item Name or Part Number.
  4. Select whether the results are grouped by No Grouping, Job Number, Part Number or Date Allocated.
  5. Click Search.
