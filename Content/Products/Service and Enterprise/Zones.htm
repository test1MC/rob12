Zones

Overview

In simPRO, a zone is a geographical area used to schedule efficiently and minimise travel time for technicians.

After zones are set up, they can be assigned to a site, employee, contractor, or plant item.

For example, if you assign a zone to a site, then create a job for that site, you will only be able to view and schedule technicians assigned to that zone.

Create a zone

To create a zone:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Zones.
  2. Click Create Zone.
  3. Enter a zone Name.
  4. Click Create.
 Edit a zone

To edit a zone:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Zones.
  2. Click the zone you wish to edit.
  3. Make changes as desired.
  4. Click Save.
 Delete a zone

To delete a zone:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Zones.
  2. On the zone you wish to delete, click the red delete icon.
  3. Click OK.

To delete multiple zones at once:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Zones.
  2. Select the check box for the zones you want to delete, or select Select All.
  3. Click Delete.
  4. Click OK.
 Assign a zone Assign a zone to a site

To assign a zone to a site:

  1. Create a new site, or open an existing site card file.
  2. Under Profile > Details, click the Zone field and select a zone from the drop-down list.
  3. Adjust the rest of the site details as needed, then click Finish.

To assign a zone to a site through a customer card:

  1. Create a new customer, or open an existing customer card file.
  2. Click the Sites tab.
  3. Click the site you wish to assign a zone to.
  4. Under Profile > Details, click the Zone field and select a zone from the drop-down list.
  5. Adjust the rest of the site details as needed, then click Finish.
  6. Adjust the rest of the customer details as needed, then click Finish.
 Assign a zone to an employee or contractor

To assign a zone to an employee or contractor:

  1. Create a new employee or contractor, or open an existing employee or contractor card file.
  2. Go to Profile > Schedule > Zones.
  3. Click a zone in Available Zones to add it to the Assigned Zones.
  4. Adjust the rest of the card file as needed, then click Finish.

 Assign a zone to a plant item

To assign a zone to a plant item:

  1. Create a new plant item or open an existing plant item.
  2. Under Details, ensure the Enable Scheduling check box is selected.
  3. Click the Schedule tab.
  4. Click a zone in Available Zones to add it to Assigned Zones.
  5. Adjust the rest of the plant item as needed, then click Finish.
