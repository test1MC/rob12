simPRO Menu Structure

Overview

When you log in to simPRO, you will be taken to the dashboard.

The dashboard provides an essential overview of your company's ongoing activity and information. In simPRO Enterprise, you can customise the information the dashboard displays in your security group settings.

Access the System menu, the Utilities menu and the main menu from the dashboard to manage how simPRO operates with your business.

System menu

Access the System menu in the far top right of the dashboard to manage your simPRO account.

To log out of simPRO and make the licence available for another user, click the logout icon.

Announcements from simPRO, such as feature updates, will appear in the menu bar in an orange circle icon. If there are no new announcements, the circle will not appear.

The red circle icon indicates there are system alerts that require your attention. System alerts, which are not to be confused with notifications, may appear in the event of:

  • Expiry of licences or customer contracts.
  • Quotes, jobs or tasks due for follow up.
  • Low stock levels.
  • Required asset or plant management.

To access your employee card file, hover over your name, then click My Account. Click Change Password to update your password.

Hover over System to access:

Setup

Access setup options for your system, forms, defaults, mobile, company and accounts integration.

User Log

View current licences in use and the system access logs.

Subscriptions / Licences

Manage the number of licences available to your business. To change the number of licences, go to System > Subscriptions / Licences and change as desired.

The Subscriptions / Licences option will only appear in the System drop-down menu for users with company management security group permissions.
