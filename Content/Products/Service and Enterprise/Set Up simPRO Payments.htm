Set Up simPRO Payments

Fees and settlement periods Australia

You will receive a customer's payment approximately two business days from the date of their transaction. For example, if a customer pays your invoice through simPRO Payments on a Monday, your account will be credited that Wednesday. Payments processed on weekends, out of hours and on public holidays may take additional time.

For information on setup fees, monthly fees and transaction fees for simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay) in Australia, please see the simPRO website.

 New Zealand

You will receive a customer's payment approximately three business days from the date of their transaction. For example, if a customer pays your invoice through simPRO Payments on a Monday, your account will be credited that Thursday. Payments processed on weekends, out of hours and on public holidays may take additional time.

For information on setup fees, monthly fees and transaction fees for simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay) in New Zealand, please see the simPRO website.

 United Kingdom

You will receive a customer's payment approximately seven business days from the date of their transaction. For example, if a customer pays your invoice through simPRO Payments on a Monday, your account will be credited the following Wednesday. Payments processed on weekends, out of hours and on public holidays may take additional time.

For information on setup fees, monthly fees and transaction fees for simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay) in the United Kingdom, please see the simPRO website.

 United States

You will receive a customer's payment the following business day from the date of their transaction. For example, if a customer pays your invoice through simPRO Payments on a Monday, your account will be credited that Tuesday. Payments processed on weekends, out of hours and on public holidays may take additional time.

For information on setup fees, monthly fees and transaction fees for simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay) in the United States, please see the simPRO website.

 Register for simPRO Payments

You can register for simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay) through your simPRO system. Only users with security group permission for System > Setup > Defaults can complete this process.

Please note that when you click Register, your information will be sent to IntegraPay, the third party provider for simPRO Payments. IntegraPay will then contact you directly to set up your new merchant account.

By registering, you acknowledge that you will be submitting your details to a third party, you agree to the Third Party Services clause of the simPRO Terms & Conditions, and you agree to be contacted by IntegraPay. Review simPRO's Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.

To register for simPRO Payments:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Defaults > Extensions.
  2. Under simPRO Payments, click Register.
  3. Enter all required information.
  4. Click Register.

After you register, IntegraPay will contact you within 48 hours by phone or email. They will then email you detailed information, and an application form you will need to complete and return to set up a merchant account with IntegraPay.

If you have any questions about the application form, please contact IntegraPay using the details provided on the application form.

 Receive confirmation of registration

After you return your completed application form to IntegraPay, if your application is successful, you will receive an email from simPRO confirming you have successfully created a new merchant account and activated simPRO Payments. This will occur within three business days. You will need to follow the instructions below to finalise setup in your system.

If your application for a merchant account is not successful, IntegraPay will contact you directly.

 Finalize simPRO Payments setup in your system

After simPRO has contacted you to confirm simPRO Payments has been activated in your system, you need to complete a few simple steps to make it work the way your business requires.

Enable payment options

Once simPRO Payments is activated in your system, all invoices to customers will contain a link to pay the invoice online (if set up through your invoice form setup).

To enable the remaining simPRO Payments options:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Defaults > Extensions.
  2. Under Collect simPRO Payments Via, select payment options as desired (see explanation below).
  3. Click Save.
  4. For each option you enable, you need to follow the steps below to finalise the simPRO Payments setup:
    • Enable Connect payment methods
    • Enable Invoice pages in the customer portal
    • Add a link to invoices
    • Add a link to emails

The three options are:

Mobile

Enable mobile billing in Connect so technicians can collect credit card payments in the field.

Enterprise / Service

Enable employees with the necessary security group permissions to collect payments over the phone through simPRO Enterprise or Service.

Customer Portal

Enable customers to pay invoices through the customer portal, depending on the pages you have enabled. This option also enables the Pay Online link to appear in customer invoices.
Associate payment methods

To enable customers to pay you through simPRO Payments, you need to associate simPRO Payment credit card types with your existing default payment methods.

To associate payment methods with simPRO Payments:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup.
  2. In the left column, click Accounts, then click Payment Methods.
  3. Under simPRO Payments, click the Card Type you wish to associate with an existing payment method.
  4. Under Edit simPRO Payment Method, select the corresponding default payment method Type from the drop-down list for that card type.
  5. Click Save.

If you do not wish to make a certain card type available for customers to use in simPRO Payments, do not associate it with an existing payment method, or delete the existing payment method if necessary.

 Pass on transaction fees

To pass transaction fees on to your customers, you need to apply a Finance Charge to the relevant card type.

To apply a finance charge to a card type:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup.
  2. In the left column, click Accounts, then select Payment Methods.
  3. Click the payment method you wish to apply a finance charge to.
  4. Enter the desired Finance Charge percentage.
  5. Click Save.
 Add a link to invoices

To add a Pay Online link to your invoices to customers, you need to include credit cards as a payment option in the How to Pay section of your invoice form setup.

To check or enable this:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Forms Setup.
  2. In the left column, click Invoice.
  3. Click the How to Pay sub-tab.
  4. Under Payment Options, check the columns to ensure that Credit Card has been assigned. To assign it, select it from the Options Assigned drop-down list, then click Add Fields.
  5. Click Save.

The Pay Online link will only appear in your customers' invoices if you have enabled the Customer Portal payment option.

 Add a link to emails

To add an online payment link to the body of an email when emailing an invoice or statement, you need to adjust your Email Template settings.

To add an online payment link to emails:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Forms Setup.
  2. In the left column, click Invoice or Statement as desired.
  3. Click the Email sub-tab.
  4. Place the cursor in the Body text box, in the place you want the link to appear.
  5. Under Insert Field, click Online Payment Link.
  6. Format the rest of the Body as desired.
  7. Click Save.

Your invoice or statement emails will now automatically include the online payment link.

If your invoices are in multiple or foreign currencies, do not use this feature. Foreign currency payments cannot be processed through simPRO Payments.

 Use simPRO Payments in the customer portal

If you allow them, customers can pay invoices from within your customer portal in Invoices.

To activate paying invoices through the customer portal:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Defaults > Customer Portal > Enabled Pages.
  2. Under Invoices, select Unpaid and Statement.
  3. Click Save.
 Enable payment methods for Connect

To allow your technicians to collect a credit card payment on their mobile device before leaving a site, you need to enable simPRO Payments-associated payment methods for Connect.

To enable payment methods for Connect:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Mobile > General.
  2. Under Billing Settings, check Mobile Billing is enabled, or click Enable.
  3. Under Payment Methods, select Enable for Connect for the payment methods you wish to make available through Connect.
  4. Click Save.

If mobile billing is not enabled under simPRO Payments in Extensions, technicians will only be able to record credit card payments in the field, in addition to payments made with cash or cheque.

 Use simPRO Payments in a multi-company build

To set up simPRO Payments in a multi-company build that does not share default settings:

  1. Follow the registration process above for each company that requires simPRO Payments.
  2. After you are notified that simPRO Payments has been activated in your system, go into your Template company.
  3. Go to System > Setup > Defaults > Extensions.
  4. Under Collect simPRO Payments Via, ensure Customer Portal is selected.
  5. Go to System Setup > Accounts > Payment Methods.
  6. Associate payment methods with the required simPRO Payments methods as above.
  7. Repeat this process for each individual company you want simPRO Payments to be available in.
 Start using simPRO Payments

Once you have finalised setup, you can start using simPRO Payments straight away!
