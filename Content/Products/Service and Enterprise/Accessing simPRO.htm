Accessing simPRO

Overview

You can log in to your simPRO build at any time, and from any device or internet browser.

To log in you will need:

  • A stable internet connection
  • A web browser, preferably Google Chrome
  • The Login Address, Username and Password given by simPRO.
    • The Login Address will most likely appear as 'https://[yourcompanyname].simprosuite.com'

Your username and password can be adjusted and saved in Profile > Settings in your employee card file, and are issued by your system administrator.

In the interest of security, do not share your login credentials. The user log / history files are recorded based on the login credentials and are used for data tracking.

Log in to simPRO

​To log in to simPRO:

  1. Open the web browser.
  2. Enter the Login Address URL in the address bar, then press Enter on your keyboard.
  3. Enter your Username and Password.
  4. Click Login.
Log in with your Google account

Alternatively, you can use your Google account to log in to simPRO.

Open the web browser.

  1. Enter the Login Address URL in the address bar, then press Enter on your keyboard.
  2. Click Sign in with Google to open the Request for Permission page.
  3. Click Allow to open a different simPRO login page.
  4. Enter your Username and Password.
  5. Click Login.
 Log out of simPRO

To log out of simPRO, click the logout icon in the System menu in the top right corner.

Once you have finished using simPRO, you need to log out to release the licence for another employee to use.
