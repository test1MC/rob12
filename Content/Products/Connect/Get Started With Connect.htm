Get Started With Connect

Overview

You can use Connect to view, create and update jobs, quotes and activities. You can also update schedules, manage purchase orders and, in simPRO Enterprise, tasks, and create invoices in the field.

To log in to Connect to make use of these features, you need to Get licences, Enable mobility, and Download Connect. You can then update your settings in simPRO when you Set Up Connect according to your business needs.

Get licences

To use simPRO Connect, you need to purchase a licence for each individual who will be using the app.

To purchase licences for Connect or adjust the quantity of active licences, existing clients can go to System > Subscriptions (simPRO Service ) / Licences (simPRO Enterprise) in their simPRO system. New clients can contact simPRO through the simPRO website.

Licences are a monthly recurring fee, and you can adjust the number of active licences each month. For example, you may wish to purchase additional licences for temporary staff during a busy period, and deactivate those licences when you no longer need them.

As Connect is a separately licensed add-on product, your simPRO user licence does not give you access to Connect, nor does giving your field employees access to Connect require you to give them access to simPRO.

Download Connect

simPRO Connect is free to download from the App Store and Google Play for Compatible devices. Simply access the relevant store online, and search for simPRO Connect. You may need to sign in to the store and accept terms and conditions.

We recommend that you allow automatic updates on your device, if available. This will ensure you are always running the most up-to-date version of Connect.

Troubleshooting

If you experience any difficulty with downloading the app, or if you cannot see the app in the store:

  • Check that your device and operating system are Compatible devices for Connect.
  • Update to a more recent version of iOS or Android, unless other app compatibilities prevent you from doing so. Even if your device is not listed as compatible with Connect, the app may work so long as you are using a supported operating system.
  • Contact your device manufacturer or mobile service provider if you have trouble accessing your device store.

If you are using a compatible device and operating system but you are still experiencing difficulty, contact simPRO Support and provide the following information:

  • The make and model of your device, e.g. iPhone 4s or Samsung Galaxy S3.
  • The operating system and version, e.g. iOS 7.1 or Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean).
  • The issue you are experiencing.

Enable mobility

To allow your employees and, in simPRO Enterprise, contractors to use Connect, you need to assign licences to their employee or contractor cards.

To assign a mobility licence to an employee or contractor:

  1. Go to People > Employees / Contractors and click the desired employee or contractor.
  2. Click Profile > Settings.
  3. Under Account Setup, ensure that a Username and password have been set.
  4. Under Licences, select Mobility.
  5. Click Save to update your changes.

If the Mobility check box is greyed out, this means all of your available licences are in use. You can remove a mobile licence from another employee / contractor and reassign it to another, or alternatively, you can purchase a new licence. To remove a licence, clear the Mobility check box in the desired employee's / contractor's card, then click Save.

Username and password

If you are creating a new employee or, in simPRO Enterprise, contractor, or granting access for the first time, a default username will be shown and the password will be blank. If the username you enter is already in use, a red cross will appear and you will not be able to save it. A green tick indicates a unique username.

The username and password you give a user will apply to all areas of simPRO that you have given them access to, including as a general User of simPRO, the customer portal, and in simPRO Enterprise, POS, Call Centre, and as a User of the Employee Portal.

You can change the username and password at any time.

Log in to Connect

When you have successfully downloaded the app, obtained and enabled user licences and set a username and password, you can log in to Connect to access the app's functionalities.

To log in to Connect:

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Enter your simPRO URL. This is the web address you use to access simPRO, for example, yourcompany.simprosuite.com or yourcompany.simprocloud.com.
  3. Enter your simPRO Username and Password.
  4. Tap Login.

You will then be taken to the Connect dashboard. See Navigate the dashboard for more information.

You can also access Connect's In-app settings before logging in by tapping Settings on the login page.

To log out of Connect, tap the logout icon available in the top right in any page in Connect, then tap Yes to confirm. Connect will remember your simPRO URL and username on the device for your next login.
