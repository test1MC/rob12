Why Can't I Access the Ideas Portal?

Overview

If you have received an email notification that a feature idea you have created or subscribed to has been updated, you need to log in to your simPRO build to directly access the feature idea in the Ideas Portal.​

Alternatively, log in to your simPRO build, go to Help > Submit an Idea > My subscriptions, then locate the updated idea.

Unsubscribe from feature update emails

To unsubscribe from feature update emails:

  1. Log in to simPRO Service or Enterprise as normal.
  2. Go to Help > Submit an Idea.
  3. In the left column, click My Subscriptions.
  4. Click the feature you wish to unsubscribe from.
  5. Click Unsubscribe on the right.
