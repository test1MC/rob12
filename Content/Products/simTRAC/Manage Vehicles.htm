Manage Vehicles

Overview

Use the Manage Vehicles page to change vehicle details. You can also perform a once-off service in the Maintenance History tab in a vehicle.

Edit a vehicle

To change vehicle details:

  1. Go to Settings > Manage Vehicles.
  2. Use the search field to find a specific vehicle.
  3. Click the edit icon.
  4. Enter vehicle information, such as make, model, and year of manufacture. Some of this information is included in service booking emails sent from maintenance planner.
  5. Click Update.

Only vehicles that have a simTRAC tracker installed will be available in this list.

Driver History

While editing a vehicle, click the Driver History tab to view what drivers have been assigned to a vehicle in the past.

 Maintenance History

While editing a vehicle, click the Maintenance History tab to view a list of all services performed on that vehicle.

You can also perform a once-off service on a vehicle without having to book it in the maintenance planner.

To perform a once-off service:

  1. Click Perform Once-Off Service.
  2. Enter service details.
  3. Click Save.

Once-off services can be edited and deleted, unlike booked services.
