Manage Geofences and Sites

Overview

Manage sites and geofences in simTRAC in Settings > Manage Geofences and Sites.

You can edit and delete geofences and sites, as well as add alert triggers to receive immediate updates of vehicle locations by email or on your desktop.

Manage geofences

Use geofences to mark larger areas such as city and state boundaries and toll roads.

Edit a geofence

To edit geofence details:

  1. Go to Map > GeoFences > Manage.
  2. Alternatively, go to Settings > Manage Geofences and Sites.
  3. Click the edit icon on the geofence you wish to edit.
  4. Make changes as desired.
  5. Click Update.

If you wish to change the shape of a geofence without editing other details, click Edit on the geofence in the list. Alternatively, click the geofence on the map and click Edit.

Click and drag the white dots as desired to change the shape of the geofence. Click Save to finish your changes, or click Cancel. To undo an individual change, click the undo icon.

 Delete a geofence

To delete a geofence:

  1. Go to Map > GeoFences > Manage.
  2. Alternatively, go to Settings > Manage Geofences and Sites.
  3. Click the delete icon on the geofence you wish to delete.
 Add an alert trigger to a geofence

If you wish to receive immediate updates on vehicles entering or exiting a particular geofence, you may wish to add an alert trigger to it.

 Manage sites

Use sites to mark smaller areas such as employees' homes and work sites.

Edit a site

To edit site details:

  1. Go to Map > GeoFences > Manage.
  2. Alternatively, go to Settings > Manage Geofences and Sites.
  3. Click the edit icon on the site you wish to edit.
  4. Make changes as desired.
  5. Click Update.

If you wish to move a site or change the size without editing other details, you can click Edit on the site in the list. Alternatively, click the site on the map and click Edit.

To move a site, click and drag the white circle in the centre. To change the size, click and drag the white dots on the site boundary.

Click Save to finish your changes, or click Cancel to return to the initial location or size. To undo an individual change, click the undo icon.

 Delete a site

To delete a site:

  1. Go to Map > GeoFences > Manage.
  2. Alternatively, go to Settings > Manage Geofences and Sites.
  3. Click the delete icon on the site you wish to delete.
 Add an alert trigger to a site

If you wish to receive immediate updates on vehicles entering or exiting a particular site, you can add an alert trigger to it.

 Create a site type

If you manage many different sites, you can search and report on these sites according to their type, for example, customer sites, employee homes and work sites.

To create a site type:

  1. Go to Settings > Manage Sites & Geofences.
  2. Click Manage Site Types.
  3. Click Add Site Type.
  4. Name the new site type.
  5. Click Create.

To edit a site type, click the edit icon. Make changes as desired, then click Update.

To remove a site type, click the delete icon.
