Connect Tools

Navigate the dashboard

The dashboard is the first page you view after logging in to Connect, and allows access to all of Connect's functionalities.

Tap the Connect icon in the top left to View notifications.

The focus date is displayed in the top centre of the dashboard and controls which jobs, quotes and activities are displayed when you View the runsheet for Today. It defaults to the current day during which you are logged in to Connect. To change the focus date, simply tap the date, select a different date as desired, then tap Finished.

System alerts, such as a warning to sync Connect with simPRO if a sync has not been performed for more than twelve hours, will appear beneath the focus date. Tap an alert to action it.

To log out of Connect, tap the power button icon in the top right.

The white list menu at the top of the page allows you to view the jobs, quotes, activities and, in simPRO Enterprise, tasks that are due or scheduled:

Runsheet View the runsheet to find all assigned or scheduled jobs, quotes and activities. The number next to Today indicates how many jobs, quotes and activities are assigned or scheduled for the focus date. The number next to All indicates the total number of assigned or scheduled jobs, quotes and activities according to their status, as set up when you Manage data retrieval.
Tasks (only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise) View and complete tasks created in Enterprise. The number next to Today indicates how many tasks are required for jobs assigned or scheduled for the focus date. The number next to All indicates how many tasks are required for all assigned or scheduled jobs according to their status.
Retrieve Download data for a specific job or quote that you have been assigned or scheduled to, for example, a job that has been removed from the runsheet upon completion depending on how you Manage sync operations. To download data, simply tap Retrieve, enter the Job / Quote ID number, then tap Find.

The blue panel in the centre of the dashboard offers three quick create options:

Job / Quote (simPRO Service) 

Project (simPRO Enterprise)

 Create a job in Connect or Create a quote in Connect if you have enabled this function in General settings in simPRO.
Activity Schedule activities created in simPRO to keep track of non-billable time.
PO (only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise) Create stock purchase orders or Request a purchase order number to be completed in Enterprise to re-stock your storage device.

The options at the bottom of the dashboard allow you to manage how the data you have entered in Connect reflects and updates in simPRO:

Sync Sync all data in Connect with simPRO.
Settings Manage your In-app settings including data retrieval, sync operations and your Connect and device information.

Stock POs

(only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise)

 View a list of all outstanding and receipted stock purchase orders created in Connect. See Create stock purchase orders for more information.
Reports Generate and send reports for job cards or runsheets.

Purchase orders created in jobs will not appear when viewing Stock POs from the dashboard (only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise).

 View notifications

When a job, quote or activity is scheduled, edited or removed in simPRO, or when a simPRO user right-clicks a schedule and clicks Send > Notification, you will receive a notification in Connect when connected to the internet. If you are using Connect offline, new notifications will not appear until you are online again.

Notifications are only received for additions or changes to the current day schedule according to the focus date. A new notification will appear in the top of the screen for a few seconds. The number of new notifications will also appear in a blue circle over the Connect icon and will remain until the notifications have been viewed.

To view and open notifications, tap the Connect icon. Read notifications display with grey font and icons.

You can turn off notifications that appear outside of the app in your device settings. Notifications will still appear in the Connect app.

Send manual notifications / messages

When scheduling a job, quote or activity in simPRO, you can manually send a notification in addition to the automatic notification if you want to include a message for a field technician.

Notifications to Connect are delayed by 30 seconds when scheduling a job, quote or activity from within Schedules. This is to prevent multiple or unnecessary notifications while schedules are being finalised.

To manually send a notification from simPRO:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day / Week / Project View.
  2. Right-click the desired time block.
  3. Hover over Send and click Notification.
    • This option will only appear if the technician has a mobile licence allocated in their employee or, in simPRO Enterprise, contractor card.
  4. Enter the desired message, then click Send > OK.

Alternatively, if your notification is not related to activities, quotes or jobs, click the technician's name, then click Send Notification. The message will then appear on the Notifications page in Connect.

You can only manually send notifications from simPRO to field technicians with a mobile licence enabled in their employee or, in simPRO Enterprise, contractor card. See Enable mobility for more information.

 View the runsheet

Use the runsheet to view details for jobs, quotes, and activities that are due or scheduled. You can also contact site, customer or project managers and locate sites on your device's mapping solution.

To access the runsheet, open Connect and tap Runsheet. Tap Today to view all assigned jobs, quotes and activities that are due or scheduled for the focus date selected on the Connect dashboard.

To change the focus date, tap Back, then tap the date at the top of the page, select the desired date, then tap Finished. When Connect has finished performing a sync with simPRO, the runsheet will display all assigned jobs, quotes, and activities due or scheduled for the updated date.

Tap Other to view all other assigned jobs and quotes according to their status, in addition to activities scheduled on the focus date. You can control which statuses you view in Other in your In-app settings.

View the runsheet for a 48-hour period

Enable 48-hour scheduling in your In-app settings to view and schedule jobs, quotes and activities within a 48-hour period of time. This option is useful if you frequently schedule field employees for night shifts.

To view your schedule for a 48-hour period, access your Today runsheet. You can then view all assigned or scheduled jobs, quotes, and activities within a 48-hour period, starting from the focus date set in the dashboard.

Jobs scheduled over midnight will split into two job cards in the runsheet; one for before midnight, and one for after. Any changes made in the pre-midnight job card will reflect in the post-midnight job card.

Access a job or quote

Jobs and quotes listed in the runsheet will display the customer name and job or quote number as well as the cost centre number and description. They will also show the scheduled time of the job or quote, the due date, and the site name and address. Activities listed in the runsheet will display the activity type, start and finish times, and any relevant notes.

To find a job or quote according to specific details, tap Filter, then select the Customer or Site, or alternatively, enter the Job / Quote ID. Then tap Filter.

Tap the phone icon to view contact information for the Site, Job or Quote Customer or Project Manager.

If you are using Connect on a device that can place phone calls and has an active connection to the mobile network, tap the desired phone number to initiate a phone call with the relevant contact.

If you are using a device that cannot make phone calls, tap the desired phone number, then tap:

Send Message To send the desired contact a message using iMessage. To set up iMessage for your device, go to Settings > Messages.
Add to Contacts To add the number to an existing contact or, alternatively, create a new contact in your device's contact list.
Copy To copy the number.

Alternatively, you can simply use the contact information in Connect to make a call from your mobile phone.

Tap the pin icon to locate the site in your device's default mapping solution. Tap the information icon to view a brief overview of the quote or job including the customer, job or quote number and cost centre description, Mobile Status, Job / Quote Status and the job or quote description.

Simply tap a job, quote or activity to open it. If a job or quote was not scheduled or assigned for the specified focus date, a Select Date to Open bubble will appear. Tap Today to open the job or quote according to the current focus date. To open the job or quote according to the originally scheduled or due date, tap the relevant displayed date.

 View and complete tasks

This feature is only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise.

Tasks available in the Connect dashboard are created and assigned to customers in Enterprise. You can then view, edit and complete tasks in Connect, and record the actual amount of time it takes to complete a task. You can also complete tasks within scheduled quotes and jobs.

To access a task from the dashboard, tap Tasks.

Tap Today to view tasks due to be completed by the current focus date. Tap All to view tasks due to be completed, regardless of the focus date.

To complete a task in Connect:

  1. Go to the Dashboard > Tasks.
  2. Tap the desired task.
  3. Review the task description.
  4. Under More information, review the Estimated Time for the task, as well as the Customer, Job, Site information, Status and Priority.
  5. Tap Start to record the actual time taken to complete the task.
  6. Enter Notes as desired.
  7. Enter the Date Completed.
  8. Tap Finished.

Your tasks in simPRO will be updated when you Sync Connect with simPRO.

When you have completed a task with a Date Completed, the task will be marked as Closed in Enterprise.

Tap Stop when timing a task to accurately record how long it takes to complete.

 Sync Connect with simPRO

Information is shared between simPRO and Connect by syncing. This ensures that new or updated jobs, quotes, activities or schedules are reflected in simPRO and Connect for a smooth workflow.

When connected to the internet, data is first synced from Connect to simPRO. Receiving data from simPRO may take some time depending on the amount of data updated, for example, the catalogue takes more time to update in Connect than a job does.

Sync in the background

If you tap Submit on the Submit page of a job or quote in Connect, a background sync with simPRO will begin and you will then be taken back to the runsheet.

Background syncing makes it easy to progress through a job or quote workflow without having to wait for the syncing process to complete. The background sync only syncs the item being submitted, and does not Sync all data.

While Connect is performing a background sync, the relevant job or quote will have a spinning icon in the lower right corner.

Other jobs or quotes on the runsheet will be accessible, but the job or quote that is being synced will be greyed out and restricted from access until the sync has finished.

When the background sync has finished, a confirmation bubble will appear and you will be able to access the job again.

Connect cannot perform a background sync when you change the job status or submit a job while you Work offline, although your updated data will still be stored on the device. When an internet connection is re-established, you can open the created job according to the date it was last modified offline, tap Submit and the background sync will perform as normal.

Sync all data

As background syncing does not sync all data, it is recommended to sync Connect with simPRO at least once a day to ensure data is up to date.

To perform a full sync, tap Sync on Connect's dashboard while your device is connected to the internet. The syncing screen will appear and all Connect features will be unavailable until the sync is complete.

Work offline

If your Connect device cannot access the internet or you have tapped Go Offline in your In-app settings, you can still use Connect offline. In this case, Connect will not reflect changes made in simPRO as the app cannot sync offline. If a sync is in progress when the device goes offline, the data will be available to upload when a connection is re-established, at which point it is recommended you Sync all data.

In addition to syncing, the following Connect functionalities are restricted in offline mode:

  • Receiving notifications
  • Creating a job or quote
  • Adding catalogue items to the Used Materials page of a job
  • Creating, adding items to or receipting a purchase order / purchase order number from the dashboard or the POs page of a job
  • Downloading attachments from a job or quote
  • Accepting a quote in the Quote Summary page
  • Changing a job or quote status in the Submit page
  • Converting a quote to a job in the Submit page
  • Accessing the Billing workflow of a job or quote
  • Sending reports.
 Schedule activities

In Connect, you can use activities to schedule and keep track of non-billable time such as annual leave, training, and toolbox talks. To make activities available to schedule in Connect, you need to set them up in simPRO.

You can view scheduled activities in the runsheet. Tap an activity to view more specific information in Notes.

To schedule an activity:

  1. Tap Activity.
  2. Under Select Activity, select the type of activity you wish to schedule from the drop-down list.
  3. Select the Start and Finish times.
  4. Enter Notes as desired.
  5. Tap Finished.

The activity will then automatically sync with simPRO and appear on the runsheet.

 Create stock purchase orders

This feature is only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise.

Restock your storage device by creating a purchase order in Connect, or alternatively, Request a purchase order number to be completed and receipted in Enterprise. When your ordered items have been fully received, you can also receipt the order in Connect.

To view, Add more items to a purchase order, Receipt a purchase order in Connect, go to Dashboard > Stock POs. Here you can view a list of all purchase orders made, including their order number, supplier, date ordered and order status.

The black box icon indicates an order is still outstanding. A green box icon indicates an order has been receipted in Enterprise or Connect.

To create a purchase order in Connect:

  1. Go to the Dashboard > PO, or alternatively, Dashboard > Stock POs > New PO.
  2. Search and select the desired Supplier.
  3. Enter Private Notes as desired.
  4. Tap Request PO.
  5. Tap Add Materials.
  6. Enter the name or part number of the desired item in Search Catalogue, then tap Search.
    • Alternatively, tap Search to view all catalogue items.
  7. Tap Filter > Select Group to narrow your search according to a specific catalogue group.
    • Tap Select Sub-Group to narrow your search according to a specific catalogue sub-group.
  8. Enter the desired Qty for the relevant items, then tap Add Parts.
    • ​​Alternatively, tap the add icon as desired, then tap Add Parts.
  9. Tap Finished.

To edit an item's quantity in a purchase order, tap the pencil icon, enter the desired number, then tap Finished. To delete an item, tap the pencil icon, then tap Delete > OK to confirm.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet to create purchase orders.

Add more items to a purchase order

To add more items to a purchase order in Connect:

  1. Go to Dashboard > Stock POs.
  2. Tap the desired purchase order.
  3. Tap Catalogue.
  4. Search and add items as normal.
  5. Tap Finished.

Your added items will appear under Materials in the purchase order, including a part description, quantities and price.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet to add items to purchase orders.

Request a purchase order number

To create an empty order to be completed and receipted in Enterprise, you need to request a purchase order number in Connect.

To request a purchase order number:

  1. Go to the Dashboard > PO, or alternatively, Dashboard > Stock POs > New PO.
  2. Search and select the desired Supplier.
  3. Enter Private Notes as desired.
  4. Tap Request PO > Finished.

Receipt a purchase order

To receipt a purchase order in Connect:

  1. Go to the Dashboard > Stock POs.
  2. Tap the desired purchase order.
  3. Tap Receive Goods.
  4. Enter the Packing Slip No.
  5. Tap Receipt All > Finished.

You can only perform a full receipt of goods in Connect and your device needs to be connected to the internet.

 Generate and send reports

Generate and send reports of the runsheet or job cards from Connect.

To send a job card or runsheet report:

  1. Go to the dashboard, then tap Reports.
  2. Select the desired Company if you are operating in a multi-company build.
  3. Under Report, select:
    • Job Cards to send a report of all job cards within the specified date range.
    • Runsheet to send a report of the runsheet, including all scheduled or assigned jobs, quotes and activities, for the specified date range.
  4. Enter the Start Date and End Date to narrow the report to a specific date range.
  5. Tap Submit.

When the report is submitted, an email will be sent to the email address specified in your employee, or in simPRO Enterprise, contractor card in simPRO.
