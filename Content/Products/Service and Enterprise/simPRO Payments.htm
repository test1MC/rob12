simPRO Payments

Overview

simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay) offers a range of convenient options for customers to pay you on-site, online, or over the phone. To activate, you will need to follow the registration instructions for your country, then make a few final changes to adjust this powerful feature to best suit your business.

Rates vary by country. View your country's setup instructions for full rate details.

IntegraPay is a third-party provider for simPRO Payments. IntegraPay is compliant with the Payment Card Industry (PCI) Data Security Standard v3.1, which means that payment security is in accordance with the highest level industry standard. By registering for simPRO Payments, you acknowledge that you will be submitting your details to a third party, you agree to the Third Party Services clause of the simPRO Terms & Conditions, and you agree to be contacted by IntegraPay. Set up simPRO Payments

 How can your customers use simPRO Payments?

Pay invoices online

Once simPRO Payments is activated in your system, your customers can click a link in their invoice to pay immediately with a credit card. Enable customers to pay invoices with credit cards by going to System > Setup > Forms Setup > Invoice > How To Pay > Payment Options and check that Credit Card is assigned.

To pay an invoice online, your customers can simply:

  1. Click the link in the How to Pay section of their invoice.
    • Alternatively, if they are viewing a printed copy of the invoice, they can type in the link in their browser, then enter the invoice number.
  2. Enter their credit card details, including Name on Card, Expiry, Card Number and CVV.
  3. Click Pay.
  4. Click Continue to confirm the payment.
  5. If the payment is successful, a confirmation will appear, along with a receipt number.

Pay invoices through the customer portal

Once simPRO Payments is activated in your system, if your customers use the Customer Portal, they can pay their invoices through the portal. Ensure that invoices are visible to customers in System > Setup > Defaults > Customer Portal > Enabled Pages.

To pay invoices through the customer portal, your customers can simply:

  1. Sign into the portal as usual.
  2. Locate the desired invoice(s) in Invoices > Unpaid or in Invoices > Statement.
  3. Click Options > Pay Online, or select multiple invoices and click Pay Online at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Enter their credit card details, including Name on Card, Expiry, Card Number and CVV.
  5. Click Pay.
  6. Click Continue to confirm the payment.
  7. If the payment is successful, a confirmation will appear, along with a receipt number. Click Close.

In your simPRO system, the payment will immediately apply to the invoice, and the invoice's status will be updated accordingly.

 Process a credit card payment over the phone

Once simPRO Payments is activated in your system, you can easily use simPRO Payments to process a credit card payment from a customer on the phone.

To process a credit card payment over the phone:

  1. Locate the desired invoice, through Invoices > Unpaid, or under Customers by clicking Options > View > Statement, or any of the other methods you usually use to apply payments.
  2. Click Options > Apply Payment, or if viewing a customer statement select the desired invoices and click Pay Invoice at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Select the simPRO Payments-associated credit card type from the Method drop-down list.
  4. Enter the customer's Name on Card, Expiry, Card Number and CVV details.
  5. Complete the rest of the payment details as required, including the Amount Applied.
  6. Click Process Payment.
  7. Click Continue to confirm the payment.
  8. If the payment is successful, a confirmation will appear, along with a receipt number. Click OK to close.

To instantly email the customer a receipt, click the Forms tab, select the Payment Release form, and send the email as usual.

You can also use this process to apply payments when a customer posts you the How To Pay section of their invoice with their handwritten credit card details.

 Process a credit card payment in the field

Once simPRO Payments is activated in your system, technicians can use simPRO Payments through Connect to collect a credit card payment on their mobile device before leaving a site. Ensure that Mobile Billing is enabled in System > Setup > Mobile > General > Billing Settings.

To process a credit card payment in the field using Connect:

  1. Create an invoice as usual.
  2. Tap Options, then tap Apply Payment in the drop-down list.
  3. Select the simPRO Payments-associated credit card type from the Payment Method drop-down list.
  4. Enter credit card details as required.
  5. Tap Make Payment.
  6. Tap Continue to confirm the payment.
  7. If the payment is successful, a confirmation will appear, along with a receipt number. Tap OK to close.
