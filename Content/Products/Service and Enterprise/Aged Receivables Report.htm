Aged Receivables Report

Overview

Use the Aged Receivables report to see customers with outstanding invoices and displays them in 30 day increments up to more than 91 days overdue. This report may be used by account departments to follow up on overdue invoices. The report includes recurring invoices.

View the Aged Receivables report

To view the report:

  1. Go to Reports > View Reports.
  2. Hover over Sales and click Aged Receivables.
  3. Select a date under Invoices Up To to view all invoices issued before that date.
  4. Filter the report by selecting from the drop-down list.
  5. Click Search.

Selected filters change what is displayed on screen but does not alter statements or how much a customer owes. Additionally, the statement is created at a point in time for all invoices up to the selected date and will show the entire amount owed.

 Print or email statement / overdue letter

You can print or email a statement, overdue letter or final overdue letter to give to customers. Go to System > Setup > Forms Setup > Statement / Overdue Letters > Display Options to enter a new form title to display on the printable form document. The form will still retain its simPRO default title when you select it from the Form drop-down list.

To email the statement / overdue letter to customers:

  1. Generate the report.
  2. Select the check boxes for each customer you would like to email.
  3. Under Print Options, select the form:
    • Statement – displays a table of invoices, dates and amounts owing. Pay information is displayed in the footer of the form.
    • Overdue Letter – a statement with an overdue notice.
    • Overdue Letter – Final - a statement with a final overdue notice.
  4. Click Email at the bottom of the page.

To print the statement / overdue letter:

  1. Select the check boxes for each result you would like to print.
  2. Select the form type under Print Options:
    • Statement – displays a table of invoices, dates and amounts owing. Pay information is displayed in the footer of the form.
    • Overdue Letter – a statement with an overdue notice.
    • Overdue Letter – Final - a statement with a final overdue notice.
  3. Click Print at the bottom of the page.

If you are printing fewer than 200 statements, a PDF will be generated for printing.

If you are printing more than 200 statements or overdue letters, you will be prompted to print in smaller batches of up to 200. Click a batch to generate a PDF.

 Download report

To download the report, click Download CSV.
