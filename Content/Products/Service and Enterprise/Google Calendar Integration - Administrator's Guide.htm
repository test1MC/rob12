Google Calendar Integration: Administrator's Guide

Install simPRO Calendar Integration

This process can only be done by your Google Apps for Work administrator.

To install the simPRO Calendar Integration app in Google:

  1. Log in to your Google account.
  2. Go to the simPRO Calendar Integration page in the Google Apps Marketplace by clicking here.
  3. Click INSTALL APP.
  4. Under Turn ON for, select the users or organisations to use this feature.
  5. Select the I agree to... check box if you agree to Google's Terms of Service.
  6. Click Accept.

  7. After the simPRO Calendar... has been installed! screen appears, close that browser tab or window.

Return to the main Google Calendar Integration article.

 Edit simPRO Calendar Integration settings

This process can only be done by your Google Apps for Work administrator.

To edit simPRO Calendar Integration app settings:

  1. Log in to your Google account.
  2. Click the Google Apps icon in the top right of the screen (the nine dots shaped into a square).
  3. Click the Admin icon.
  4. Go to Apps > Marketplace Apps > simPRO Calendar Integration.
  5. Click Settings and edit as desired.
 Uninstall simPRO Calendar Integration

This process can only be done by your Google Apps for Work administrator.

To uninstall the simPRO Calendar Integration app:

  1. Log in to your Google account.
  2. Click the Google Apps icon in the top right of the screen (the nine dots shaped into a square).
  3. Click the Admin icon.
  4. Go to Apps > Marketplace Apps > simPRO Calendar Integration.
  5. Click the Remove App icon (the rubbish bin) in the top right.
  6. Click REMOVE APP.
