Customer Groups (Enterprise only)

Overview

Use customer groups to categorise your customers by what type of customer they are, such as Domestic, Industrial, Commercial, etc. Each customer can only be assigned to one group. You can also filter customer groups for most reports.

Create a customer group

To create a customer group:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Customer Groups.
  2. Click Create Customer Group.
  3. Name the Customer Group. This will appear on the customer card file when assigned.
  4. Click Create.
 Assign a customer to a group

To assign a customer to a group:

  1. Go to People > Customers.
  2. Click the desired customer or create a new one.
  3. In the Profile tab, click the Settings sub-tab.
  4. Select from the Customer Profile drop-down list.
  5. Click Save or Finish to update your changes.
 Delete a customer group

To delete a customer group, click the red delete icon next to the group you would like to delete.

To delete multiple groups, select the check boxes next to the group you would like to delete and click Delete.
