Storage Devices

Overview

In simPRO, storage devices are physical locations where you store stock or inventory, such as a field technician's vehicle, warehouse, shed etc.

Use storage devices to organise and allocate stock, including monitoring stock levels, transferring stock to other storage devices and performing stock takes and adjustments. You can also assign a storage device to an employee / contractor.

Create a storage device

To create a storage device:

  1. Go to Materials > Stock.
  2. Click Create Storage Device.
  3. Enter a Storage Device Name.
  4. Select Default Device if this is your most commonly used storage device.
  5. The storage device will then be automatically selected when required in a workflow.
  6. Click Create.

To edit a storage device, go to Materials > Stock, then click the desired device or click Options > Edit.

 Delete a storage device

You cannot delete a storage device that has items in stock. Before deleting a storage device, you need to transfer any associated stock to another storage device, or alternatively, perform a stocktake to remove all items.

To delete a storage device:

  1. Go to Materials > Stock.
  2. Locate the desired storage device, then click Options.
  3. Click Delete > OK.
 Assign a storage device to an employee / contractor

Assign storage devices to employees / contractors to restrict their access to storage devices in Connect, as well as where they can assign and transfer stock as per their security group settings. You can assign one storage device to multiple people.

To assign a storage device to an employee / contractor:

  1. Access the desired employee / contractor card file.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings > Storage Device.
  3. Select the desired Storage Device from the drop-down list.
  4. Click Finish.
