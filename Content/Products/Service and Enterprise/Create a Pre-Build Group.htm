Create a Pre-Build Group

Create a pre-build group

Before creating a pre-build, you will need to create pre-build groups to organise your pre-builds and make them easier to locate.

To create a pre-build group:

  1. Go to Materials > Pre-Builds.
  2. Click Create Group.
  3. Enter a Group Name.
  4. Click Save.

After creating a group, you can also create subgroups by opening the group and clicking Create Subgroup.

Use the drag handles to arrange groups and subgroups as desired.

 Delete a pre-build group or subgroup

To delete a group or subgroup:

  1. Go to Materials > Pre-Builds.
  2. Navigate to the group or subgroup you wish to delete. Do not open it.
  3. On the desired group or subgroup, click Options > Delete Group / Subgroup.
  4. Click OK to confirm.

To delete multiple groups:

  1. Go to Materials > Pre-Builds.
  2. Navigate to the groups you wish to delete. Do not open them.
  3. Select the check boxes of the groups to delete, or click Select All at the bottom of the page.
  4. Click Delete at the bottom of the page.
  5. Click OK to confirm.

Deleting a pre-build group or subgroup will also delete or archive all the pre-builds it contains. Pre-builds will be archived if they have history.
