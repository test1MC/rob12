Settings

Overview

Use the settings page to manage your personal details and preferences, company details, and more. Click Settings in the top right corner, next to the log out button.

My Details

Click My Details to update your personal details, email address and password as desired.

Password Change

Change your password in the lower part of the My Details page.

It is recommended that you change your password as soon as you first log in.

Click Update to save changes.

 My Preferences

Click My Preferences to set preferences for vehicle marker size, messaging, and the length of alert sirens.

Click Update to save changes.

 Report Subscriptions

Add, edit and remove Report Subscriptions.

 Maintenance Planner

Use Maintenance Planner to manage service types, vehicle services and alerts.

 Manage Company Details

Click Manage Company Details to view device licences and update company timezone and email address.

Select the simPRO Integration check box and enter your simPRO Build URL to begin integrating simTRAC with simPRO.

Click Update to save changes.

 More

From the Settings page you can also Manage Users, Manage Vehicles, Manage Groups, Set up New Alerts, Manage Geofences and Sites, drivers, and tags.

If you cannot see some of these options on the settings page, it means you do not have access to them. Another user with access will have to change your user settings to allow you to view them.
