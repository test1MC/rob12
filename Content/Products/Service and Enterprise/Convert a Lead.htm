Convert a Lead

If a lead is successful and the customer wants a quote, you can convert a lead to a quote.

To convert a lead:

  1. Go to Leads > Open Leads.
  2. Click Options. Alternatively, right-click the desired lead.
  3. Click Convert to Quote.
  4. Click OK.
  5. Click the Quotes tab and find the newly created quote.
  6. Create the quote as normal.

Converted leads will be moved to Closed Leads.

The Description from the converted lead will carry over to the Summary of the newly created quote.
