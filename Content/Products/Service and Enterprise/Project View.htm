Project View

Overview

Go to Schedules > Project View to schedule employees to project jobs. Use the Project View tab to schedule one employee to multiple jobs at a time, or use the Employee View tab to schedule multiple employees to one job at a time.

When you schedule an employee in Project View, they will be scheduled for their entire shift that day and if you want to adjust the hours you can do so in Day View or Week View.

To use Project View, you will need to have your employees' available hours set up in their card files.

Schedule projects in Project View

Project View displays multiple project jobs in a list, so that you can easily share your resources among several projects.

Each project has two rows. The bottom row displays existing schedule blocks, and the top row is for creating new schedule blocks. Days that are shaded grey indicate that the resource is unavailable.

To schedule employees to multiple jobs in Project View:

  1. Go to Schedules > Project View.
  2. Select the month by clicking the blue arrows above the schedule.
  3. Select the project Stage to view projects currently in that stage.
  4. Filter the projects as desired, by selecting a Customer, Site, Zone, Cost Centre, or assigned Employee. Click the field to view the drop-down list.
  5. Under View No. Jobs, enter the number of jobs you would like to display, then click outside of the field to filter the projects.
  6. Select whether you want to schedule Individual Resources or Team Resources.
  7. Select an individual employee, contractor, plant item or team from the Select Resources drop-down.
  8. Click Expand next to a project to view existing schedule blocks.
  9. Click the Select Cost Centre field under the job number to select a cost centre.
  10. Click and drag on the top row next to the job number to create a schedule block.

To schedule employees to a single job in Project View:

  1. Go to Schedules > Project View.
  2. Select the month by clicking the blue arrows above the schedule.
  3. Search and select the desired Job No.
  4. Select whether you want to schedule Individual Resources or Team Resources.
  5. Select an individual employee, contractor, plant item or team from the Select Resources drop-down.
  6. Click Expand next to a project to view existing schedule blocks.
  7. Click the Select Cost Centre field under the job number to select a cost centre.
  8. Click and drag on the top row next to the job number to create a schedule block.
 Schedule in Employee View

Employee View is similar to Day View. Resources are listed on the left and you can search for a specific job, quote or activity to schedule using the search bar.

If you click the Employee View tab after scheduling in Project View, only the selected resource will be listed. To view all resources, click Filter and select All Available Employees from the Employee drop-down.

To schedule in Employee View:

  1. Go to Schedules > Project View > Employee View.
  2. Select Job, Quote or Activity from the drop-down next to the search bar.
  3. Search and select the desired job / quote / activity. If desired, click Advanced to filter your search.
    • If scheduling a job with multiple cost centres, select the cost centre from the drop-down below the search bar.
  4. If desired, filter employees by selecting a Cost Centre, Zone or Team, or even select an individual Employee.
  5. Select the month by clicking the blue arrows above the schedule.
  6. Click and drag on the schedule next to the desired resource to create a schedule block.
