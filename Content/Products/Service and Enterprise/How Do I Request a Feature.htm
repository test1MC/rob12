How Do I Request a Feature?

Overview

If you have an idea to improve how you use simPRO, you can submit an idea in the Ideas Portal for the simPRO team to review and consider for future releases, as well as for other simPRO users to view, vote for, and comment on.

The simPRO team considers several factors when selecting features for future release updates, most importantly its value to the product, the user, and their business.

Before you submit an idea, check the release notes and the product roadmaps for Enterprise & Service, simTRAC, and Connect to see if your idea is already on its way.

Access the Ideas Portal

To view the Ideas Portal, access your simPRO Service or Enterprise build as normal, then go to Help > Submit an Idea.

You need to supply an email address to access the Ideas Forum so that you can idea and keep up to date with their progress. If you do not have one entered in your simPRO employee card file, you will be prompted to enter one before you access the Ideas Portal. Enter the desired email address, then click Confirm.

When you have accessed the Ideas Portal, click the tabs to view the most Recent, Trending, and Popular ideas. You can also click My ideas / My votes to view the ideas you have created / voted for, or perform a search to locate a particular idea.

Use the Filter By Status options to filter ideas according to the current status of the idea as defined by the simPRO team:

Likely to implement

The feature will likely be implemented into simPRO depending on demand by customers.

Will not implement

The feature will not be implemented into simPRO due to reasons such as technical restraints, industry practices, not fitting with the simPRO vision for the product, etc.

Already exists

The feature already exists in simPRO.

Planned

The feature is currently planned and ready for approval by the simPRO Product Manager. Once approved, the feature will be estimated by the development team and added to the development backlog for release in a future update.

In Next Release

The feature will be released in the next update.

Shipped

The feature has been developed, implemented, and released.

You can also view ideas according to product categories, including Connect, eForms, Integrations with the software, simPRO, and simTRAC.

You can only access the Ideas Portal through your simPRO Service or simPRO Enterprise build, but you can still post ideas for Connect, eForms, simTRAC, or integrations with simPRO.

Submit an idea for simPRO

To submit an idea:

  1. Access your simPRO build.
  2. Go to Help > Submit an Idea.
  3. Click Add a new idea.
  4. Select the applicable simPRO product.
  5. Enter Your idea in a short summary.
  6. Enter more details and attachments in the text box below as desired.
  7. Select the applicable category for your idea.
  8. For example, if your idea was related to handling customers in simPRO, you would select People.
  9. Click Share Idea.

Your idea will then be created in the Ideas Portal for others to view, vote for, and comment on.

To edit your idea, go to the Ideas Portal, click My ideas, then click the applicable idea and click Edit Idea. When you have finished making changes, click Save.

Vote for and comment on an idea

If there is an idea in the Ideas Portal that you would also like to have implemented in simPRO, you can vote for it to show your support.

To vote on an idea, access the Ideas Portal as normal, click the tabs to view the most Recent, Trending, or Popular ideas, then click Vote to the left of the desired idea.

You can also add a comment to an idea if you would like to add input or ask for clarification. To add a comment, locate the desired idea, then click the speech bubble icon to the bottom right. Then, enter your comment and any attachments as desired, and click Post Comment.

This will then send an email notification to anyone who has subscribed to the idea.

Subscribe to an idea

Subscribe to ideas to receive email notifications when the idea has been updated. To subscribe to an idea, access the Ideas Portal as normal, click the desired idea, then click Subscribe.

To unsubscribe from an idea and stop receiving email notifications, click Unsubscribe.
