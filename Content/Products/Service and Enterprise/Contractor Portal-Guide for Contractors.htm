Contractor Portal: Guide for Contractors

Overview

Access the contractor portal to view and update jobs and quotes created in simPRO. You will only see work orders and invoices for your contractor company.

Log in to the contractor portal

To log in, you will need your login details, including a URL (web address), username, and password as provided by your simPRO user.

To log in to the contractor portal:

  1. Enter the URL in your web browser.
  2. Enter your Username and Password.
  3. Click Login.

You will then be taken to the Pending Jobs page in the portal, to the Pending Jobs page.

You can access the main menu options across the top of the screen and your company and name will appear in the top right corner next to the log out icon.

 Change profile details

In the contractor portal you can edit staff member profiles, including contact details.

To change profile details:

  1. Go to Staff, then click the desired staff member.
  2. Click the desired staff member.
  3. Adjust details as desired.
  4. Click Finish.
 Create a new staff member

You can add staff members so that they can be scheduled and receive work orders. Once you have submitted a staff member, a simPRO user will need to set up their username and password before they can log into the portal.

To create a new staff member:

  1. Go to Staff, then click Create New.
  2. Enter the Name, Position, and Street Address of the staff member.
  3. Select the Date of Commencement and Date of Birth if desired.
  4. Enter Primary Contact details.
  5. Go to Custom to enter additional information about the individual according to custom fields.
    • The Custom tab will only appear if custom fields have been set up by the simPRO user.
  6. Go to Licences to enter any relevant licences that the individual holds.
    • Enter the Licence Name, Licence Reference, and / or Licence Expiry Date, then click Save. You can then select the Licence Name when editing or creating other staff members.
    • When a staff member's Licence Expiry Date is approaching, the simPRO user will receive an alert.
  7. Go to Emergency to enter emergency contact details for the staff member.
  8. Click Finish > View Staff.
    • Alternatively, click Add New to continue creating staff members.
 Schedule staff

To schedule staff to jobs:

  1. Go to Schedule.
  2. Next to the search bar, select Quote / Job.
  3. Search and select the desired quote / job.
  4. Under the Job No. select the applicable cost centre (if required) and the schedule rate.
  5. Click the blue arrows to change the month as desired, then click the desired date.
  6. On the same line as the contractor you wish to schedule, click to add a schedule block to the desired time.
  7. Use the drag icon to drag the schedule block to another time, or hover over the block, then drag the white arrows to extend or shorten it.

Consult the Legend below the schedule to determine job status by colour.

Once a schedule block has been placed, right-click it to view the following options:

Cut Schedule

Cut this schedule block so that it can be pasted to another staff member. Click and drag on the schedule to paste the block.

Copy Schedule

Make a copy of this schedule that can be pasted to another staff member.

Remove Block

Remove this schedule block.

Remove Schedule

Remove all blocks for this schedule.

Jobcard

View or Print the job card for this schedule.
Mark a job as complete

Once the work for a job has been completed, you can mark the job as complete.

To mark a pending job as complete, go to Jobs > Pending, then click Options > Mark as Complete on the applicable job. The job will now appear under Jobs > Complete.

Update the job card

After you have completed the job, you can record the actual work completed in the job card.

To update a job card:

  1. Locate the desired job in Jobs > Pending / Complete.
  2. Click Options > View Job Card on the desired job.
  3. Click the Actual tab.
  4. Enter the Work Completed.
  5. Select the Date Performed.
    • ​​Click Add Block to add schedule blocks if applicable, then select a Start and Finish time and the Schedule Rate.
  6. Enter Material Notes.
  7. Under Materials Available, select a Storage Device, then search and select the materials you wish to assign.
  8. Enter the Quantity, click Assign Materials.
  9. Click the Attachments tab to upload any relevant attachments.
  10. Click Finish.
 Approve and reject RCTIs

View and approve or reject recipient-created tax invoices (RCTIs) in the contractor portal as created by the simPRO user.

To approve or reject RCTIs:

  1. Go to Invoices > RCTIs.
  2. Review the Amount column for the desired RCTI.
  3. Click Options > Approve RCTI Invoice / Reject RCTI Invoice as desired.
    • If you have rejected the RCTI, enter a Reject Reason, then click Reject.

When the RCTI is approved or rejected, it will be removed from the Recipient Created Tax Invoice list.
