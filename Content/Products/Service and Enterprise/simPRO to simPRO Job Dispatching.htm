simPRO to simPRO Job Dispatching

Overview

If one of your contractors is also using simPRO to manage their own business, you can create and dispatch a work order in simPRO that automatically creates a new job in the contractor's own simPRO build. Both parties will need to set this up in their simPRO builds using the steps below.

Set up steps for the contractor

If you are the contractor to receive dispatched jobs, follow this process. This must be done before the job creator can complete setup in their own simPRO build.

Check or adjust the job creator's customer card file in your simPRO build:

  1. Under People > Customers, create a customer card file for the job creator, or open their existing customer card file.
  2. Go to the Settings sub-tab.
  3. Ensure the customer has a Username and Password, or enter new ones. You will need to send the job creator these details.
  4. Select the Allow customer to send jobs to your system check box.
  5. Click Finish.

Finally, send the job creator:

  • the username and password from their customer card file.
  • your own simPRO build address, such as brisbanelabour.simprosuite.com.

Ensure the job creator then sets up dispatching in their own simPRO build.

 Set up steps for the job creator

If you are the business dispatching jobs to a contractor, follow this process.

First, ensure the contractor sets up dispatching in their own simPRO build.

To complete this process you will need:

  • the contractor's simPRO build address, such as brisbanelabour.simprosuite.com.
  • the username and password the contractor entered in your customer card file in their simPRO build.

Adjust the contractor's card file in your simPRO build:

  1. Under People > Contractors, create a contractor card file for the contractor, or open their existing contractor card file.
  2. Go to the Settings sub-tab.
  3. Under Contractor's simPRO, select the Allow jobs to be sent to contractor check box.
  4. Enter the contractor's simPRO Address, and use the simPRO Username and simPRO Password you received from the contractor.
  5. Click Finish.
 Dispatch a job to a contractor's simPRO

After completing setup, you can dispatch jobs to a contractor that will automatically create a new job in the contractor's simPRO build.

To dispatch a job directly to a contractor's simPRO build:

  1. Schedule the job to the contractor, or create a new work order from the Contractor tab within the job.
  2. Open the new work order through Jobs > Contractor Jobs.
  3. Click Dispatch at the top of the screen.
  4. Click OK.

A dialogue box will appear to confirm the work order has successfully been dispatched.

In the contractor's simPRO build, a new Pending job will automatically be created.
