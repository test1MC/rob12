Rated People Integration

Overview

Once you have integrated simPRO with Rated People, you will be able to retrieve your Purchased Leads from the Rated People website to be created as quotes in simPRO. New customers and sites will also be created in simPRO when necessary.

All customers from Rated People will currently be created as individuals in simPRO.

Set up simPRO with Rated People

To set up simPRO to integrate with Rated People:

  1. Go to System > Setup, then click the Rated People Integration tab.
  2. Under Rated People, select On.
  3. Enter your Rated People User ID, Rated People Username and Rated People Password.
  4. Select the Default Cost Centre.
    • When simPRO creates quotes for Rated People leads, they will automatically be assigned to this cost centre.
  5. Click Save.

Retrieve purchased leads

To retrieve leads purchased from Rated People:

  1. Go to Utilities > RP Integration.
  2. Click Get Available Leads in the top right.

All available leads will be retrieved and created as quotes in simPRO, as well as any relevant customers and sites. You can view a summary of all new data created in simPRO in the Transaction History below.

View Rated People information in quotes

When a purchased lead becomes a quote in simPRO, the following information is created in simPRO:

simPRO field Rated People field Clarification
Customer / Site Customer If the customer does not exist in simPRO, a new customer and site will be created.
Request No. Job Reference The reference is generated by Rated People.
Description
  • Specialisation
  • Tradesperson's skill
  • Lead Title
  • Lead Description
  • Job Timing
  • Hiring Position
 Multiple fields from Rated People will populate the Description field.
Notes
  • Home Ownership Status
  • Job Budget
  • Purchasing position of job
 Multiple fields from Rated People will populated the Notes field.
Created Date Submission Date This is the date that the lead was submitted to the Rated People site.
Due Date Expiry Date The expiry date is generated by Rated People.
Quote Stage N / A This is automatically set to In Progress.
Tags N / A All quotes created from leads will be identified by the Rated People Lead tag. You can filter this tag when reporting on quotes.
Quote Type N / A All quotes created from leads will be service quotes.
Cost Centre N / A The default cost centre, as defined in System > Setup > Rated People Integration, will be added to the quote.
One Off Item N / A A non-billable one off item will be created in the cost centre showing the purchased lead price.
