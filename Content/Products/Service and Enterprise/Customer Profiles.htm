Customer Profiles

Overview

Create customer profiles and assign them to customers so that you can easily identify the quality or maturity of your relationship with the customer. By giving customers a profile, such as Gold, Silver or Bronze, you can better manage the level of commitment or financial risk when doing business with your customers. Each customer can only have one customer profile. You can also filter Customer Groups for most reports.

Create a customer profile

To create a customer profile:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Customer Profile.
  2. Click Create Customer Profile.
  3. Name the customer profile. This will appear on the customer card file when assigned.
  4. Click Create.
 Assign a profile to a customer

To assign a profile to a customer:

  1. Go to People > Customers.
  2. Click the desired customer or create a new one.
  3. In the Profile tab, click the Settings sub-tab.
  4. Select from the Customer Profile drop-down list.
 Delete a customer profile

To delete a customer profile, click the red delete icon next to the profile you would like to delete.

To delete multiple customer profiles, select the check boxes next to the profiles you would like to delete and click Delete.
