Financial Defaults

Overview

Go to System > Setup > Defaults > Financial to set up Accounts and Invoicing details shared between simPRO and your accounting package. Be sure to click Save after making any changes.

These account numbers or names all relate to the codes in your integrated accounting package's chart of accounts.

Set up account defaults

Enter default accounts to record financial transactions, such as income, deposit and expenses. The Accounts options available are:

Finance Charge Label Rename the Finance Charge field. The new name will be used throughout the system and on forms.
Income Account Default income account, overridden by cost centres to break up income.
Deposit Account Default deposit account, overridden by payment methods to break up payments and to post to the correct account in your accounting package.
Expense Account Default expense account, overridden by cost centres to break up expenses for material purchases. This default account is also used for stock purchases that are not inventory tracked.
Retention Asset Account An asset account for retention being withheld by your customers for your completed works, until claimed.
Retention Liability Account A liability account for retention you are withholding from contractors invoicing you for works they have completed.

Finance Charge Account Account for fees associated with using various credit cards. This field can be renamed as desired.
Freight Account Account for tracking transactions when sites charge you for freight and you record it in the Freight field, rather than as a catalogue item on the order.
Restocking Fee Account Account for site credits when they charge a restocking fee.
(UK only) CIS Tax Suffered Account Account for CIS deductions on sales invoices.
(UK only) CIS Liability Account Account for CIS deductions on contractor invoices.
CIS Tax Suffered Account Account for CIS deductions on sales invoices.

Start of Financial Year Select the desired start date for the financial year.

Set up invoicing defaults

Before you invoice and collect payments, set up your invoicing defaults such as payment terms and a late payment fee. The Invoicing options available are:

Show Sell and Cost Prices Display sell and cost prices inclusive or exclusive of tax.
Payment Terms A default payment term for customers. This will apply to all new customers. Select Update all Customers to apply it to all existing customers.
Late Payment Fee The percentage of an invoice to increment each month when customers have not met your payment terms.
Tracking Helps classify transactions for use in your accounting package. Based on categories (selected at each transaction) or business groups (integrated with cost centres).
Multiple Currencies Useful if you have customers or sites who use a currency different to your default currency. Set up currencies under System > Setup > System Setup > Currencies.
Retentions Hold retentions inclusive or exclusive of tax.
