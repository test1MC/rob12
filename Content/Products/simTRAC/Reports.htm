Reports

Overview

Generate and view reports in the Reports tab. You can also send regular reports with Report Subscriptions.

Run reports

To generate a report:

  1. Click the Reports tab.
  2. Select the type of report.
  3. Select the start date and end date.
  4. Select which vehicles or groups of vehicles you would like to report on.
  5. Different types of reports may have additional details you can select.
  6. Click Launch Report.

If desired, you can email a report or export it as a CSV or PDF file.

 Report History

View a list of all previous reports in the Report History tab. You can also see whether a report was generated in the Reports tab or by a report subscription.

Use the search bar to find a specific report.

To open a report, click the folder icon.

To download a report, click the download icon.

To remove a report from report history, click the delete icon.
