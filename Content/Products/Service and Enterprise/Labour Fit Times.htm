Labour Fit Times

Overview

Create fit time multipliers to assist in estimating the amount of additional labour required for a catalogue or pre-build item added to a job.

For example, you can assign a labour fit time multiplier to a job if you need extend the default fit time for a catalogue item or pre-build due to circumstances such as small ceiling space, or concrete walls instead of plasterboard walls.

Create a labour fit time

To create a labour fit time:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Labour Fit Times.
  2. Click Create Fit Time.
  3. Enter a Fit Time Name.
  4. Enter the desired Fit Time Multiplier.
    • This will multiply the amount of Time billed for the item it is assigned to.
  5. Click Create.

Use the drag handles to arrange labour fit times as desired.

 Edit a labour fit time

To edit a labour fit time:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Labour Fit Times.
  2. Click the labour fit time you wish to edit.
  3. Make changes as desired.
  4. Click Save.
 Delete a labour fit time

To delete a single labour fit time:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Labour Fit Times.
  2. On the labour fit time you wish to delete, click the red delete icon.
  3. Click OK.

To delete multiple labour fit times at once, select the labour fit times you wish to delete, or select Select All, then click Delete > OK.

 Assign a labour fit time to a job or quote

To assign a labour fit time to a job or quote:

  1. Create a job as normal or edit an existing job.
  2. Click Parts & Labour.
  3. Add a catalogue item or pre-build as normal.
  4. Click Fit Time in the Time column of the item / pre-build.
  5. Select the desired Fit Time.
    • The estimated time of the job will automatically update.
