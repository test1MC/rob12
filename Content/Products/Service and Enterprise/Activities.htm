Activities

Overview

You can use activities to schedule and keep track of non-billable time, such as annual leave, training, and toolbox talks.

Once an activity is created, you can schedule it the same way you schedule quotes and jobs in simPRO, using the schedule day, week, or manual view, as well as in Connect.

If the technician being scheduled for an activity is enabled for Mobility, they will automatically receive a notification in Connect when an activity is created or updated. Alternatively, you can manually send a notification containing a message relevant to the activity.

Create an activity

To create an activity:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup.
  2. In the left column, click Activities.
  3. Click Create Activity.
  4. Enter an Activity Name.
  5. If applicable, select Associate Labour Cost.
  6. Select an existing Schedule Rate.
  7. Click Create.
 Edit an activity

To edit an activity:

  1. Go to System > System Setup > Activities.
  2. Click the activity you wish to edit and make changes as desired.
  3. Click Save.
 Delete an activity

To delete a single activity:

  1. Go to System > System Setup > Activities.
  2. Click the red delete icon on the activity you wish to delete.
  3. Click OK.

To delete multiple activities at once:

  1. Go to System > System Setup > Activities.
  2. Select the activities you want to delete, or select Select All.
  3. Click Delete.
  4. Click OK.

 Schedule an activity Schedule an activity in day or week view

Use Day View and Week View to schedule activities using visual time blocks.

You can schedule activities for a specific technician while viewing their entire schedule for 24 hours or for a whole week in Day View and Week View respectively, as well as schedules of other technicians.

Scheduled activities are colour-coded as pink. Use the Legend at the bottom of the page for quick colour code reference.

In Day View, each technician has a grey box next to their name. The number next to the clock icon displays a technician's available hours on a standard work day. The spanner icon displays how many jobs they have scheduled.

When you schedule an activity for a Mobility-enabled technician, an automatic notification will be sent to their Connect device 30 seconds after the activity has been scheduled. Alternatively, you can manually send notifications to Connect that include a message relevant to the activity.

To schedule an activity in Day View or Week View:

  1. Go to Schedule > Day View / Week View.
  2. Under Schedule, select Activity from the drop-down list.
  3. Click Search Activities and select an activity from the drop-down list.​
    • Alternatively, click Search Activities or Search to view all activities, or alternatively, enter the activity, then click Search.
      • Narrow your search results using the Cost Centre, Zone, Employee and Stage filters as desired, then locate and click the desired activity.
  4. Click the displayed month to select a schedule date from the calendar.
    • Alternatively, if in Day View, click the single blue arrows to view the desired month, then click the grey dates to view the desired schedule.
    • If in Week View, click the single blue arrows to move the displayed week forward or back by a day. Click the double blue arrows to move the displayed week forward or back by a week.
  5. Click Filter to view options to narrow the schedule to a particular Employee, Cost Centre, Zone or Team.
  6. Click the time block you wish to schedule the activity in.
    • Use the white drag icon to drag the activity to a different time block or employee schedule.
    • Use the white arrows to extend or shorten the length of the time block.
  7. Click Finish.

​To schedule the same activity for a different technician, simply right-click the activity, click Copy Schedule, then right-click the schedule of the desired technician and click Paste Schedule.

To remove a scheduled activity, right-click the activity, then click Remove Block.

 Schedule a recurring activity

To schedule a recurring activity:

  1. Schedule an activity as normal.
  2. Right-click on the activity, then click Recur Schedule.
  3. Select from the When and Every drop-down lists to choose how often you want this activity to be scheduled.
  4. Under Until, select the date for which you wish the schedule to stop recurring.
  5. Click Save.

To remove all recurring activities scheduled from a certain day onwards, right-click the activity, click Remove Recurring, then click OK to confirm. Removing the original scheduled activity will remove all recurring activities.

 Schedule an activity manually

Use Manual View to schedule activities for a single technician using a timesheet process instead of the visual format used in Day View and Week View.

To schedule an activity manually:

  1. Go to Schedule > Manual View.
  2. Select the employee for whom you wish to schedule an activity from the drop-down list in the top left corner. If the employee has any existing jobs, quotes or activities, these will appear at the top of the table.
  3. Under Type, select Activity from the drop-down list.
  4. Under Reference, search for the desired activity or simply select from the drop-down list.
  5. Enter the Date, as well as Start and Finish times of the activity.
  6. Select a Rate if applicable.
  7. Click Save or Finish.

To delete an activity in Manual View, select the check box next to the desired activity, then click Delete.

You cannot send schedule notifications in Manual View.
