Connect Overview

Overview

simPRO Connect is an add-on product of simPRO in the form of a mobile application for field employees to use on a smart phone or tablet when working in the field.

Effectively an electronic runsheet and job card, Connect enables field employees to:

  • View job, quote, activity and, in simPRO Enterprise, task details.
  • Schedule activities
  • Create new jobs and quotes, as well as new customers, sites, and, in simPRO Enterprise, leads. See Connect Jobs and Connect Quotes for more information.
    • Update times, pricing and work notes, and for jobs and quotes.
    • Complete pre-audits and post-audits for jobs and quotes.
    • Photograph jobs, documents, and, in simPRO Enterprise, assets and attach these to a job or quote.
  • View, create and receipt job purchase orders and, in simPRO Enterprise, Create stock purchase orders.
  • With simPRO Enterprise, Manage customer assets.
  • Sign off on a job
  • Submit and convert a quote.
  • Invoice a job and Email a job invoice.
  • Apply a payment to a job

You can reflect any activity or changes made in Connect in your simPRO system when you Sync Connect with simPRO

Compatible devices

The current version of simPRO Connect is designed to work on the following mobile phone devices:​

  • iPhone 5, iOS 7.1
  • iPhone 6, iOS 9.2.1
  • Samsung Galaxy Note, Android 4.1.2
  • Samsung Galaxy Note 3, Android 4.4.2
  • Samsung Galaxy S3, Android 4.3
  • Samsung Galaxy S4, Android 4.3
  • Samsung Galaxy S5, Android 5.0
  • Samsung Note GT-N7000B, Android 4.1.2

simPRO Connect is also designed to work on the following tablet devices:

  • iPad Air, iOS 7.1
  • iPad Fourth Generation, iOS 7.1
  • iPad Mini, iOS 8.1
  • Nexus 7 (second generation), Android 4.4.2
  • Samsung Galaxy Tab 3, Android 5.0
  • Samsung Note GT-N8020, Android 4.1.2

Other operating systems and devices

Other devices not yet tested by simPRO may also be compatible with simPRO Connect. If you choose to use Connect on a different device, it is recommended that you test Connect functionalities on the device thoroughly. You can notify simPRO Support if you are successfully operating Connect on a device that is not listed here.

Please note that newer or older versions of the same device or operating systems may not be compatible with Connect, as there may have been significant changes between version releases. If a device or operating system works with an earlier version of the app, it may not work with a newer version of the same app.

Providers may delay the release of new OS versions on their network for quality assurance purposes. If the desired OS version is not pre-installed on the device, check with the provider to ensure it is available for download on their network.
