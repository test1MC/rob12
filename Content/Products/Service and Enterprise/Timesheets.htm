Timesheets

Overview

Use timesheets to reconcile your schedule-based timesheets into an output appropriate for payroll purposes.

Generate a timesheet

To generate a timesheet:

  1. Go to Schedules > Timesheets.
  2. Select a Date Range.
  3. Under Display Format, select:
    • Visual to display all relevant schedules in Week View format for each employee.
    • Summary to display the number of hours for each schedule rate for each employee, as well as the labour cost for each employee. This can be downloaded as a CSV file.
  4. Select the Schedule Type.
  5. Select to show Leads, Quotes, Jobs and / or Activities as desired.
  6. Select to show Leads, Quotes, Jobs and / or Activities as desired.
  7. Select to show Quotes, Jobs and / or Activities as desired.
  8. If you have selected:
    • Visual: select Employees and Contractors from the drop-down list to view their relevant schedules.
    • Summary: select whether you wish to include only Contractors, only Employees or All.
    • Visual: select Employees and Contractors from the drop-down list to view their relevant schedules.
    • Summary: select whether you wish to include only Contractors, only Employees or All.
  9. If you have selected: Visual, select employees from the drop-down list to view their relevant schedules.
  10. Click Submit.

The timesheet will display below. To print the timesheet, click Print.

 Download timesheets for MYOB Payroll

If you use MYOB as an accounting package, you can download timesheets from simPRO to import into MYOB after you have set up your employees and schedule rates correctly.

To export a timesheet to MYOB:

  1. Generate a timesheet as normal.
  2. Select Summary as the Display Format.
  3. Click Submit.
  4. Click the download icon on the far right above the table.
  5. Click MYOB Payroll.

This will give you a CSV file that can be imported into MYOB.
