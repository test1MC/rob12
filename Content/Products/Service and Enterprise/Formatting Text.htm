Formatting Text

Overview

Use simPRO's text formatting features to customise how text displays on screen, in forms, and in Connect. These formatting options are available in simPRO Enterprise and Service only, for text in a range of fields including Description, Notes and Email Templates.

You can make text bold, italic, underlined or with a strikethrough; undo and redo formatting alterations; print the entered text; and change text and background colour.

Use these keyboard shortcuts are available for more efficient formatting:

CTRL + B

Bold

CTRL + I

Italics

CTRL + U

Underline

CTRL + Z

Undo

CTRL + Y

Redo
View formatted text in Connect

You can view formatted text in Connect in the Description and Notes fields.

To view a Job Description, go to the Runsheet and tap the desired job. On the Details page, tap Works to be Performed.

To view Job Notes, tap Notes > Overview.
