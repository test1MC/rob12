Connect Quotes

Overview

Use Connect to view, create, update, and email quotes in the field. Once a quote is completed and approved by the customer, you can then convert the quote to a job in Connect.

Create a quote in Connect

Create a quote in the field and generate quote details by selecting from customers, sites, cost centres and service fees created in simPRO. You can also create a new customer, site, or, in simPRO Enterprise, lead.

Once you have created a quote, customer, site, or lead, you can then Sync Connect with simPRO so you can manage and update quote information as normal in your simPRO system.

To create a quote in Connect:

  1. Go to the dashboard, then, if you are working with simPRO Service, tap Quote. If you are working with simPRO Enterprise, tap Project.
  2. If you are working with simPRO Enterprise and operating in a multi-company build, select the desired Company.
  3. Under Type, tap Quote.
  4. Select a Customer by tapping Select customer, then entering and tapping the desired customer name from the drop-down list.
    • If you are working with simPRO Enterprise, use the filter options to view Customers, Leads, or All.
    • Alternatively, you can Create a customer or lead in a quote.
  5. Tap Select site to select the desired site from the drop-down list.
    • Use the filter options to view Customer sites or All sites.
    • Alternatively, you can create a New Site. Enter the Site Name and address details, then tap Finished.
  6. Select a Cost Centre.​
  7. Assign a Technician.
    • ​​Only field technicians with the same cost centre and / or zone applied to their card file in simPRO will be available to select. The current Connect user will be assigned by default.
  8. Enter the Due Date for the quote to be completed.
  9. Select a Salesperson if desired.
  10. Apply a Service Fee if desired.
  11. Enter an Order No, Request No, and Description of the Quote or Cost Centre if desired.
  12. Tap Finished.

You will then be taken to the Details page to begin your quote workflow. To advance through the workflow, use the jump menu in the top left or the navigation arrows at the bottom of the page.

To return to the dashboard, tap the Connect logo in the top left.

To create quotes in Connect, you need to enable your project creation user permissions enabled in your General settings in simPRO and your device needs to be connected to the internet.

To create a new customer or, when working with simPRO Enterprise, lead in a quote:

  1. Create a quote in Connect as normal.
  2. Tap New Customer.
  3. Tap Individual / Company to define the customer type.
  4. Enter the First Name and Last Name / Company Name.
  5. If you are working with simPRO Enterprise, select the Customer Type.
  6. Enter the customer's Individual Phone / Company Phone number, Email, and address details.
  7. Tap Finished.

 View quote details

When you access a quote in Connect, you will be taken to the Details page. As the first page of the workflow, the Details page is the first page where you can Adjust the mobile status of a quote. You can also View schedule and site details, and View descriptions, materials and notes.

Adjust the mobile status of a quote

At the top of every page in the Connect workflow is the status bar. This displays the customer, quote number and the status of the quote. If you have not yet started work on a quote, the Start Travel and Start Work status options will appear. These options create timestamps that record actual times spent working on a quote.

Tap Start Travel to update the mobile quote status to Travelling. This will activate geostamping of the status location if you have enabled it in your Geostamping settings, as well as record the timestamp. If you have set up automatic triggers, the status of the quote will update in simPRO accordingly. Alternatively, if you wish to make travel time non-billable, you can Schedule activities.

Tap Start Work to create a timestamp to begin the quote. This will again activate geostamping of the status location and, if enabled, update the quote status in simPRO accordingly. The status options will then disappear as the quote is in progress.

View schedule and site details

View the focus date and scheduled time for the quote below the status bar. Tap the focus date to change it as desired, then tap Finished. The site contact and address information are below the focus date.

Tap the phone icon to view contact information for the Site, Quote Customer, or Project Manager if you are working with simPRO Enterprise. If you are using Connect on a device that can place phone calls and has an active connection to the mobile network, tap the desired phone number to initiate a phone call with the relevant contact.

If you are using a device that cannot make phone calls, tap the desired phone number, then tap:

Send Message Send the desired contact a message using iMessage. To set up iMessage for your device, go to Settings > Messages.
Add to Contacts Add the number to an existing contact or, alternatively, create a new contact in your device's contact list.
Copy Copy the number.

Tap the pin icon to locate the site in your device's default mapping solution.

View descriptions, materials and notes

Tap Work To Be Performed to view the Quote Description, the relevant cost centre and the Parts List, containing the quantities of all billlable and non-billable items required for the quote. The currency icon indicates that an item is billable.

Tap Materials Required to view material items applied to the quote in simPRO as well as their estimated quantities.

Tap Notes > Overview to view private notes entered in simPRO regarding the quote, customer, or site. Tap Other Information to view order numbers or request numbers if applicable.

 View quote pricing

Add billable materials to a quote, Add a labour rate, and Edit final pricing in the Pricing page. You can also enter or adjust the quote Description.

If the customer has approved and signed off the quote, any changes to the Pricing page will delete the signature, regardless of whether the price of the quote has been altered. See Adjust the quote after signing off for more information.

You need to set up automatic job card approval for Connect to adjust quote pricing and your device needs to be connected to the internet.

Add billable materials to a quote

You can Add items from a catalogue / pre-build / stock as well as Add service fees to a quote. Tap the pencil icon on an item to edit the quantity and unit price, or delete the item.

Add items from a catalogue / pre-build / stock

To add items from your catalogue / pre-builds / stock to a quote:

  1. Navigate to the Pricing page of the desired quote using the jump menu or navigation arrows.
  2. Tap Add Material > Catalogue / Pre-Builds / Stock Items.
  3. Tap Filter > Select Group / Select Storage to narrow your search according to a specific catalogue group / pre-build group / storage device if desired.
    • Tap Select Sub-Group to narrow according to a specific catalogue / pre-build sub-group.
  4. Enter the name or part number of the desired item in Search Catalogue / Prebuilds / Stock, then tap Search.
    • Alternatively, tap Search to view all catalogue / pre-build / stock items.
  5. Enter the desired Qty for the relevant items, then tap Add Parts.
    • ​​Alternatively, tap the add icon as desired, then tap Add Parts.
  6. Tap Finished.

Add service fees

To add a service fee to a quote:

  1. Navigate to the Pricing page of the desired quote using the jump menu or navigation arrows.
  2. Tap Add Material > Service Fees.
  3. Under the relevant cost centre, select the service fee you wish to add to the quote.
  4. Tap Finished.

Add a labour rate

To add a labour rate to a quote in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Pricing page of the desired quote using the jump menu or navigation arrows.
  2. Tap Add Labour.
  3. Select the labour rate you wish to apply to the quote.
  4. Tap Add.

Tap the pencil icon to edit the time and sell price of a labour rate, or delete the labour rate.

Edit final pricing

**screenshot

You can edit the final pricing of a quote in the Pricing page in Connect, as well as update the simPRO-defined tax code and apply a discount or additional fee.

To edit the final price of a quote in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Pricing page of the desired quote using the jump menu or navigation arrows.
  2. In the Quote Totals table, tap Edit.
  3. Enter the desired final Price.
  4. Select the desired Tax Code.
    • Select Inc. Tax to include tax in the final price, or clear the option to exclude tax from the final price.
  5. Under Direction, select:
    • Down (Discount) to apply a discount to the final price if desired.
    • Up (Fee) to apply an additional fee to the final price if desired.
  6. Enter the Percentage or Amount of the Discount / Fee if applicable.
  7. Tap Finished.

If you are using Connect with simPRO Enterprise and a quote has multiple cost centres, you can only edit the final pricing of the cost centre sub-total. You cannot edit the Quote Totals.

 Complete pre-audits for quotes

Complete pre-audits in Connect to assess safety conditions, work methods and equipment quality before starting work on a quote.

simPRO audits are arranged in three types: Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS), Job Safety Analysis (JSA) and Quality Assurance (QA). Set Up Connect Audits in simPRO.

Pre-audits marked as Required are mandatory to complete and will restrict access to almost all other pages in the workflow until they are completed. A green spanner icon indicates a pre-audit is in progress. Certain questions in pre-audits may contain responses that, if selected, are set up to prevent the user from submitting a quote as Complete.

Once a pre-audit is complete and a sync is performed, you can view the completed audit in Connect and simPRO.

To complete a pre-audit in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Pre Audits page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Select additional field technicians to include to complete the audit if desired, then tap Next.
  3. Answer the questionnaire as required, tapping Next to continue.
  4. Confirm any audit messages, then tap Get Signature.
  5. Trace your signature using a finger or using a stylus, then tap Done.
    • To re-write your signature, tap Cancel > Get Signature.
  6. Tap Finished.

To start a pre-audit again and erase your existing answers, tap Cancel > Yes to confirm, then begin the audit as normal.

You can still access the Pricing, Attachments and Email Quote pages in the workflow when a required pre-audit has not been completed.

View completed pre-audits

After you Sync Connect with simPRO, completed pre-audits will save as PDF attachments to the quote and can be viewed in Attachments in Connect and simPRO.

PDFs will be formatted according to the audit type selected.

 View attachments

View and download quote attachments to your mobile device, as well as Capture and upload an image and Upload an image from the gallery to a quote in Connect. After syncing your Connect device, you can view attachments in simPRO in Details > Attachments in the quote.

The larger the quantity of attached photos, the longer a sync from Connect to simPRO will take. It is recommended you perform a sync of quotes with multiple attachments with a stable internet connection. When syncing with simPRO, Connect will reduce the size of attachments to reduce the time to upload.

The download icon indicates an attachment has not been downloaded to the device. The magnifying glass icon indicates that the attachment has been downloaded and can be viewed.

To view and download an attachment in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Attachments page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap the desired attachment to download and view on your device.
  3. Depending on your device, copy, message or email the audit as desired.

Tap the trash icon to delete an attachment.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet to download attachments.

Capture and upload an image

You can capture a photo from within the app to upload as an image attachment to a quote in Connect.

To capture a photo to upload to a quote:

  1. Navigate to the Attachments page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap Take Photo.
  3. Capture the desired image using your device's camera functionalities.
    • Tap Retake to retake a photo.
  4. When you have captured the desired image, tap Use Photo.
  5. Enter the desired image name in Enter new name here.
  6. Tap Finished.

If you tap Cancel when renaming the image, the image will still appear as an attachment under its original name.

Upload an image from the gallery

To upload a photo from your device's image gallery to a quote in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Attachments page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap Gallery.
  3. Navigate through the image gallery as your device permits, then then tap the desired photo.
  4. Enter the desired image name in Enter new name here.
  5. Tap Finished.

If you tap Cancel when renaming the image, the image will still appear as an attachment under its original name.

 Enter work notes

Enter work notes to describe the works completed for a quote in Connect. You can also view descriptions of past works completed on the quote by a field technician under History.

To enter information about works completed for a quote:

  1. Navigate to the Work Notes page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Enter notes as desired.
    • Alternatively, tap Insert Script to select a script created in simPRO and enabled for Connect to describe works completed, then enter any additional information as required.
  3. When finished, continue through the workflow as desired.

To view work notes in simPRO after syncing, simply access the relevant quote card, then click Actual > Work Completed.

 Complete tasks

This feature is only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise.

View, edit and complete tasks in Connect. You can also record the actual amount of time it takes to complete a task.

Tasks available in Connect are created and assigned to customers in Enterprise. You can access tasks from the dashboard, or alternatively, within a scheduled or assigned quote. To access a task assigned to a specific customer of a quote, access the quote from the runsheet, then navigate to the Tasks page using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.

To complete a task in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Tasks page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
    • Alternatively, go to the dashboard, then tap Tasks.
  2. Tap the desired task.
  3. Review the task description and Date Due.
    • If you are completing a task from the dashboard, you can also review the Estimated Time for the task, as well as the Customer, Quote, and Site information, Status, and Priority.
  4. Tap Start to create a timestamp a record the actual time to complete the task.
  5. Enter Notes as desired.
  6. Enter the Date Completed.
  7. Tap Finished.

Your tasks in Enterprise will be updated when you Sync Connect with simPRO. When you have completed a task with a Date Completed, the task will be marked as Closed in Enterprise.

You need to tap Stop when timing a task to accurately record how long it takes to complete.

 Approve and email the quote

The Quote Summary page of the workflow allows you to record a customer's approval of a quote and alters your Connect workflow accordingly. You can also Enter a PO number supplied by the customer, and Email a quote to the customer using specific quote forms.

If a customer chooses not to approve a quote and proceed with the job, tap No under Is the customer ready to accept?, and proceed through the workflow as normal.

**screenshot - customer approval

If a customer chooses to approve a quote and proceed with the job, you need to collect their signature. When you Sync Connect with simPRO you can View signatures in simPRO when you access the quote card. You can then Submit and convert a quote in the Submit page.

To view and sign off on a quote in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Quote Summary page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Under Does the customer want to proceed?, tap Yes.
  3. Enter a PO number if required.
  4. Verify the Client Name.
  5. To edit, tap the name and make changes as desired.
  6. Tap Get Signature.
  7. Allow the customer to trace their signature on the screen by hand or using a stylus.
  8. To rewrite a signature, tap Delete Signature > Yes > Get Signature.
  9. Tap Done.

To change whether the customer has accepted and approved the quote, tap the pencil icon next to The customer has NOT ACCEPTED / The customer has ACCEPTED.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet for a customer to accept a quote.

If you Adjust the quote after signing off in any way, the signature will be deleted.

Email a quote to the customer

Email quote forms to a customer from Connect. You need to enable the forms you wish to send from Connect in System > Setup > Forms Setup > Quotes > Forms in simPRO.

**screenshot** - Connect Quotes UI - email quote forms

To email a quote form to the customer:

  1. Navigate to the Quote Summary page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu, then tap Email Quote.
    • Alternatively, access the jump menu, then tap Email Quote.
  2. Tap Add Recipients to send the email to other recipients (the customer's or your email address should appear by default).
    • Under Emails, select the desired simPRO-defined email address from the drop-down list, then tap Finished.
    • Alternatively, enter the desired email address under Manual Entry, then tap Finished.
  3. Select the desired quote form from the drop-down list.
  4. Leave the Subject and Message blank to send the Email Template as set up in System > Setup > Forms Setup > Quotes > Email.
    • Alternatively, manually enter a Subject and Message to override the template.
  5. Tap Send.

Enter a PO number

If the customer has been set up in simPRO to require a purchase order number, you need to enter this information when signing off the quote. A purchase order number is an extra reference for the customer and does not have to be a number.

In Connect, you can submit, but not convert the quote without a PO number. This is different to the workflow in simPRO where a PO number can be supplied after a quote conversion.

Adjust the quote after signing off

When you attempt to alter the quote pricing or the PO number after the customer has signed off, a warning will appear to advise you that any changes will delete the customer signature. This will occur even if the final price of the quote remains the same.

Tap Yes to continue making changes. Tap Cancel to leave the quote pricing as is and retain the customer signature.

 Record quote times

Use the Times page of the workflow in Connect to Edit actual times and Add a new time block to reflect hours worked or time spent on a quote. You can also Resolve mismatching times to match the scheduled time.

Edit actual times

To edit actual time for a quote:

  1. Navigate to the Times page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Under Actual Time (date), select your actual start and finish times from the drop-down lists.
  3. If applicable, select a user-defined schedule Rate (such as Overtime 2.0x) from the drop-down list.
  4. Continue with the workflow.

Schedule rates will only be available to select in Connect if enabled in simPRO under System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.

Add a new time block

To add a new time block to a quote:

  1. Navigate to the Times page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap New Time Block.
  3. Select Start and Finish times.
  4. If applicable, select a user-defined Rate (such as Overtime 2.0x​) from the drop-down list.
  5. Tap Finished.

By default, the logged-in user will be selected as the field technician. You can add other technicians or plant items from the Technician drop-down list if they are assigned to the applicable cost centre and zone.

Schedule rates will only be available to select in Connect if enabled in simPRO under System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.

Locked schedules cannot be updated.

Resolve mismatching times

A Time Mismatch warning will appear if your recorded actual time does not match the original scheduled time for the quote.

To resolve mismatching times:

  1. Tap Resolve on the Time Mismatch warning.
  2. Tap Update Actual Times.
  3. Tap the Delete icon to delete the old schedule. If the times are close enough, the actual time will automatically be deleted in favor of the scheduled time.
  4. Submit the quote as normal to Sync in the background, or alternatively, Sync all data as normal.

When you access the quote in simPRO, the schedule will be updated with the new time.

 Complete post-audits for quotes

Complete post-audits in Connect that have been set up in simPRO to assess safety conditions, work methods and equipment quality after completing work on a quote.

simPRO audits are arranged in three types: Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS), Job Safety Analysis (JSA) and Quality Assurance (QA). See Set Up Connect Audits for more information.

Post-audits marked as Required are mandatory to complete and will prevent a quote from being submitted with a Complete status until they are completed. A green spanner icon indicates a post-audit is in progress. Certain questions in post-audits may contain responses that, if selected, are set up to prevent the user from submitting a quote as Complete.

To complete a post-audit in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Post Audits page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Select additional employees to include to complete the audit if desired, then tap Next.
  3. Answer the questionnaire as required, tapping Next to continue.
  4. Confirm any audit messages, then tap Get Signature.
  5. Trace your signature by hand or using a stylus, then tap Done.
    • To re-write your signature, tap Cancel > Get Signature.
  6. Tap Finished.

To start a post-audit again and erase your existing answers, tap Cancel > Yes to confirm, then begin the audit as normal. Once a post-audit is complete and a sync is performed, you can View completed post-audits in Connect and simPRO.

View completed post-audits

After you Sync Connect with simPRO, completed post-audits will save as PDF attachments to the quote and can be viewed in Attachments in Connect and simPRO.

PDFs will be formatted according to the audit type selected.

 Submit and convert a quote

When you submit a quote in Connect that a customer has approved on the Quote Summary page, you can Convert a quote to an assigned job with a due date to be started immediately.

Alternatively, you can Convert a quote to an unassigned job and leave it to be assigned and scheduled in simPRO.

**SCREENSHOT**

Your device needs to be online and have a stable internet connection to submit and / or convert a quote to a job. If you attempt to convert a quote to a job without an internet connection, tap Try Again to re-attempt the conversion, or alternatively, tap Try Later to return to the runsheet and attempt to convert the quote when you have a stable internet connection.

Convert a quote to an assigned job with a due date

To convert a quote to an unassigned job in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Submit page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Select Completed from the drop-down list.
  3. Select Start Work immediately.
  4. Tap Submit.

You will then be taken to the Details page of the converted job, which will have been auto-assigned to you. The job will appear on the Today page of the runsheet.

A converted job can only be assigned to the field technician who submitted the quote in Connect. You can reassign or reschedule the job to another technician in simPRO.

Convert a quote to an unassigned job

To convert a quote to an unassigned job in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Submit page of the desired quote using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Select Completed from the drop-down list.
  3. Tap Submit.

You will then be taken to the runsheet with the converted job in the Other page. You can then schedule the job in simPRO as normal.
