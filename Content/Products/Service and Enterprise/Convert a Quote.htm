Convert a Quote

Overview

To convert a quote to a job, recurring job, or recurring invoice, click Options > Convert To > Job / Recurring Job / Recurring Invoice > OK.

Alternatively, access the desired quote, click Finish, then click Convert To > Job / Recurring Job / Recurring Invoice > OK.

If the quote has been approved, it will move to Quotes > Closed and the job / recurring job / recurring invoice will be created. The cost centre will be locked according to settings in System > Setup > Defaults > System > Jobs and the Next Due Date for converted recurring jobs / invoices will be the same as the conversion date.

You can then update the new job / recurring job / recurring invoice as desired. To access the original quote from within the converted job / recurring job, go to Details > Settings > Options, then click Open Quote No.

If the job cost centre has been locked by items and price, any additional parts added to the job either in Parts & Labour or from a receipted purchase order not created in the quote will be made non-billable, and you will not be able to add additional labour rates to the job.
