Materials Cost The total estimated cost of all billable materials added to the cost centre.
Resources Cost

The total estimated costs associated with labour and plant resources added to the cost centre, including overhead. Click the grey drop-down arrow to view the following costs:

  • Labour is the total estimated costs associated with labour resources, excluding the overhead.
  • Labour Hours is the total number of labour hours added to the quote.
  • Overhead is the total overhead recovery that applies to the quote based on the number of applicable resource hours.
Materials Markup The overall markup on materials in absolute value. This figure represents the difference between the total materials cost and the total material sell.
Resources Markup The overall markup on resources in absolute value. This figure represents the difference between total resource cost and total resource sell.
Discount / Fee

The discount or fee added as an absolute value or as a percentage of the combined Materials Cost, Resources Cost, Materials Markup, and Resources Markup.

To add a discount / fee, click the pencil icon, then select Down / Up, enter either a percentage or an absolute value, select if the discount / fee is Tax Inclusive, then click Save.
Sub Total The combined Materials Cost, Resources Cost, Materials Markup, Resources Markup and Discount / Free. Click the pencil icon to manually adjust this price.
Tax code applied The tax applied to the Sub Total. Click the pencil icon to select a different tax code, then click Save.
Total The combined Sub Total plus tax. Click the pencil icon to manually adjust this price.
Gross P / L The difference between total direct costs excluding overhead and the Sub Total.
Gross Margin The Gross P / L divided by the Sub Total as a percentage.
Nett P / L The difference between total direct costs including overhead and the Sub Total.
Nett Margin The Nett P / L divided by the Sub Total as a percentage.​
