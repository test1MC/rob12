Archive Reasons

Overview

If you set up archive reasons, when you archive a lead, quote or job in simPRO, you will be prompted to select the reason why. By creating standard archive reasons to select from, such as 'Lost to competitor' or 'Response time too slow', you can easily create reports on archived leads, quotes or jobs to analyse why your company might not be following through with certain opportunities.

Create an archive reason

To create an archive reason:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup.
  2. Hover over Archive Reasons, and click the option you wish to create an archive reason for (Leads, Quotes or Jobs).
  3. Click Create Archive Reason.
  4. Enter a Name.
  5. Click Create.
 Edit an archive reason

To edit an archive reason:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Archive Reasons > Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  2. Click the archive reason you wish to edit.
  3. Change the Name as desired.
  4. Click Save.

Use the drag handles to reposition archive reasons. You may wish to place frequently used reasons at the top of the list so they appear first when archiving.

 Delete an archive reason

To delete an archive reason:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Archive Reasons > Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  2. Click the red delete icon on the archive reason you wish to delete.
  3. Click OK.

To delete multiple archive reasons at once:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Archive Reasons > Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  2. Select the archive reasons you want to delete, or click Select All.
  3. Click Delete.
  4. Click OK.

Deleting an archive reason does not remove it from the record of archived projects it was selected for.

 Select an archive reason when archiving

You are prompted to select an archive reason when archiving a project.

To select an archive reason when archiving:

  1. Go to Leads, Quotes or Jobs > Open Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  2. Go to Leads, Quotes or Jobs > Open Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  3. Go to Quotes or Jobs > Open Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  4. On the lead, quote or job you wish to archive, click Options > Archive Lead / Quote / Job.
  5. On the lead, quote or job you wish to archive, click Options > Archive Lead / Quote / Job.
  6. On the quote or job you wish to archive, click Options > Archive Lead / Quote / Job.
  7. Select a Reason from the drop-down list.
  8. Click Archive.

To view a closed project's archive reason:

  1. Go to Leads, Quotes or Jobs > Closed Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  2. Go to Leads, Quotes or Jobs > Closed Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  3. Go to Quotes or Jobs > Closed Leads / Quotes / Jobs.
  4. Click the desired lead, quote or job.
  5. Click the desired lead, quote or job.
  6. Click the desired quote or job.
  7. Go to Details > Logs.
  8. In the Message column, look for 'Archived'. The reason will be written in the same line, such as 'Archived Quote - Lost to Competitor'.

Create a report on archive reasons

Select Archived Reasons when creating a Lead / Quote Activity report for a better understanding of why opportunities were not converted to jobs. Create this report through Reports > View Reports > Leads / Quotes > Activity.
