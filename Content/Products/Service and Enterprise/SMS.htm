SMS

Overview

simPRO has the ability to send SMS (short message service) to mobile devices. You can send broadcast SMS, for example, to remind employees and customers of meetings, or directly from a job, quote or activity in the Schedule View.

To use SMS in simPRO, you need to register with the third party provider in your country and purchase blocks of SMS. In Australia and New Zealand, SMS is powered by third party provider Streetdata. In the United Kingdom, this service is powered by third party provider Textlocal. SMS in simPRO is not yet supported in the United States.

Set up SMS

To use SMS in simPRO, you need to go to System > Setup > Defaults > Extensions > Mobility Extensions, then under SMS, select On.

Then when you return to the dashboard and access the Utilities menu, the SMS option will be accessible.

To set up SMS messaging in simPRO:

  1. Go to Utilities > SMS.
  2. Click Register.
  3. On the Textlocal Login page, click Sign up for a free account now and enter information as required, remembering the password you create for the account.
  4. When you have received a confirmation email from Textlocal, return to the Send SMS page in simPRO.
  5. In the Username and Password text boxes, enter the email address and password you used to create a Textlocal account.
  6. Click Update.

Learn more here about SMS pricing and instructions for registering with Streetdata, the Australia and New Zealand third party provider for SMS in simPRO.

To set up SMS messaging in simPRO in Australia and New Zealand:

  1. Go to Utilities > SMS.
  2. Click Register.
  3. On the Streetdata Registration page, enter information as required, then click Next.
  4. When you have received a confirmation email from Streetdata, return to the Send SMS page in simPRO and enter the Username and Password as assigned in the email.
  5. Click Update.

 Send an SMS Send a custom message

To send a custom message:

  1. Go to Utilities > SMS.
  2. Under Send When, select:
    • Immediately to send the SMS immediately.
    • Specified Date to send the SMS at a specific Date and Time.
  3. Select from the Recipient List as desired.
    • Alternatively, add mobile numbers manually in the To text box. Use commas to separate multiple numbers.
  4. To enable recipients to respond to a different mobile number than that entered in your employee card file, enter the number in the From text box.
  5. Enter the desired Message.
    • Alternatively, click Insert Script to enter an SMS script set up in simPRO.
  6. Click Send SMS.

The Recipient List will only display employees / customers with a mobile phone number entered in their card file.

 Send a schedule notification

To send an SMS directly from the Schedule View:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day View / Week View / Project View.
  2. Right-click the desired time block.
  3. Hover over Send and click SMS.
  4. Enter SMS details as normal.
    • The recipient should populate with the scheduled person if they have a mobile number entered in their card file.
  5. Click Send.
