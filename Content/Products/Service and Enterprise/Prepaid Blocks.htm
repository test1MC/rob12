Prepaid Blocks

Overview

After creating Prepaid Labour Types, you can create prepaid blocks, which you then use to sell prepaid time to a customer.

In simPRO, prepaid blocks are billed to customers through a prepaid job. You can create a catalogue of prepaid blocks, sorted into groups and sub-groups, to suit various customers and work types.

Create a prepaid block group or subgroup

Before you create a prepaid block, you first need to create a group for it.

To create a prepaid block group or subgroup:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Prepaid Blocks.
  2. Click Create Group, or click an existing group then click Create Subgroup.
  3. Enter a Name.
  4. Click Save.
 Edit a prepaid block group or subgroup

To edit a prepaid block group or subgroup:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Prepaid Blocks.
  2. On the group or sub-group you wish to edit, click Options > Edit Group / Subgroup Details.
  3. Make changes as desired.
  4. Click Save.
 Delete a prepaid block group or subgroup

To delete a prepaid block group or subgroup:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Prepaid Blocks.
  2. On the group or subgroup you wish to delete, click Options > Delete Group / Subgroup.
  3. Click OK.
  4. To delete multiple groups or subgroups at once:
  5. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Prepaid Blocks.
  6. Select the groups or subgroups you wish to delete, or click Select All.
  7. Click Delete.
  8. Click OK.

Deleting a group or subgroup will archive or delete all prepaid blocks within it. Prepaid blocks with history will be archived, while prepaid blocks without history will be deleted. Users without the relevant security group settings will not be able to perform this function.

 Create a prepaid block

When creating and editing a block, you can choose the amount of time it is for. If you sell prepaid time by the hour, enter one hour; if you want to sell large blocks of time, such as 20 hours, enter that length.

To create a prepaid block:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Prepaid Blocks.
  2. Create or click a group or subgroup.
  3. Click Create Prepaid Block.
  4. Enter a Name.
  5. Enter a Part No., Prepaid Time, and Sell Price as desired.
  6. Select a Sales Tax Code.
  7. Click Create.
 Edit a prepaid block

To edit a prepaid block:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Prepaid Blocks.
  2. Click the relevant group and subgroup.
  3. Click the prepaid block you wish to edit.
  4. Make changes as desired.
  5. Click Save.
 Sell prepaid time to a customer

After setting up prepaid blocks, you can sell them to a customer by adding them to a prepaid job and invoicing the job.

To sell prepaid time to a customer:

  1. Go to Jobs > Create New Prepaid Job.
  2. Search and select a customer name and site, and insert other relevant details.
  3. Click Next.
  4. Select a Cost Centre and click Add.
  5. Click the cost centre you just added.
  6. Enter a Description.
  7. Click the Prepaid Blocks tab, then click the sub-tab Prepaid Blocks.
  8. Click through the prepaid block group and subgroup to find your prepaid blocks.
  9. On the prepaid block you want to add, enter the number of blocks desired.
  10. Click Add Items.
  11. Edit the remaining job details as desired, then click Finish.
  12. Click Invoice Job.
  13. Finish and send the invoice as usual.

To convert a service job to a prepaid job, open the job, go to Details > Settings, click Job Setup, then select Prepaid as the Job Type.

 View a customer's prepaid time

To view the prepaid time a customer has purchased:

  1. Open the customer's card file.
  2. Click the Rates tab.
  3. Click the Prepaid Time sub-tab.

If desired, you can select an expiry date for the prepaid time using the Date Expires date picker.

 Use prepaid time

To use prepaid time on a job:

  1. Create a job as usual and invoice it.
  2. If prepaid time is available for that customer and labour type, the Prepaid Time section will appear at the bottom of the Tax Invoice page.
  3. Enter the amount of prepaid Time To Use.
  4. Finish and send the invoice as usual.

Expired prepaid time cannot be used on a job.

If a multiplier was selected when creating the prepaid labour type, real labour hours will be reduced accordingly. For example, if a prepaid labour rate has a multiplier of 2, two prepaid labour hours will be worth one real hour of labour.
