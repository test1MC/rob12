Set Up Xero with simPRO

Overview

Before you begin posting your simPRO transactions to Xero, you need to enable the accounting link in simPRO.

Enable the Xero accounting link

To enable the Xero accounting link in simPRO:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Accounts Integration.
  2. Click the Xero logo.
  3. Adjust settings and export options as desired.
  4. Click Save.

To access the accounting link, click Dashboard in the top left corner, then go to Utilities > Xero.

 Adjust Xero settings

You need to adjust the following settings to enable the Xero accounting link in simPRO:

Invoice Status

Select Awaiting Approval to approve invoices in Xero before you can apply payment.

Select Approved and Awaiting Payment to send payments at the same time as the invoice.

Inventory Journal (Manual Journal) Status

Select Draft or Posted.
Adjust Xero export options

You can transfer the following types of transactions to Xero:

  • Customers
  • Invoices
  • Suppliers
  • Supplier invoices
  • Payments

To enable a transaction type to be transferred, select On. To prevent it from being transferred, select Off.

You can select from the following settings for importing and exporting Payments between Xero and simPRO:

Both (Import to simPRO and Export to Xero)

Transfer payments both ways between simPRO and Xero.

On (Export to Xero)

Only transfer payments from simPRO to Xero.

On (Import to simPRO)

Only transfer payments from Xero to simPRO.

Off

Do not transfer payments at all.
Prepare simPRO for Xero

To make sure your transactions post to Xero correctly, your data in simPRO needs to match your data in Xero. This includes customers and suppliers (which can be imported from Xero in the initial import), tax codes, and accounts.

You can use the Tax Code Mapping and Account Mapping tabs in the Xero accounting link to make sure your tax codes and accounts match up.

Map accounts

In simPRO your accounts are linked to your cost centres, payment methods and Financial Defaults. You will need to map accounts from all of these places to your accounts in Xero.

To map your simPRO accounts to accounts in Xero:

  1. Go to Utilities > Xero.
  2. Click the Account Mapping tab.
    • If you are not connected to your Xero account, click Connect to Xero, log in as normal, and click Authorise if necessary. You will then be returned to simPRO and the Account Mapping tab will appear.
  3. Select the Xero Account that corresponds with the applicable:
    • Defaults: the account names entered in System > Setup > Defaults > Financial.
    • Cost Centres.
    • Payment Methods.
  4. Click Save.

When you change a setting in this area, go to System > Setup > Defaults > Financial in simPRO to ensure the change has reflected accurately.

Tracked Inventory in Xero is unrelated to inventory tracking in simPRO. Inventory journals generated by simPRO are transferred into Xero as Manual Journals. Learn more in our Inventory Tracking article.

 Map tax codes

You can map your tax codes in simPRO to tax codes in Xero, and prevent the possibility of error incurred by individually entering the corresponding Xero tax codes when creating tax codes in simPRO.

To map your simPRO tax codes with tax codes in Xero:

  1. Go to Utilities > Xero.
  2. Click the Tax Code Mapping tab.
    • If you are not connected to your Xero account, click Connect to Xero, log in as normal, and click Authorise if necessary. You will then be returned to simPRO and the Tax Code Mapping tab will appear.
  3. Under Xero Tax Code (Income) select the relevant tax codes from the drop-down lists to match them with your simPRO tax codes.
  4. Under Xero Tax Code (Expense) select the relevant tax codes from the drop-down lists to match them with your simPRO tax codes.
  5. Click Save.
