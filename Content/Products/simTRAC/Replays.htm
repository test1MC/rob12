Replays

Overview

Use the Replays tab to view a vehicle's movements on the map over a specified period of time.

View a replay

To view a replay:

  1. Click the Replays tab.
  2. Select a vehicle from the drop-down list.
  3. Use the date picker to select a start date and use the sliders to specify the start time.
  4. Use the date picker to select a finish date and use the sliders to specify the finish time.
  5. Click Load.
  6. Click the eye icon and select any activities you want to see in the replay.
  7. Click play.

Key icons on the map indicate locations where the vehicle was turned off. Red exclamation icons indicate locations of selected activities.

To view a different replay, click Change.

 Advanced

To skip to a specific place in the replay, use the slider or click an address in the list.

If your replay takes place over several days, click the date and select from the date picker to skip to a specific day.

Click fast forward to watch the replay at double speed.
