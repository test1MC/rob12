Connect Jobs

Overview

Use Connect to view, create, update, complete and invoice jobs in the field. You can work through jobs created in simPRO, as well as jobs converted from quotes in both simPRO and Connect. See Submit and convert a quote for more information about quotes in Connect.

Alternatively, you can Create a job in Connect.

The diagram below displays the recommended job workflow to use in Connect. You can customise your workflow to suit your business by adjusting your pages setup.

Create a job in Connect

Create a new job in the field using Connect. You can generate job details by selecting from customers, sites, cost centres and service fees created in simPRO. Alternatively, you can create a new customer, site, or, when working with simPRO Enterprise, lead.

Once you have created a job, customer, site, or lead, you need to Sync Connect with simPRO, where you can manage information as normal.

To create a job in Connect:

  1. Go to the dashboard, then, if you are working with simPRO Service, tap Job. If you are working with simPRO Enterprise, tap Project.
  2. If you are working with simPRO Enterprise and operating in a multi-company build, select the desired Company.
  3. Under Type, tap Job.
  4. Select a Customer by tapping Select customer, then entering and tapping the desired customer name from the drop-down list.
    • If you are working with simPRO Enterprise, use the filter options to view Customers, Leads, or All.
    • Alternatively, you can Create a customer or lead in a quote.
  5. Tap Select site to select the desired site from the drop-down list.
    • Use the filter options to view Customer sites or All sites.
    • Alternatively, you can create a New Site. Enter the Site Name and address details, then tap Finished.
  6. Select a Cost Centre.​
  7. Assign a Technician.
    • ​​Only field technicians with the same cost centre and / or zone applied to their card file in simPRO will be available to select. The current Connect user will be assigned by default.
  8. Enter the Due Date for the job to be completed.
  9. Select a Salesperson if desired.
  10. Apply a Service Fee if desired.
  11. Enter an Order No, Request No, and Description of the Quote or Cost Centre if desired.
  12. Tap Finished.

You will then be taken to the Details page to begin your job workflow. To advance through the workflow, use the jump menu in the top left or the navigation arrows at the bottom of the page.

To return to the dashboard, tap the Connect logo in the top left, then tap Runsheet > Back.

To create jobs in Connect, you need to have your project creation user permissions enabled in simPRO and your device needs to be connected to the internet.

Create a customer or lead in a job

To create a new customer or, when working with simPRO Enterprise, lead in a job:

  1. Create a job in Connect as normal.
  2. Tap New Customer.
  3. Tap Individual / Company to define the customer type.
  4. Enter the First Name and Last Name / Company Name.
  5. If you are working with simPRO Enterprise, select the Customer Type.
  6. Enter the customer's Individual Phone / Company Phone number, Email, and address details.
  7. Tap Finished.

 View job details

When you access a job in Connect, you will be taken to the Details page. As the first page of the workflow, the Details page displays important information about a job, including the schedule, the customer and site address, the job description and cost centres and the materials to be used.

Adjust the mobile status of a job

At the top of every page in the Connect workflow is the status bar. This displays the customer, job number and job status.

If you have not yet started work on a job, the Start Travel and Start Work status options will appear. These options create timestamps that are used to set actual times spent working on a job.

Tap Start Travel to update the mobile job status to Travelling. This will activate geostamping of the status location if enabled in your Geostamping settings, as well as record the timestamp. If you have set up automatic triggers, the status of the job will update in simPRO accordingly.

Alternatively, if you wish to make travel time non-billable, you can Schedule activities.

Tap Start Work to create a timestamp to begin the job. This will again activate geostamping of the status location and, if enabled, update the job status in simPRO. The status options will then disappear as the job is now in progress.

View schedule and site details

View the focus date and scheduled time for the job below the status bar. Tap the focus date to change it as desired, then tap Finished. The site contact and address information are below the focus date.

Tap the phone icon to view contact information for the Site, Customer or Project Manager. If you are using Connect on a device that can place phone calls and has an active connection to the mobile network, tap the desired phone number to initiate a phone call with the relevant contact.

If you are using a device that cannot make phone calls, tap the desired phone number, then tap:

Send Message Send the desired contact a message using iMessage. To set up iMessage for your device, go to Settings > Messages.
Add to Contacts Add the number to an existing contact or, alternatively, create a new contact in your device's contact list.
Copy Copy the number.

Tap the pin icon to locate the site in your device's default mapping solution.

View descriptions, materials and notes

Tap Work To Be Performed to view the Job Description, the relevant cost centre and the Parts List, containing the quantities of all billlable and non-billable items required for the job. The currency icon indicates that an item is billable.

Tap Materials Required to view only the billable items applied to the job in simPRO as well as their estimated quantities.

Tap Notes > Overview to view private job notes entered in simPRO.

 Complete pre-audits for jobs

Complete pre-audits in Connect to assess safety conditions, work methods and equipment quality before starting a job.

simPRO audits are arranged in three types: Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS), Job Safety Analysis (JSA) and Quality Assurance (QA). Set Up Connect Audits in simPRO to make them available in Connect.

Pre-audits marked as Required are mandatory to complete and will restrict access to almost all other pages in the workflow until they are completed. A green spanner icon indicates a pre-audit is in progress. Certain questions in pre-audits may contain responses that, if selected, are set up to prevent the user from submitting a job as Complete.

Once a pre-audit is complete and a sync is performed, you can view the completed audit in Connect and simPRO.

To complete a pre-audit in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Pre Audits page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Select additional field technicians to include to complete the audit if desired, then tap Next.
  3. Answer the questionnaire as required, tapping Next to continue.
  4. Confirm any audit messages, then tap Get Signature.
  5. Trace your signature using a finger or a digital pen, then tap Done.
    • To re-write your signature, tap Cancel > Get Signature.
  6. Tap Finished.

To start a pre-audit again and erase your existing answers, tap Cancel > Yes to confirm, then begin the audit as normal.

You can still access Attachments and Purchase Orders in the workflow when a required pre-audit has not been completed.

View completed pre-audits

After you Sync Connect with simPRO, completed pre-audits will save as PDF attachments to the job and can be viewed in Attachments in Connect and simPRO.

PDFs will be formatted according to the audit type selected.

 Complete tasks

This feature is only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise.

View, edit and complete tasks in Connect. You can also record the actual amount of time it takes to complete a task.

Tasks available in Connect are created and assigned to customers in Enterprise. You can access tasks from the dashboard, or alternatively, within a scheduled or assigned job. To access a task assigned to a specific customer of a job, access the job from the runsheet, then navigate to the Tasks page using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.

To complete a task in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Tasks page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
    • Alternatively, go to the dashboard, then tap Tasks.
  2. Tap the desired task.
  3. Review the task description and Date Due.
    • If you are completing a task from the dashboard, you can also review the Estimated Time for the task, as well as the Customer, Job, and Site information, Status, and Priority.
  4. Tap Start to create a timestamp a record the actual time to complete the task.
  5. Enter Notes as desired.
  6. Enter the Date Completed.
  7. Tap Finished.

Your tasks in Enterprise will be updated when you Sync Connect with simPRO. When you have completed a task with a Date Completed, the task will be marked as Closed in Enterprise.

You need to tap Stop when timing a task to accurately record how long it takes to complete.

 Record used materials against a job

Record and edit materials used for a job in Connect.

Used materials can be added in three ways:

Required Add required materials to a job. These items are pre-defined as required for the job in simPRO.
Stock Items Add stock items from the relevant storage devices, depending on your storage device access.
Catalogue

Add materials from the catalogue without raising a purchase order or updating inventory. This is useful if you are using an item not recognised by simPRO as stock and do not have time to perform a stock adjustment.

You can allocate or order used materials added from the catalogue in simPRO, or when you Create a purchase order in Connect.

You can also enter Notes regarding used materials, which will update to the job card in simPRO under Actual > Material Notes. To edit a used item's quantity, tap the pencil icon, enter the desired number, then tap Finished. To delete an item, tap the pencil icon, then tap Delete > OK to confirm.

Add required materials to a job

To add pre-defined required materials:

  1. Navigate to the Used Materials page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap Add Material > Required.
  3. Verify the required quantities required of the desired items under Req.
  4. Enter the desired quantities under Qty, then tap Add Parts.
    • Alternatively, tap the add icon as desired, then tap Add Parts.
  5. Tap Finished.

Add stock items

To add used materials from stock:

  1. Navigate to the Used Materials page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap Add Material > Stock Items.
  3. Tap Filter > Select Storage to select a storage device to add stock from if desired.
  4. Enter the name or part number of the desired item in Search Stock, then tap Search.
    • Alternatively, tap Search to view all stock items.
  5. Enter the desired Qty for the relevant items, then tap Add Parts.
    • ​​Alternatively, tap the add icon as desired, then tap Add Parts.
  6. Tap Finished.

It is possible to add more items than are available under Stock in the stock list. This is useful if your inventory is incorrect, as Connect will notify you that a stock adjustment in simPRO is required.

Add materials from the catalogue

To add used materials from the catalogue:

  1. Navigate to the Used Materials page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap Add Material > Catalogue.
  3. Tap Filter > Select Group to narrow your search according to a specific catalogue group if desired.
    • Tap Select Sub-Group to narrow according to a specific catalogue sub-group.
  4. Enter the name or part number of the desired item in Search Catalogue, then tap Search.
    • Alternatively, tap Search to view all catalogue items.
  5. Enter the desired Qty for the relevant items, then tap Add Parts.
    • ​​Alternatively, tap the add icon as desired, then tap Add Parts.
  6. Tap Finished.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet when adding catalogue items to a job.

 View, create and receipt job purchase orders

To view purchase orders for a job in Connect, go to the runsheet, tap the desired job, then navigate to the POs page using the navigation arrows or the jump menu. You can then Create a purchase order, Add more items to a catalogue purchase order, Request a purchase order number, and Receipt a purchase order.

Purchase orders and purchase order numbers created in Connect can also be updated and receipted in simPRO.

Under Job Purchase Orders, you can view a list of all purchase orders made, including their order number, site, date ordered and order status.

The black box icon indicates an order is still outstanding. If an order has been fully receipted, a green box icon will appear.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet to create purchase orders.

Create a purchase order

To order specific items for a job from the catalogue, you need to create a new purchase order.

To create a new purchase order for a job in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the POs page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap New PO.
  3. Search and select the desired Supplier.
  4. Enter Private Notes as desired.
  5. Tap Request PO.
  6. Tap Add Materials.
  7. Enter the name or part number of the desired item in Search Catalogue, then tap Search.
    • Alternatively, tap Search to view all catalogue items.
  8. Tap Filter > Select Group to narrow your search according to a specific catalogue group.
    • Tap Select Sub-Group to narrow according to a specific catalogue sub-group.
  9. Enter the desired Qty for the relevant items, then tap Add Parts.
    • ​​Alternatively, tap the add icon as desired, then tap Add Parts.
  10. Tap Finished.

You can then view the order in the job card in simPRO, or alternatively, in Materials > Purchase Orders > Pending.

To edit an item's quantity on a purchase order in Connect, tap the pencil icon, enter the desired number, then tap Finished. To delete an item, tap the pencil icon > Delete > OK to confirm.

Add more items to a catalogue purchase order

To add more items to a catalogue purchase order for a job in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the POs page of the desired job.
  2. Tap the relevant purchase order.
  3. Tap Catalogue.
  4. Search and add items as normal.
  5. Tap Finished.

You can only add items to catalogue purchase orders and your device needs to be connected to the internet. You cannot add catalogue items to item entry or description purchase orders.

Request a purchase order number

To create an empty order to be completed and receipted in simPRO, you need to request a purchase order number in the relevant job in Connect.

To request a purchase order number for a job:

  1. Navigate to the POs page of the desired job.
  2. Search and select the desired Supplier.
  3. Enter Private Notes as desired.
  4. Tap Request PO.
  5. Tap Finished.

You can then complete and receipt the purchase order number in simPRO.

Receipt a purchase order

To receipt a job purchase order in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the POs page of the desired job.
  2. Tap Receive Goods.
  3. Enter the Packing Slip No.
  4. Tap Receipt All.
  5. Tap Finished.

You can only perform a full receipt of goods in Connect and your device needs to be connected to the internet.

 Manage customer assets

This feature is only available when Connect is working with simPRO Enterprise.

In simPRO, assets are items or equipment owned by your customers. You may have sold the customer these assets, or you might be doing planned or reactive maintenance of the assets as part of a contract.

To manage assets in Connect, you need to associate assets to sites manually or through an import in Enterprise. You can then add assets to a job, Search customer assets, Add a customer asset to a job and site, and Record asset test results.

To add a site asset to a job in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Assets page of the desired job.
  2. Tap Search Asset fields, enter the desired asset, then tap Search.
    • Alternatively, Search customer assets using the different search and filter options.
    • Alternatively, tap Search to view all assets.
  3. Ensure that either the All or Site option is selected.
  4. Tap the asset you wish to add.
  5. Tap Add to Job.
  6. Tap Finished.

Search customer assets

Tap Search to refine your search for customer assets according to the following options:

All Displays all assets.
Jobcard Displays all assets from all cost centres on the job card that the field technician is scheduled to.
Job Displays assets from every cost centre scheduled, except for the current job card.
Site Displays site assets that are not on the job card.

The Job and Jobcard options are combined when the Include Job Assets filter is applied. You can filter assets according to their type and test status.

You can access assets from all cost centres on the job card regardless of the cost centres you are scheduled to if you are the assigned field technician for the job.

Add a customer asset to a job and site

To add a new customer asset to a job and site:

  1. Navigate to the Assets page of the desired job.
  2. Tap Add New.
  3. Select the asset type from the Select Type drop-down list.
  4. Enter the required information in the fields defined in Enterprise.
  5. Tap Finished > Return to Assets.

To review the added asset, Search customer assets as normal. To edit an asset, navigate to the Assets page of the relevant job, then tap the desired asset and tap Edit. When you have finished making changes, click Finished.

When you edit an asset on Connect, the updated information will be reflected in Enterprise.

Record asset test results

You can use Connect to record test results when testing assets in the field.

Asset test results can be recorded as a Pass or Fail or No Test, as well as specific data defined by test readings, for example electrical readings, pressure readings, temperatures etc.

If you have purchased Maintenance Planner, you can use additional functionalities to help you test and manage assets, such as service levels and failure points and recommendations. You can also assign test readings to specific service levels in Enterprise so that only the necessary test readings will appear in Connect when you perform a particular service.

To record asset test results:

  1. Navigate to the Assets page of the desired job using the jump menu.
  2. Tap the asset you wish to record a result for.
  3. Choose an option:
    • Tap Pass if the test was successfully completed.
    • Tap No Test if the test was not done or is incomplete. (please ensure you check your industry standards for non-compliance/no test)
    • Tap Fail if the asset failed during testing. If you have Maintenance Planner, you can then select Failure Points and Recommendations for rectification of the failure.
  4. Enter Result Notes as desired.
  5. If you have set up test readings for the asset, tap the Test Readings tab to record specific asset data. When using Maintenance Planner and have linked your test readings to service levels, only the test readings will show, which are associated to the current service level.
  6. Tap Finished.

Tap Past Results to view an asset's past test results as well as the testing field technician, as well as the date and job for which the asset was tested. Alternatively, you can view an asset's test results in Enterprise by going to Reports > Assets > Asset Test History, and then searching for the desired asset.

After syncing, the asset's test results will be viewable in Enterprise in the relevant job's Customer Assets tab.

 View job attachments

View and download job attachments to your mobile device, as well as Capture and upload an image and Upload an image from your device to a job in Connect. After syncing your Connect device, you can view attachments in simPRO in Details > Attachments in the job.

The larger the quantity of attached photos, the longer a sync from Connect to simPRO will take. It is recommended you perform a sync of jobs with multiple attachments with a stable internet connection. When syncing with simPRO, Connect will reduce the size of attachments to reduce the time to upload.

The download icon indicates an attachment has not been downloaded to the device. The magnifying glass icon indicates that the attachment has been downloaded and can be viewed.

To view and download an attachment in Connect:

  2. Tap the desired attachment to download and view on your device.
  3. Depending on your device functionalities, copy, message or email the audit as desired.

Tap the trash icon to delete an attachment.

Your device needs to be connected to the internet when downloading attachments.

Capture and upload an image

You can capture a photo from within the app to upload as an image attachment to a job in Connect.

  1. Navigate to the Attachments page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap Gallery.

If you tap Cancel when renaming the image, the image will still appear as an attachment under its original name.

 Record job times

Use the Times page of the workflow in Connect to Edit actual time, Add a new time block, and Use 48-hour scheduling to reflect hours worked on a job. You can also Resolve mismatching times to match the scheduled time.

This information is used to generate a timesheet in simPRO to assist with job costing and payroll processes.

Edit actual time

To edit actual time for a job:

  1. Navigate to the Times page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Under Actual Time (date), select your actual start and finish times from the drop-down lists.
  3. If applicable, select a user-defined schedule rate from the drop-down list.
  4. When finished, continue through the workflow as desired.

Schedule rates will only be available to select in Connect if enabled in simPRO under System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.

Add a new time block

To add a new time block to a job:

  1. Navigate to the Times page of the desired job using the navigation arrows or the jump menu.
  2. Tap New Time Block.
  3. Select Start and Finish times.
  4. If applicable, select a user-defined Rate (such as Overtime 2.0x​) from the drop-down list.
  5. Tap Finished.

By default, the logged-in user will be selected as the field technician. You can add other technicians or plant items from the Technician drop-down list if they are assigned to the applicable cost centre and zone.

Schedule rates will only be available to select in Connect if enabled in simPRO under System > Setup > System Setup > Labour > Schedule Rates.

You can also schedule field technicians across midnight if you have enabled 48-hour scheduling.

Locked schedules cannot be updated.

Use 48-hour scheduling

If you have enabled 48-hour scheduling in your in-app settings, you can select start times from the following day when scheduling a job.

When you schedule a job over midnight, the time block will then split into two to account for the shift before and after midnight.

If 48-hour scheduling is not enabled, you can only schedule a time block starting from the current day according to the focus date.​

Resolve mismatching times

A Time Mismatch warning will appear if your actual time does not match the original scheduled time.

To resolve mismatching times:

  1. Tap Resolve on the Time Mismatch warning.
  2. Tap Update Actual Times.
  3. Tap the trash icon to delete the old schedule.
  4. When finished, continue through the workflow as desired.

The schedule will be updated with the new time in simPRO after performing a sync.

If the times are close enough, the actual time will automatically be deleted in favour of the scheduled time.

To sign off a job in Connect:

  3. Tap Get Signature.
  4. Trace the signature on the screen using a finger or digital pen.

When the job has been submitted, a background sync will perform and the signatures will upload to the job card in simPRO.

View signatures in simPRO

  2. Click Schedule.
  3. Click Options > Job Card > Open Job Card on the relevant time block.
  4. Click Mobile.

You can view the collected signatures under Signatures.

Adjust sign off area settings

To edit the confirmation statement that a customer or field technician signs off on, you need to adjust sign off area settings in simPRO. You can also adjust whether you show customer or field technician signatures in the job card.

To adjust sign off area settings in simPRO:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Forms Setup.

  2. In the left column, click Job Card > Sign Off Area.

  3. Under Customer / Technician:
    • ​​Select On to show customer / field technician signatures in the job card. Select Off to hide them.
    • Enter or adjust the Statement you wish your customer / field technician to sign off on.

 Invoice a job

Create, edit and email invoices for jobs in Connect.

You can only create full invoices of jobs in Connect. To use progress claims (only available in simPRO Enterprise) or deposit invoices in Connect, you will need to create them in simPRO before attending the relevant job. Alternatively, you can create a full invoice in Connect and apply a partial payment.

If a job has been partially invoiced in simPRO, you can create an invoice from the Pricing page as normal, but instead of creating a new invoice, tap Invoice Remaining.

To create an invoice for a job in Connect:

  1. Navigate to the Billing > Invoices in the desired job using the jump menu.
    • Alternatively, navigate to the Pricing page after submitting a job or by using the jump menu.
  2. Tap Create Invoice / Invoice Remaining.
  3. Adjust the Description as defined in simPRO as desired.
    • Alternatively, tap Insert Script to insert a script created in simPRO.
  4. Review the Invoice Total.
  5. Tap Continue.

You will be taken to the Payment page where you can apply a payment, or alternatively, email the invoice to the customer.

An invoice number will be generated and will reflect in simPRO after syncing.

When invoicing a customer in Connect with an simPRO-created progress claim or deposit invoice, editing billable items and final pricing will be restricted after the invoice has been created.

Edit a job invoice

To edit an invoice:

  1. Navigate to Billing > Invoices in the desired job using the jump menu.
  2. Tap the list icon next to the invoice, then tap Edit Invoice.
  3. Make changes as desired.
  4. Tap Continue.

You will then be taken to the Payments page. You can make a payment, or alternatively, tap Skip to email the invoice.

Email a job invoice

To email an invoice:

  1. Navigate to Billing > Invoices in the desired job using the jump menu, tap the list icon, then tap Email Invoice.
    • Alternatively, tap Skip on the Payments page after creating an invoice, or to email an invoice with a record of payments applied, tap Make Payment > Continue.
  2. Tap Add Recipients to send the email to other recipients (the customer's or your email address should populate Invoice Recipients by default).
    • Under Emails, select the desired simPRO-defined email address from the drop-down list, then tap Finished.
    • Alternatively, enter the desired email address under Manual Entry, then tap Finished.
  3. Enter the Subject and Message of the email as desired.
    • Alternatively, leave blank if you have invoice email templates set up in simPRO.
  4. Tap Send.

You will then be taken back to the Invoices page.

You will receive an email notification to the account that is sending the invoice in the event that an email is unsuccessful.

 Apply a payment to a job

Process and apply partial and full payments to invoiced jobs in Connect. You can process payments for a range of payment methods as set up in simPRO, including credit cards if you have activated simPRO Payments.

When you have finished processing the full payment, you can then email the invoice including the payments applied. Alternatively, you can email the invoice without applying a payment by tapping Skip in the Payment page.

You need to enable mobile billing and the relevant payment methods in simPRO to apply payments in Connect.

To apply a payment to an invoiced job:

  1. Navigate to the Billing > Invoices in the desired job using the jump menu.
  2. Tap the list icon of the desired invoice, then tap Apply Payment.
    • ​​Alternatively, if you are editing or creating the invoice, tap Continue.
  3. Under Apply Amount, enter the payment amount you wish to apply.
    • Alternatively, enter the percentage of the due balance that you wish to apply a payment to.
  4. Select the Payment Method.
  5. Enter the Date of payment.
  6. Enter the Cheque # and Notes as desired.
  7. Tap Make Payment > Continue to confirm.

Apply a credit card payment using simPRO Payments

To apply a credit card payment to an invoiced job using simPRO Payments (powered by IntegraPay):

  1. Apply a payment as normal.
  2. Select the simPRO Payments-associated credit card type from the Payment Method drop-down list.
  3. Enter credit card details, including Name on Card, Card CVV and Expiry as required.
  4. Enter notes as desired.
  5. Tap Make Payment.
  6. Tap Continue to confirm.
  7. If the payment is successful, a receipt number will be created in simPRO and a confirmation will appear along with an IntegraPay transaction ID. Tap OK to close.

 Connect job warnings

Job warnings appear in the Pricing page when the pricing is not reflective of the parts and labour applied to the job, as well as when purchase orders have not been processed appropriately.

Material warnings

Warning When does this warning appear? How can I address it?
There are more materials used on job cards than have been able to be assigned from stock. The recorded quantities of stock items assigned to the job are higher than those available in the relevant storage device.

You may need to either perform a stock adjustment in the relevant storage device, or create and receipt the relevant stock purchase order in Enterprise or Connect. When the required stock has been allocated and / or receipted, tap Assign Stock to resolve the warning and update the billed materials.

If the required stock has not been allocated / receipted, the warning will not resolve and will remain on the Pricing page.
The amount of materials assigned is different to the amount of materials being billed. Billable items added to the job in Connect are different to the actual stock assigned to the job in simPRO.

You may need to either perform a stock adjustment in the relevant storage device, or create and receipt the relevant stock purchase order in Enterprise or Connect. When the required stock has been allocated and / or receipted, tap Update Materials to resolve the warning and update the billed materials.

If the required stock has not been allocated / receipted, the additional materials will be removed from Materials table and the customer will only be billed for the assigned materials.​
The receipted price of some or all of the materials assigned is different than the cost of the materials being billed out. The actual cost of items assigned to the job from stock or from a purchase order in Connect have been changed since the items were assigned as required for the job in simPRO. Tap Update Prices to adjust the cost prices or sell prices of the billed materials.

Timesheet warnings

Warning When does this warning appear? How can I address it?
The amount of time scheduled is different to the amount of time being billed. The Qty of hours in a time block in the Labour table has been edited to differ from the time block as defined in the Times page of the workflow. Tap Update Time. The Qty of hours will then update the time block to correspond to that of the Times page.

Purchase order warnings

Warning When does this warning appear? How can I address it?
This Job does not have an order number - however the customer requires one. A customer has been set up in simPRO to require a purchase order number for their reference, but the relevant number has not been entered when creating the job in simPRO. This warning only prompts the field technician to update the job card in simPRO and cannot be addressed in Connect. To update a job card in simPRO with an order number, go to Jobs, locate the relevant job card, then go to Details > Settings and enter the Order No. as supplied by the customer.
This Job has purchase orders still outstanding. A purchase order created for the job in simPRO or Connect has not been receipted. If the ordered items have been fully received, you can resolve the warning by receipting the relevant purchase order in Connect to resolve the warning. If the items have not yet been fully received, consult management to evaluate whether it is appropriate to process the invoice at this time.
