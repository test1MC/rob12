Profit / Loss Job Report

Overview

Use the Profit / Loss Job report to determine how your business has performed on individual jobs within the specified date range. Managers may use this report to find exceptions on margin levels. This report includes invoiced jobs only.

View the Profit / Loss Job report

To view the report:

  1. Go to Reports > View Reports.
  2. Hover over Profit/Loss and click Job.
  3. Under Invoiced, select the date range.
  4. Under Stage, select Fully Invoiced to display only fully invoiced jobs, or All to display fully and partially invoiced jobs.
  5. Filter the report by selecting from the drop-down lists.
  6. Click Search.

Red values indicate loss and green values indicate profit.
