Reports

In simPRO you can generate reports to maintain transparency throughout all areas of your business. Report on profitability, productivity, labour recovery, employee activity and more.

You can use the Aged Receivables report to create statements and overdue letters to send to customers.

Profitability, productivity and labour recovery

Job Productivity

Reports > View Reports > Employees

Determines how efficient an employee has been on jobs.

Timesheet

Schedule > Timesheets

An operational tool specifically designed for reconciling your schedule-based timesheets into an output appropriate for payroll purposes.

Labour Productivity

 Reports > View Reports > Employees

A review of employee hours and how productive those hours have been.

Invoices

Reports > View Reports > Sales

Used to create a list of invoices created for customers.

Value

 Reports > View Reports > Stock

A snap-shot of stock value as at specified date. It could be used to determine the value of stock in selected storage devices before stock taking or at the end of financial period.

Purchases

Reports > View Reports > Supplier

Used to view supplier and / or contractor invoices or orders within the selected date range.

Business Toolkit

 Utilities > Business Toolkit

A mechanism for analysing current month-to-month profitability, and applying a what-if scenario to optimising company returns.

Reports > View Reports > Sales Reports on expected sales on jobs, quotes and prospects for given date range and employee.

Items Sold.

 Reports > View Reports > Sales Used to display a list of items sold. It can be used to include all items (including those that make up pre-builds) to view which items are sold or used most on jobs, which helps with better stock purchasing, planning and usage

Leads List

 Reports > View Reports > Leads

Displays a list of leads. The list is also attainable by using the All tab of lead search pages.

Leads Status

Reports > View Reports > Leads

Displays a list of leads by status. It uses the date the lead was issued as the primary filter. It uses the date the quote was issued as the primary filter. Entering a date range will include all quotes (service, project, recurring etc.), issued within the defined period. Statuses and automatic triggers are set up in system setup.

Entering a date range will include all quotes (service, project, recurring etc.), issued within the defined period. Statuses and automatic triggers are set up in system setup.

Programmed Preventative

 Reports > View Reports > Maintenance Planner

An overview of what asset types were serviced in the month and show if the all passed or if there was any failures it does not give specific assert details.

Within the matrix the tests are displayed as an icon as described below.

:: At least one asset test failed

:: All Tested assets Passed

:: No failed Asset tests, but at least one asset yet to be tested

Testing Averages

 Reports > View Reports > Maintenance Planner

Displays employee testing information by date range. Testing Average - Assets quantity tested / Days of scheduled by employee by month/week. It can be use to compare between different testing employee.

Recurring

 Reports > View Reports > Jobs A future planning report designed to be used to create a list of jobs that will be created for recurring projects. It is similar to the recurring jobs page, but has a greater level of filtering and contains the added details of when the last recurring job was carried out. This report could be filtered and downloaded to submit to management or clients wishing an overview of the recurring maintenance to be performed at a given site/s.

Contract Profitability

 Reports > View Reports > Profit/Loss Collates all the jobs and invoices (routine or recurring) that are associated with a contract and can be viewed for a given time period.

Maintenance Jobs Created

 Reports > View Reports > Maintenance Planner Generates a table of maintenance services to be completed, grouped by Asset type. These are services that have been generated by the maintenance planner, and in some cases may represent multiple service levels within the one job.

Jobs Activity

 Reports > View Reports > Jobs Examines how busy the business has been within a defined period of time. It looks at any activity / transaction on jobs for the date range selected and will not normally include committed costs.

Jobs List

 Reports > View Reports > Jobs Displays a list of jobs. The list is also attainable by using the All tab of job search pages. Much of this report’s functionality is available on the All Jobs tab and in conjunction with modify table view can customise the results for each stage an export as required. However this report is an advanced tool appropriate for senior management review.

Profit/Loss Jobs

 Reports > View Reports > Profit/Loss Determines how your business performed on individual jobs over a period of time by invoiced amount.
