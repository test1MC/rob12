Set up Company Information

Overview

Go to System > Setup > Company to set up basic company information. Some of this information will appear on different correspondence generated in simPRO, depending on how you've set up your Forms Setup. If you have a Multi-company build, you will need to click the desired company before updating company details.

Company Information

Enter and update your company information as desired. Banking details are needed if you include a Direct Deposit payment option in the How to Pay section of your invoices.

You can also change the Time Zone and Default Language for your company. To change the default language for individual Employees, go to the Settings tab in their employee cards.

 Multi-company Information

In a multi-company build, each company has it's own company information as well as a label and an assigned colour that display in the Company Selector.

The Label can be up to sixteen characters.

Click and drag the slider to select the colour.

If your multi-company build has a different default currency for each company, this will be displayed under Company Information.

 Upload company logo

You can upload either a image and / or pdf background to use as your letterhead on customer correspondence.

To upload an image file:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Company.
  2. Under Company Logo, click Choose File / Browse.
  3. Open your image file. Compatible file types include .jpeg, .png, .bmp, .tiff and .gif.
  4. Click Save.

The logo will display under Company Logo. It will also be visible on your company's simPRO login page.

 Upload PDF background

To upload a PDF background:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Company.
  2. Under PDF Background, click Choose File / Browse.
  3. Open your PDF file.
  4. Select On or Off to show the background always or never respectively. Alternatively, choose to show the background for Print Only, Screen Only, or Email Only.
  5. Click Save.
