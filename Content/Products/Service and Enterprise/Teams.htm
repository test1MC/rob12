Teams

Overview

Teams are groups of employees, contractors, and plant items that can be scheduled as one unit. This is useful for avoiding scheduling conflicts and making your scheduling workflow more efficient.

If any employee, contractor, or plant item in a team has already been scheduled to another job, the team as a whole will appear unavailable for scheduling, although the employees, contractors, and plant items will still be available individually. Individual employees, contractors, and plant items can be assigned to multiple teams.

This feature is best used for teams that will be assembled for the long term, rather than teams that change regularly.

You may wish to create a team for all employees so that you can easily schedule public holidays for everyone at once. You can also create a team for all technicians to schedule activities such as safety meetings.

Create a team

To create a team:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Teams.
  2. Click Create Team.
  3. Enter the team Name.
  4. Under Members, click the Available Resources you wish to add to the team, including employees, contractors, and plant items.
    • Alternatively, click Add All.
    • Click Assigned Resources to remove them, or alternatively, click Remove All.
  5. Click Schedule.
  6. Select the Shift Start and Shift End days and times for the team, then click Add.
  7. Click the Available Cost Centres and Available Zones to assign them.
    • Teams can only be scheduled to quotes / jobs with the same cost centre and / or zone assigned.
  8. Click Create.

To delete a team, click the red delete icon. This will not delete the individual members of the team. Schedules that were created for the team will continue to apply to the individual members after the team is deleted, but will no longer display as a team in the schedule view.

Only plant items that have been enabled to be scheduled will be available in the list.

 Schedule a team

You can schedule teams both within a job and in the schedule Day / Week View. Hover over a team schedule block to view the team members and assigned hours.

Schedule a team from a job

You can schedule teams to a job or quote the same way you would schedule an individual employee, contractor, or plant item.

To schedule a team:

  1. Access the desired quote / job.
  2. Click the Schedule tab.
  3. Under Teams, click the calendar icon next to the team you wish to schedule.
  4. Click and drag the schedule block to the desired time.
  5. Click the exit icon to close the window.

The schedule will then split between the team members under Scheduled Resources.

 Schedule a team in Day / Week View

You can also schedule teams in the schedule Day View and Week View. Here, you can schedule teams to activities as well as jobs and quotes.

To schedule a team to a job, quote, or activity:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day View / Week View.
  2. Select Job, Quote or Activity as desired from the drop-down list next to the search bar, then search and select the desired job / quote / activity.
  3. Select Team Resources from the drop-down list above the list of resources.
  4. Click and drag the schedule block on the line of the team you wish to schedule.
  5. Click Finish.

When you select Individual Resources, a schedule block will appear for each individual team member.
