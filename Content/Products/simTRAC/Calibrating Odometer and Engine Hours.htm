Calibrating Odometer and Engine Hours

Overview

To use Maintenance Planner effectively, you must calibrate your odometer and / or engine hours so that the tracker is in sync with the vehicle.

If you aren't monitoring your vehicles' engine hours, then you won't need to calibrate the engine hours.

Calibrate odometer and engine hours

To calibrate odometer and engine hours:

  1. Go to Settings > Manage Vehicles.
  2. Click the cog wheels icon next to the vehicle you wish to calibrate.
  3. Enter the numbers for Odometer and / or Engine Hours.
  4. Click Calibrate.

The new settings will update the tracker when simTRAC receives the next update from the vehicle.
