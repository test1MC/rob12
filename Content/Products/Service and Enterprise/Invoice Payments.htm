Invoice Payments

Overview

When customers make payments on invoices you have issued them for jobs, you can apply payments to their invoices in simPRO depending on your security group settings. If you have activated simPRO Payments, you can also apply payments made by credit card over the phone.

You can begin the process of applying a payment to an invoice in a number of ways:

  • By invoice: Go to Invoices > Unpaid Invoices, find the desired invoice, right-click it or click Options, and click Apply Payment.
  • By customer: Go to Invoices > Unpaid Invoices, search for the customer, select the desired invoices, and click Apply Payment at the bottom of the table.
  • By customer statement: Go to People > Customers, find the customer, and right-click it or click Options > View > Statement. Select invoices to pay then click Pay Invoices at the bottom of the table.
  • By invoiced job: Go to Jobs > Invoiced Jobs, find the desired invoiced job, click the ... button to expand it, find the desired invoice in the Unpaid Invoice list, then right-click it or click Options, and click Apply Payment.
  • Within an invoice: Open an invoice, click the Payments tab, and click Add Payment.

Once a payment has been applied to an invoice, it is not recommended to edit it or delete the payment, as it may have already posted to your accounting package.

Apply a payment to an invoice

To apply a payment to an invoice:

  1. Begin the process as above.
  2. Select a payment Method, and enter a Deposit Account if it does not automatically populate.
  3. Adjust the Amount Applied as applicable.
  4. Select Archive Job if you want the job to be archived after this payment is applied.
  5. Click Submit.
  6. Email a payment receipt, or click Finish.

Set up payment methods and assign deposit accounts to them through System > Setup > System Setup > Accounts > Payment Methods.

 Apply a payment by credit card over the phone

If you have activated simPRO Payments, you can easily apply credit card payments from a customer on the phone.

To apply payments made by credit card over the phone:

  1. Begin the process as above.
  2. Select the simPRO Payments-associated credit card type from the Method drop-down list.
  3. Enter the customer's Name on Card, Expiry, Card Number and CVV details.
  4. Complete the rest of the payment details as required, including the Amount Applied.
  5. Click Process Payment.
  6. Click Continue.
  7. If the payment is successful, a confirmation will appear, along with a receipt number. Click OK to close.

To instantly email the customer a receipt, click the Forms tab, select the Payment Receipt form, and send the email as usual.

You can also use this process to apply payments when a customer mails you the How To Pay section of their invoice with their handwritten credit card details.

 Email a payment receipt

To email a payment receipt:

  1. Apply a payment to an invoice and click Submit.
  2. Click the Forms tab.
  3. Enter details as required.
  4. Click View to preview the receipt, then close the preview.
  5. Click Send and OK.
  6. Click OK.
  7. Click Submit and / or Finish.

To adjust the payment receipt form title on the printable PDF document, include a Paid stamp on the payment receipt, or display a paid invoice as an invoice or receipt, go to System > Setup > Form Setup > Invoice > Display Options and make changes as desired.

 View payments made

To view all payments, go to Invoices > Payments.

To view payments made on a specific invoice:

  1. Open the desired invoice.
  2. Click the Payments tab.

Alternatively, find the invoice and click the ... button to expand a Payments list.

If your business collects payments in the field with Connect, check the Mobile > Payments Collected report for a convenient summary.

 Advanced

Enable payment imports with Xero

If you use Xero as your accounting package and have integrated it with simPRO, you can enable the ability to import payments into simPRO. Go to System > Setup > Accounts Integration, adjust the Payments options as necessary, then click Save.

Tips

Save customer banking details

If your customers frequently pay by cheque, save time by setting up their payment details in their customer card file, removing the need for repeated entry of their information. Open their card file, go to Profile > Settings, and fill out their Banking information. The next time you process a cheque from that customer, all you will need to manually enter is their cheque number.
