Business Groups

Use business groups to group costs centres together. Costs centres can be included in more than one business group.

Create a business group

To create a business group:

  1. Go to System > Setup > Accounts > Business Groups.
  2. Click Create Business Group.
  3. Name the business group.
  4. Click Available Cost Centres to assign them. Click Assigned Cost Centres to remove them.
  5. Click Create.

Business Groups may also be used for Category Tracking within your accounts package.
