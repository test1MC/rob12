Use Plant Items

Overview

To keep track of where plant items are being used on jobs, you can schedule them, or alternatively, check out a plant item to an employee. Since you cannot double-book plant items, you will always know where your plant items are and which ones are still available.

Schedule a plant item

Schedule plant items in the same way you schedule employees. You can also create teams with both employees and plant items to schedule them together.

To schedule a plant item:

  1. Go to Schedules > Day View.
  2. Select Show All to make sure you can see your plant items.
  3. Ensure that Job is selected next to the search bar, and search and select the desired job.
  4. You can only assign plant items to jobs if have assigned the correct cost centres and zones to the plant item.
  5. Select the cost centre, if applicable.
  6. Click and drag to create a schedule block for the plant item.
  7. Click Finish.

You can also schedule plant items from the Schedule tab in jobs.

 Check out / check in plant items

You can check in and permanently or temporarily check out plant items to an employee or contractor using the options in the plant item list, or the Details tab.

To check out / check in using Options:

  1. Go to Utilities > Plant & Equipment.
  2. Click the Plant Type that contains the plant item. Alternatively, use the local search bar.
  3. Click Options > Check-In Plant Item / Check-Out Plant Item on the desired plant item.
  4. If you have clicked Check-In Plant Item, select either Check In, or alternatively, Check In & Check Out to immediately check out the item again to a different person.
  5. Select the employee or contractor you wish to Check Out / Check In & Check Out to.
  6. Click Save.

To check in or check out multiple items, select the check boxes and click Check-In Plant Items / Check-Out Plant Items at the bottom of the page.

To check out / check in using the Details tab:

  1. Go to Utilities > Plant & Equipment.
  2. Click the Plant Type that contains the plant item. Alternatively, use the local search bar.
  3. Click the desired plant item.
  4. In the Details tab, the Status will display whether the plant item is currently checked out. Click Check In / Check Out as applicable.
  5. If you have clicked Check-In Plant Item, select either Check In, or alternatively, Check In & Check Out to immediately check out the item again to a different person.
  6. Click Save.
  7. Click Finish.

To view all previous times when the plant item was checked out and checked in, click History.
