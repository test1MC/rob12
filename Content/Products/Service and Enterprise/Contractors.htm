Contractors

Overview

Create contractors in simPRO Enterprise to manage contractor details and raise work orders for jobs. You can give your contractors access to Connect, the contractor portal, or Enterprise itself so they can view their schedules and update their jobs, as well as manage their team.

Once a contractor has completed work and sent you an invoice, you can record these in Enterprise to keep track of contractor costs and ensure job profitability.

View contractors

To view contractors, hover over People in the main menu, then click Contractors in the Go To sub-menu.

To quickly locate a contractor, click the starting letter of the company name above the search bar or enter the name in the search bar and click Search.

If you have a large contractor database, click Advanced to narrow your search results according to Employee Status and State. You can then save these filter options as a saved search to access a contractor or group of contractors more quickly.

Access contractor options

Access the following options when you right-click or click Options on a contractor in the Contractors table:

Edit Contractor Details

Access the contractor card file and edit details. You can also access the contractor card file by clicking the contractor in the Contractors table.

View Contractor Schedule

View schedule blocks for the contractor.

View Contractor Timesheet

View the contractor's timesheet.

View Contractor Notes

View and create notes of correspondence between the contractor and your business.

Map Contractor

Map contractor and job locations using simTRAC.

Archive Contractor

Archive a contractor. You can view archived contractor by going to People > Contractor > Advanced > Employee Status, then selecting Archived or Active & Archived. To make a contractor active in your system again, click Options > Activate Contractor.

Delete Contractor

Permanently delete a contractor.

You can map, archive or delete multiple contractors at once by selecting the contractor check boxes and clicking the desired option below the table.

 Create a contractor

To create a contractor, hover over People and click Create New Contractor. Alternatively, go to People > Contractors > Create Contractor. Once you have entered all relevant information, click Finish to create the contractor in simPRO.

Enter details

In the Details sub-tab, enter the following information:

  1. Enter the Contractor Company Name.
  2. Enter the Contact Name and Position of the individual who will be performing the work.
  3. Click New Contact and enter information as required to add more than one contact to the same company.
  4. Enter a Street Address. ​​This is used for calculating travel distance to jobs.
    • AU: Enter the Suburb and select the State to automatically generate the Postcode.
    • UK: Enter the Postcode, then click outside the text box to generate a selection of addresses.
  5. Select the contractor's Date of Commencement and Date of Birth.
  6. Enter the ABN / GST # / VAT Reg. No. / EIN as applicable.
  7. If you do not know this information, click the search icon to search for an existing ABN / GST # / VAT Reg. No. / EIN that matches the details you have already entered. Please ensure that you check with the contractor that this number is correct.
  8. If you have integrated simPRO with simTRAC, click Connect to simTRAC to log in to simTRAC and register a vehicle to the contractor.
  9. Enter Primary Contact Details as applicable.
  10. It is mandatory to enter an Email address.
  11. Click Save or Finish.

Adjust contractor settings

Go to Profile > Settings to adjust account, user and banking settings for a contractor.

To enable access to log in to simPRO, the contractor portal, or Connect:

  1. Access the desired contractor, then go to Profile > Settings > Account Setup.
  2. Enter a Username and Password.
  3. Passwords must be at least eight characters long and include one number, one lowercase letter, and one capital letter.
  4. Select the applicable Licences:
  5. Select User to enable access to simPRO.
  6. Select Mobility to enable access to Connect.
  7. All contractors with a username and password can log in to the contractor portal regardless of licences.
  8. Select a Security Group. This is only necessary if the contractor has a User licence.
  9. Click Save or Finish.

If you have given a contractor access to simPRO, Connect or the contractor portal, you can adjust their account settings under User Profile.

To adjust user profile settings:

  1. Access the desired contractor, then go to Profile > Settings > User Profile.
  2. Select if the contractor is a Salesperson or Project Manager, then enter the Commission Rate if applicable.
  3. Select a Storage Device if applicable.
  4. Select whether the contractor wishes to View Times in Minutes or Hours.
  5. Select the Preferred Language.
  6. If the contractor has their own simPRO build, select Allow jobs to be sent to contractor so that work orders created for the contractor will automatically raise jobs in the contractor's build.
  7. Click Send Verification Code to integrate with Google Calendar.
  8. Click Save or Finish.

Go to Settings > Banking to enter banking information and Payment Terms and Invoicing preferences as agreed with the contractor to ensure your invoices applied in your accounting package are correct.

 Set schedule availability

You can set a contractor's available hours for individual days, weekends, or all weekdays, as well as assign them to cost centres, zones and teams. Work orders cannot be issued to contractors unless they are assigned to the same cost centre and / or zone as the job.

To set schedule availability:

  1. Access the desired contractor, then go to Profile > Schedule.
  2. Under Shift Start, select the Day or days you wish to schedule for, then select a starting and ending Time.
  3. Repeat as necessary until complete.
  4. Click Add.
  5. Under Available Cost Centres / Available Zones / Available Teams, click the cost centres / zones / teams you wish to assign.
  6. Alternatively, click Add All.
  7. Click Save or Finish.

To remove available hours for a specific day, click the red delete icon for the desired day, or alternatively, click Clear next to Availability to remove all available hours. To edit available hours for a specific day, select the desired Shift Start / End Day and Time, then click Add, and the existing shift will be adjusted.

Click any Assigned Cost Centres / Assigned Zones / Assigned Teams to remove them from an employee, or alternatively, click Remove All.

Contractors can still be scheduled outside of their available hours.

 Add licences

In the Licences sub-tab, enter any relevant licences that the contractor holds, such as licences for operating machinery, site inductions, drivers licence, insurance, then click Add to keep these details for reference. You will receive an alert when a licence expiry date is close.

When you add a new licence to a contractor, it will be available to select and add to other contractors.

Go to Reports > View Reports > Employees > Licences to view the Employee Licence Matrix report to view a list of all employees and contractors who hold certain licences.

 Add emergency contact details

In the Emergency sub-tab, enter emergency contact details for a contractor as applicable, then click Save or Finish​.

 Set a pay rate and bill rates

If a contractor is paid by the hour, go to Rates, then enter the applicable Pay Rate, Employment Costs, and Overhead, and click Save or Finish.

Under Bill Rates, select the applicable Labour Rate for each Schedule Rate, then click Save or Finish. This is important for scheduling contractors when labour costs are calculated from the schedule.

If your contractor is not paid by the hour, leave these empty so you can manually adjust the payment on work orders to the agreed value.

Consult your accountant when entering the Pay Rate, Employment Costs, and Overhead.

 Add attachments

Go to Attachments to add, organise and download contractor attachments for internal reference only.

 View plant items

Go to Plant to view any plant items and equipment that have been checked out to the contractor.

The Plant tab will only appear after you have saved the contractor in simPRO.

 Add contractor notes

Use contractor notes to maintain a running record of any correspondence logged against the contractor, and assign employees to follow up correspondence if required.

To add a note:

  1. Access the desired contractor, then click Notes.
  2. Click Create Note.
  3. Enter a Subject.
  4. Select a Reference and enter a No. if desired.
  5. Enter the Note.
  6. Select a Follow Up date if required and assign an employee to follow up the note.
  7. Click Save at the bottom of the note.
  8. Click Save or Finish in the top right.

The Notes tab will only appear after you have saved the contractor in simPRO.

 Assign companies

If you have a multi-company build, contractors can be shared between companies. Click the Companies tab and click any Available Companies to assign them. Click Save to update your changes.

If multiple companies are assigned, you can select a Default Company for the contractor. This is the company that the contractor most often does work for.
