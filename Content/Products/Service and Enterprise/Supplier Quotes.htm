Supplier Quotes

Overview

A supplier quote is a list of items required for a quote or job. You can email this quote to a number of suppliers. After you receive the pricing, you can compare prices, update the supplier quote and nominate the winning supplier.

Create a supplier quote

To create a supplier quote:

  1. Hover over Materials and click Create New Quote.
  2. Click the Catalogue tab.
  3. Enter the quantities of items you need, then click Add Items.
  4. Click the Supplier Quote tab. The items you added will be listed under Quote Items.
  5. To add suppliers, click Add Supplier, select from the Supplier Name drop-down list and click Save.
  6. If desired, enter Supplier Notes for the supplier and Private Notes for your own reference.
  7. Click Finish.
Send a supplier quote

To send a supplier quote:

  1. Go to Materials > Quotes.
  2. Click the supplier quote you would like to send.
  3. Click the Forms tab.
  4. Select the Supplier Quote Form to send the supplier quote as an attachment, or Email Only.
  5. Ensure the recipient's email address is correct.
  7. Click Send.
 Raise a supplier quote from a quote

To raise a supplier quote from a quote:

  1. Open the desired quote. If the quote has a single cost centre, click the Quote # in the breadcrumbs at the top of the page.
  2. Click the Project Overview tab.
  3. Select the check box for each cost centre you would like to include in the quote.
  4. Select if Purchase Prices are Based on Supplier or Based on Quote.
  5. Click Create Supplier Quote at the bottom of the page. Continue creating the supplier quote as normal.
 Raise a supplier quote from a job

There are two ways to raise a supplier quote from a job.

Project Overview

To raise a supplier quote from the Project Overview tab:

  1. Open the desired job. If the job has a single cost centre, click the Job # in the breadcrumbs at the top of the page.
  2. Click Project Overview tab.
  3. Select the check box for each cost centre you would like to include in the quote.
  4. Select if the Purchase Prices are Based on Supplier or Based on Job.
  5. Click Create Supplier Quote. Continue creating the supplier quote as normal.
 Stock

To raise a quote from the Stock tab within a cost centre:

  1. Open the desired job. If the job has multiple cost centres, click the Cost Centres tab and click a cost centre.
  2. Click the Stock tab.
  3. Click the Order sub-tab.
  4. Select if the Purchase Prices are Based on Supplier or Based on Job.
  5. Click Create Supplier Quote. Continue creating the supplier quote as normal.

If a quote or job is based on trade prices, you will not be able update the pricing from the supplier quote.

 Add attachments

To add attachments to a supplier quote:

  1. Create a supplier quote or open an exiting one.
  2. Click the Attachments tab.
  3. Click Browse to add attachments, or drag and drop attachments from your system to the Drop attachments here box.
  4. Select the Email check box if you want this attachment to be emailed with your quote.

Attachments can be renamed or deleted after they are uploaded.

 Select a winning supplier

After comparing the prices from different suppliers, update the supplier quote and select a winning supplier.

To select a winning supplier:

  1. Hover over Materials and click Quotes.
  2. Click the desired quote.
  3. Choose the winning supplier from the Won by Supplier drop-down list.
  4. Enter the Price of the items.
  5. Click Finish​.
 ​​Link a supplier quote to a quote or job

To link a supplier quote to a quote or job:

  1. Open the quote or job you wish to link to.
  2. Click the Parts & Labour tab.
  3. In the Supplier Quote drop-down list, select a supplier quote.
  4. Click Apply.

If you have not linked a supplier, the supplier quote will not be available.

 ​Create a purchase order from a supplier quote

When raising a purchase order that has items with an associated supplier quote, link the supplier quote number to override existing catalogue prices. You can only convert a supplier quote to a purchase order if the winning supplier has been selected.

To create a purchase order from a supplier quote:

  1. Hover over Materials and click Quotes.
  2. Click Options next to your desired quote, and click Convert from the drop-down list.
  3. In the Supplier Quote drop-down list, click the desired supplier quote to link to.
  4. When you have finished editing the other purchase order details, click Finish.
