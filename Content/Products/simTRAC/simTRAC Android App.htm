simTRAC Android App

simTRAC has a free app for Android. You can log in with your regular username and password. Alerts do not appear in the mobile app.

Vehicles

When you first log in, you will see a list of your vehicles. A green key icon indicates that the vehicle is turned on and a red key icon indicates that the vehicle is turned off. Tap Groups to see a list of groups. Tap a vehicle to view it on the map or send a message.

 Map

Tap Map at the bottom of the screen to view multiple vehicles on the map at once.

Tap Follow All to zoom out to view all vehicles.

 Messages

Tap Messages at the bottom of the screen to view or send messages.

The three tabs are:

Inbox

View messages received from vehicles.

Outbox

View messages sent to vehicles.

Compose

Send a new message.

To delete messages you will need to go back into the main interface and archive the messages.

 Logout

Tap Logout to log out.
