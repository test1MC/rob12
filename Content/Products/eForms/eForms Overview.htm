eForms Overview

Digitise your paper forms with simPRO eForms for use on tablet devices in the field.

Use simPRO eForms to:

  • Edit and customise your forms in the Form Designer.
  • Complete forms on tablet devices.
    • Capture and attach unlimited photographs.
    • Collect signatures and drawings.
  • Submit forms electronically to the eForms portal in real time.
  • Integrate with simPRO to auto-complete forms with data from quotes and jobs.

eForms can be used standalone or integrated with simPRO Enterprise and simPRO Service. To purchase a licence and set up eForms forms, existing simPRO clients should lodge a ticket with simPRO Support. New clients may contact us through the simPRO website.

As eForms is a separately licensed add-on product, your simPRO licence does not give you access to eForms.

Compatible devices

While it is possible to run eForms on a mobile phone device, we recommended using a tablet device with a camera that supports auto-focus.

simPRO eForms is designed to run on the following operating systems (OS):

  • Apple iOS 7.0 and above
  • Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) and above

simPRO eForms has been designed for and tested on the following tablets:

  • Apple iPad Mini
  • Apple iPad with Retina display
  • Apple iPad Air
  • Samsung Galaxy Tab 3 10.1
  • Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1 LTE

Other operating systems and tablets

Only the device versions listed above have been tested and confirmed to run simPRO eForms successfully, however other devices that have not been tested may also be compatible. Should you choose to run simPRO eForms on a different device, we recommend you test the app thoroughly before commencing actual work.

If the device you are using runs simPRO eForms successfully and is not included in the compatible tablet list above, please let us know by contacting simPRO Support.

It is not recommended to assume that newer or older versions of the same device or operating systems will also be compatible, as there may have been significant changes between releases. It is also not recommended to assume that a device or operating system that works with an earlier version of the eForms mobile app will work with a newer version. Be sure to check the compatibility list for each app you use.

If the required operating system for simPRO eForms is not pre-installed on your device, check with your provider to see if it is available to download, as new OS versions can be delayed for quality assurance purposes.
