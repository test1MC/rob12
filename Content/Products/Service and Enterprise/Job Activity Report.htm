Job Activity Report

Overview

Use the Job Activity report to examine overall job activity and transactions within the specified date range. Activity and transactions on jobs include creating jobs, purchase orders, scheduling, deposits and progress claims.

This report may be used by project managers to determine how much to claim on progress claims, which will help to ensure cash flow is maintained for project jobs. It can also be used by service managers to examine jobs that have not been invoiced, and to assess overall resourcing against the volume of work.

View the Job Activity report

To view the report:

  1. Go to Reports > View Reports.
  2. Hover over Jobs and click Activity.
  3. Select the Date Range.
  4. Under Difference Based, select whether the difference in the report is calculated by Direct Cost or by Direct Cost Inc Overhead.
  5. Filter the report by selecting from the drop-down lists.
  6. Click Search.

Red values indicate jobs invoiced below direct costs and green values indicate jobs invoiced above direct costs.
