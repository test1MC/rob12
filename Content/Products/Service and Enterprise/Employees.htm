Employees

Overview

Create employees in simPRO to manage their details and schedule them to jobs.

You can also give your employees access to Connect, the employee portal, or simPRO itself so they can view their schedules and update their jobs, as well as manage their team.

View employees

To view existing employees in the system, go to People > Employees.

To quickly locate an employee, click the starting letter of the employee's name above the search bar or enter the name in the search bar and click Search.

If you have a large employee database, click Advanced to narrow your search results according to Employee Status and State. You can then save these filter options as a saved search to access an employee or group of employees more quickly.

 Create an employee

To create an employee, hover over People and click Create New Employee. Alternatively, go to People > Employees > Create Employee. Once you have entered all relevant information, click Finish to create the employee in simPRO.

Enter details

To enter employee name, employment and contact details:

  1. Create or access the employee as normal.
  2. Enter the Employee Name and Position.
  3. Enter a Street Address. This is used for calculating travel distance to jobs.
    • AU: Enter the Suburb and select the State to automatically generate the Postcode.
    • UK: Enter the Postcode, then click outside the text box to generate a selection of addresses.
  4. Select the employee's Date of Commencement and Date of Birth.
  5. If you have integrated simPRO with simTRAC, click Connect to simTRAC to log in to simTRAC and register a vehicle to the employee.
  6. Enter Primary Contact Details as applicable.
  7. It is mandatory to enter an Email address.
  8. Click Save or Finish.

Adjust employee settings

Go to Settings to adjust account, user and banking settings for an employee.

To enable access to log in to simPRO, the employee portal, or Connect:

  1. Access the desired employee, then go to Profile > Settings > Account Setup.
  2. Enter a Username and Password.
    • Passwords must be at least eight characters long and include one number, one lowercase letter, and one capital letter.
  3. Select the applicable Licences:
    • Select User to enable access to simPRO.
    • Select Mobility to enable access to Connect.
    • All employees with a username and password can log in to the employee portal regardless of licences.
  4. Select a Security Group. This is only necessary if the employee has a User licence.
  5. Click Save or Finish.

If you have given an employee access to simPRO, Connect or the employee portal, you can adjust their account settings under User Profile.

To adjust user profile settings:

  1. Access the desired employee, then go to Profile > Settings > User Profile.
  2. Select if the employee is a Salesperson or Project Manager, then enter the Commission Rate if applicable.
  3. Select a Storage Device if applicable.
  4. Select whether the employee wishes to View Times in Minutes or Hours.
  5. Select the Preferred Language.
  6. Click Send Verification Code to integrate with Google Calendar.
  7. Click Save or Finish.

Go to Settings > Banking to enter banking information for an employee for your reference.

 Set schedule availability

You can set an employee's available hours for individual days, weekends, or all weekdays, as well as assign them to cost centres, zones and teams. Employees cannot be scheduled to a job or quote unless they are assigned to the same cost centre and / or zone as the job / quote.

To set schedule availability:

  1. Access the desired employee, then go to Profile > Schedule.
  2. Under Shift Start, select the Day or days you wish to schedule for, then select a starting and ending Time.
  3. Click Add.
  4. Repeat as necessary until complete.
  5. Under Available Cost Centres / Available Zones / Available Teams, click the cost centres / zones / teams you wish to assign.
    • Alternatively, click Add All.
  6. Under Available Cost Centres / Available Zones / Available Teams, click the cost centres / zones / teams you wish to assign.
    • Alternatively, click Add All.
  7. Under Available Cost Centres / Available Zones, click the cost centres / zones you wish to assign.  
    • Alternatively, click Add All.
  8. Click Save or Finish.

To remove available hours for a specific day, click the red delete icon for the desired day, or alternatively, click Clear next to Availability to remove all available hours. To edit available hours for a specific day, select the desired Shift Start / End Day and Time, then click Add, and the existing shift will be adjusted.

 Add licences

In the Licences sub-tab, enter any relevant licences that the employee holds, such as licences for operating machinery, site inductions, drivers licence, insurance, then click Add to keep these details for reference. You will receive an alert when a licence expiry date is close.

When you add a new licence to an employee, it will be available to select and add to other employees.

Go to Reports > View Reports > Employees > Licences to view the Employee Licence Matrix report to quickly see a list of all employees and contractors who hold certain licences.

 Add emergency contact details

In the Emergency sub-tab, enter emergency contact details for an employee as applicable, then click Save or Finish​.

 Set a pay rate and bill rates

If your employees are paid by the hour, go to Rates, then enter the applicable Pay Rate, Employment Costs, and Overhead, and click Save or Finish.

Under Bill Rates, select the applicable Labour Rate for each Schedule Rate, then click Save or Finish. This is important for scheduling employees when labour costs are calculated from the schedule.

Consult your accountant when entering the Pay Rate, Employment Costs, and Overhead.

 Add attachments

Go to Attachments to add, organise and download employee attachments for internal reference only.

 View plant items

Go to Plant to view any plant items that have been checked out to the employee.

The Plant tab will only appear after you have saved the employee.

 Add employee notes

Use employee notes to maintain a running record of any correspondence logged against the employee, and assign other employees to follow up correspondence if required.

To add a note:

  1. Access the desired employee, then click Notes.
  2. Click Create Note.
  3. Enter a Subject.
  4. Select a Reference and enter a No. if desired.
  5. Enter the Note.
  6. Select a Follow Up date if required and assign an employee to follow up the note.
  7. Click Save at the bottom of the note.
  8. Click Save or Finish in the top right.

The Notes tab will only appear after you have saved the employee.

 Assign companies

If you have a multi-company build, employees can be shared between companies. Click the Companies tab and click any Available Companies to assign them, then click Save to update your changes.

You can also select a Default Company as the company that the employee most often works for.
