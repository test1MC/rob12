Jobs

Overview

To view jobs, hover over Jobs in the main menu. You can then view jobs according to specific internal stages for your business.

These stages include Pending, Progress, Complete, Invoiced, Archived and Recurring.

Alternatively, go to Jobs > Pending, then click the All sub-tab to view all jobs.

To quickly locate a job, enter the job number or customer name in the local search bar and click Search. Alternatively, you can enter part of the customer name, then select the applicable job from the drop-down list.

If you have a large job database, click Advanced to narrow your search results according to Salesperson, Technician, Status, Cost Centre, Customer Profile, Tag, Zone, and Due Date. In simPRO Enterprise, you can also filter jobs according to Project Manager, Business Group, Customer Group, and Job Type.

Access job options

Access the following options when you right-click or click Options on a job in the jobs tables:

Edit Job Access the job Summary tab to view and edit the job. You can also click the job to access it.
Schedule Job Schedule a technician to a job in the Day View / Week View, depending on your Default View settings in System > Setup > Defaults > Schedule.
Create Progress Claim Merge the job into another job by entering the latter job number, then clicking Merge. This is useful if there are duplicated jobs in your system. If you are merging project jobs, you can also select to Merge Sections as Variations.
Job Stage Move a job to a different stage.
Map Job Map the job site location.
Merge Job Merge the job into another job by entering the latter job number, then clicking Merge. This is useful if there are duplicated jobs in your system. If you are merging project jobs, you can also select to Merge Sections as Variations.

Archive job Archive a job. You can view archived jobs by going to jobs >Archived. To make a job active in your system again, click Options >Job Stage and select the desired stage.
Delete job Permanently delete the job. If the job was converted to a job, the converted job will not be affected.

For recurring jobs, the following options are available:

Edit Job Edit the recurring job template.
Create Job Create a job from the recurring job template. This job will appear under Jobs > Pending and the Next Due Date for the recurring job template will be updated.
Skip Period Merge the job into another job by entering the latter job number, then clicking Merge. This is useful if there are duplicated jobs in your system. If you are merging project jobs, you can also select to Merge Sections as Variations.
Map Job Map the job site location.
Delete Recurring Job Delete the recurring job template. No more jobs can be created for this template.

You can map, archive, and delete multiple jobs at once, as well as convert them to jobs by selecting the check boxes, then clicking the desired option below the table.

You can also perform the following actions to multiple jobs at once:

  • Assign a salesperson / technician / project manager
  • Assign a salesperson / technician / project manager
  • Assign a salesperson / technician
  • Manually update the job status
  • Add project tags
  • Change the due date
  • Change the job stage

When you have finished selecting from the options, click Update.

Please take caution when merging jobs in simPRO Enterprise as you cannot split them again.
