Gmail Integration

Overview

This feature is only available for businesses using Google Apps for Work.

Through the simPRO Gmail Integration app, you can easily add emails as notes to customers, leads, quotes and jobs, without needing to leave your Gmail screen.

Through the simPRO Gmail Integration app, you can easily add emails as notes to customers, quotes and jobs, without needing to leave your Gmail screen.

Once installed, simPRO will automatically recognise the email sender if their contact information is saved in simPRO.

Up to 1000 characters of an email will save as a plain text note.

To use this app, your email account must be the email address saved in your employee card file in simPRO.

Install simPRO Gmail Integration

This step can only be performed by your Google Apps for Work administrator. If you are the administrator, please follow our Gmail Integration Administrator's Guide to install simPRO Gmail Integration.

 Log in to simPRO Gmail Integration

To log in to simPRO Gmail Integration through your Gmail account for the first time:

  1. Log into Gmail and open an email.
  2. Below the email, the simPRO Software - simPRO Gmail Integration window will be visible. Click Sign in with Google.
  3. Enter your Company Identifier (the first part of the URL you use to access simPRO).
  4. Enter your simPRO Username and Password.
  5. Click Login.

After completing this process once, you will subsequently only need to click Sign in with Google to log in. If you are already logged into simPRO in another browser tab or window, you will not need to click Sign in with Google, as the simPRO app will load automatically.

To view attached email notes:

  1. In simPRO, open the customer, lead, quote or job the email was attached to.
  2. In simPRO, open the customer, lead, quote or job the email was attached to.
  3. In simPRO, open the customer, quote or job the email was attached to.
  4. If viewing a quote or job, go to Details > Summary > Activity > Timeline; if viewing a lead or customer, go to the Notes tab.
  5. If viewing a quote or job, go to Details > Summary > Activity > Timeline; if viewing a lead or customer, go to the Notes tab.
  6. If viewing a quote or job, go to Details > Summary > Activity > Timeline; if viewing a customer, go to the Notes tab.
  7. Notes appear in chronological order. Click a note to view more details or to edit it.

 Create a new contact through Gmail

You can create a new contact for an existing customer using a sender's email address, if simPRO does not recognise their address. Some fields will automatically populate using email content.

To create a new contact for a customer through Gmail:

  1. In the simPRO app window at the bottom of the desired email, click Create New Contact.
  2. Search and select the desired customer to associate this contact with.
  3. Enter and adjust contact details as required.
  4. Click Send.

The new contact will be added to the selected customer's card file in simPRO, and the email will be saved as a note on that customer.
