Working on Multiple Projects

Overview

In simPRO, you can have up to five projects, such as quotes, jobs, and purchase orders open at the same time. To switch between open projects, you can use the projects menu bar at the bottom of the screen.

In this article, the term 'projects' refers to:

  • Leads
  • Leads
  • Quotes
  • Jobs
  • Purchase orders
  • Purchase order receipts
  • Recurring jobs
  • Recurring invoices
  • Take-off templates
  • Take-off templates
  • Stock takes
  • Stock adjustments

It is important to save frequently when editing a project, as after 20 minutes of inactivity, your open projects will be editable by other users. If another user attempts to access one of your open projects after this time, you will be pushed out of that project and all unsaved changes will be lost. You will also lose your changes after this time limit if you do not save your projects before turning off your computer or closing your laptop.

Use the project menu bar

When you open a project, a menu bar will appear at the bottom of your screen, listing your recently opened project. If you continue to open additional projects, they will also appear on the bar.

The project you are currently viewing will be displayed in a lighter font than the other open projects.

Click the grey arrow on the far right to hide or expand the menu bar.

If you try to log out while you have an unsaved project open in the menu bar, you will receive a warning so that you have a chance to save your changes before logging out.

 Switch between open projects

To switch between open projects, click the project you wish to open on the projects menu bar.

If you need additional information before switching, hover over an open project to see the full description.

 Open a new project with five projects already open

If you have five projects open and attempt to open a sixth, an alert will appear prompting you to close at least one project to proceed. Select the check boxes for projects you wish to close, then click one of the following options:

Save & Close

Save and close the selected projects.

Close Without Saving

Close the selected projects without saving.

Cancel

Cancel without closing any projects. The new project will not be opened.

View a project in read only mode

If you attempt to open a project which is currently in use by another user, an alert will appear.

Click Read Only to view the project without being able to edit it. If you need to make changes, click Cancel and wait for the other user to close the project.

 Work on multiple projects in a multi-company build

If you are using a multi-company build and are able to switch between companies, the colour of the square next to each project will match the company it is associated with.

Switching between projects associated with different companies will automatically switch the company being used.

 Tips

Connect updates for open projects

If a project is open in simPRO, any Connect updates will not display for that project until after it is closed in simPRO.

Import a supplier invoice to a purchase order

To import a supplier invoice to a purchase order, the purchase order must first be closed.
