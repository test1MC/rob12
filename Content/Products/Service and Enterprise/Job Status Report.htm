Job Status Report

Overview

Use the Job Status report to display a list of jobs by status within the specified date range. The report includes all jobs, including project and recurring jobs.

This report may be used to assess the progression of jobs through a company’s workflow and diagnose bottlenecks.

View the Job Status report

To view the report:

  1. Go to Reports > View Reports.
  2. Hover over Jobs and click Status.
  3. Under Job Date Issued, select the date range.
  4. Filter the report by selecting from the drop-down lists.
  5. Click Search.
