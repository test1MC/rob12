Payment Methods

Overview

Payment methods are the various ways in which your customers pay for the goods and services you provide, such as credit cards, cash, direct deposits, cheques, and even vouchers. A number of default payment methods are preset in simPRO and can be edited or deleted as required.

When you define an Account No. for a payment method, this Account No. will fill in automatically each time a payment of this type is processed. The default deposit account number is set up in System > Setup > Defaults > Financial.

You may wish to define a separate cheque clearing account, or create a payment method to handle industry specific vouchers or finance payments.

Create a payment method

To create a payment method:

  1. Go to System > Setup > System Setup > Accounts > Payment Methods.
  2. Click Create Payment Method.
  3. Enter the payment method Name.
  4. Select the payment method Type.
  5. Enter the applicable Account No.
  6. Enter a finance charge if applicable.
  7. Click Create.

Enter a finance charge

For each payment method you set up in simPRO, you can set a different finance charge as a percentage of the transaction. You can use this to pass onto your customers any additional charges associated with certain payment methods, such as bank fees for cheque deposits or credit card merchant fees.

As this will appear on invoices, you can go to System > Setup > Defaults > Financial > Invoicing > Finance Charge Label to give this charge a different name, such as 'convenience fee' or 'credit card surcharge'.

 Enable payment methods for Connect

To allow technicians to process payments with Connect, you need to enable the applicable payment methods.

To enable payment methods in Connect:

  1. Go to System > System Setup > Mobile > General > Billing Settings.
  2. Select Enable to allow mobile billing.
  3. Under Payment Methods, select Enable for Connect for each payment method you wish to be available to select to process payments in Connect.
  4. Click Save to update your changes.

Your technicians can now process payments in Connect’s billing workflow.

When selecting a simPRO Payments method in Connect, users will see the Card Type rather than the associated payment method, while reports and invoices will show the full payment method, not just the card type.

 simPRO Payments

If you have activated simPRO Payments, your available credit card types will be listed below your payment methods. View your country's setup instructions to associate simPRO Payments credit card types with your existing payment methods.
